# Act 7: The Leap of Faith
*   **Traditional Reference:** Sundara Kanda (Chapters 1 - 68)
*   **Act Focus:** Hanuman's leap across the ocean, stealth infiltration of Lanka, finding Sita in Ashoka Vatika, defeating Ravana's son Aksha Kumar, and burning Lanka using his flaming tail.

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
The search parties return empty-handed, except for the southern group. Standing on the southern cliffs, Hanuman is reminded of his divine power by Jambavan. Growing to giant proportions, Hanuman takes a leap of faith across the ocean. He overcomes various sea obstacles (Surasa, Simhika) and lands on Lanka. 

Shrinking to the size of a bug (*Anima* form), Hanuman slips past Lanka's heavy security and finds Sita in Ashoka Vatika, weeping under a tree. He climbs the tree and sings Rama's praise, dropping Rama's signet ring (*Anguliyaka*) to win her trust. Sita is overjoyed but refuses to be carried back, stating Rama must defeat Ravana to restore his honor. Hanuman decides to test Lanka's strength. He destroys the royal orchards, attracting Ravana's son Aksha Kumar. Hanuman defeats Aksha but allows himself to be captured by Indrajit to confront Ravana in court. Ravana orders Hanuman's tail to be set on fire. Hanuman uses his flaming tail to set the golden city of Lanka ablaze before escaping.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Shraddha (Unwavering Faith) & Hope:** Hanuman teaches that with pure devotion and courage, no ocean is too wide to cross. His finding of Sita brings hope to a desolate world.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Hanuman (Playable Protagonist):** Leap the ocean, use stealth to navigate Lanka, find Sita, defeat Aksha Kumar, and burn Lanka.
*   **Sita (NPC):** Resist Ravana's verbal threats, receive the signet ring, and gift her *Chudamani* (hair gem) to Hanuman.
*   **Ravana (NPC):** Court antagonist; orders the burning of Hanuman's tail.
*   **Aksha Kumar (Boss):** Ravana's youngest son, defending Lanka's palace from the giant Vanara intruder.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **The Sea Expanse:** An active aerial corridor with heavy headwinds, flying sea-serpents, and giant shadow-catching monsters.
*   **Ashoka Vatika (Lanka's Orchard):** A beautiful but heavily guarded garden filled with ashoka trees, golden streams, and captive prison-cages.
*   **Lanka Golden Citadels:** High golden towers, stone walkways, and royal courtyards, later engulfed in dynamic orange flames.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: Aksha Kumar (The Chariot Duel)
*   **Boss Character:** A fast-paced aerial boss encounter.
*   **Phase 1 (The Chariot Chase):** Aksha Kumar flies on a golden war chariot pulled by eight celestial horses, firing rapid arrows. Hanuman must leap from pillar to pillar, avoiding arrow volleys, and grapple onto the chariot.
*   **Phase 2 (Chariot Sabotage):** Once on the chariot, Hanuman engages in close combat with Aksha's elite guard while the chariot moves rapidly. The player must destroy the chariot's wings and thrusters.
*   **Phase 3 (The Crushing Blow):** Aksha falls to the ground and attacks with dual curved swords. Hanuman grows to giant *Mahima* form and slams his fists down, crushing Aksha's swords and his defensive shield.
*   **Mythological Defeat:** Hanuman grabs Aksha's chariot, spins it in the air, and throws it into the sea. Aksha is defeated, sending shockwaves of fear through Ravana's palace.

---

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Jambavan:** *"Hanuman, you are the son of the Wind! No wall can stop you, no ocean can hold you. Leap, Maruti, and find our Mother!"*
*   **Hanuman (mid-leap):** *"RAMA! The name is my wings, the truth is my wind!"*
*   **Sita (weeping):** *"Who speaks of Rama in this land of demons? Is this another illusion of Ravana?"*
*   **Hanuman (dropping the ring):** *"Behold, Mother! It is Rama's ring. I am Hanuman, his humble servant. He is coming, and his arrows will soon block out Lanka's sun."*
*   **Ravana:** *"Set this monkey's tail on fire! Let him return to his forest king with a blackened tail!"*

---

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Hanuman:**
    *   *Beginning:* White dhoti, gold armlets.
    *   *Lanka Escape:* Tail wrapped in tattered cloth, burning with bright orange fire, leaving smoke trails.
*   **Sita:** Faded, dusty orange saree, disheveled hair, showing deep grief.
*   **Aksha Kumar:** Intricate golden armor with peacock-feather shoulders, high golden helmet, wielding dual curved swords.

---

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Giant Hanuman, Micro Hanuman, Captive Sita, Aksha Kumar, Flying Chariot, Lanka Guards.
*   **Environments:** Deep blue ocean water, Ashoka Vatika (ashoka trees, red leaves), Golden Lanka (golden brick textures, fire shaders).
*   **Particle Effects:** Tail fire flames, smoke trails, floating gold rings, ocean splash, crumbling golden bricks.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* High-energy, cinematic orchestral tracks with traditional Indian flute and heavy drums; roaring fire soundscapes for Lanka's destruction.
*   *SFX:* Chariot crashing, wooden branches snapping, fire crackling, sword clashing on mace.

### Level Design Elements
*   Size-shifting mechanic (swapping between giant and micro player models).
*   Flight runner mini-game with ring-gates over the ocean.
*   Fire propagation mechanics (player spreads fire by brushing against building nodes).
