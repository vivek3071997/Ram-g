# Act 2: The Bow of Shiva
*   **Traditional Reference:** Bala Kanda (Chapters 31 - 77)
*   **Act Focus:** Arrival in Mithila, stringing and breaking the divine bow *Pinaka*, marrying Sita, and the intense duel against the legendary Sage Parashurama.

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
After cleansing the forest, Sage Vishwamitra takes Rama and Lakshmana to the beautiful kingdom of Mithila, ruled by the philosopher King Janaka. The king has declared that whoever can string the colossal bow *Pinaka*, gifted to his ancestors by Lord Shiva, shall wed his daughter Sita. 

Countless kings and demons have failed to even budge the bow, which sits on an eight-wheeled iron cart. Rama, stepping forward with humility, lifts the bow effortlessly. As he bends it to attach the string, the bow snaps with a sound like a thunderclap. Following his wedding to Sita, the party is confronted by the furious Sage Parashurama, an earlier avatar of Vishnu who vowed to destroy all arrogant warriors. Rama must defuse the sage's wrath in a high-stakes duel.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Spiritual Strength vs. Pride:** True strength does not flex or boast. While other kings fail due to their massive egos and raw muscle, Rama succeeds through pure humility, grace, and spiritual attunement.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Lord Rama (Playable):** Align the elemental nodes of Mithila's court, lift and string the Pinaka, and survive the combat test with Parashurama.
*   **Sita (Companion NPC):** Channel Earth-essence to stabilize the heavy vibrations of the bow.
*   **King Janaka (NPC):** Host the tournament and announce the wedding.
*   **Parashurama (Boss):** Test Rama's divinity in an intense, rapid-fire parry duel.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **The Great Court of Mithila:** A sprawling outdoor arena surrounded by white terracotta temples, decorated with thousands of marriage flags, flower petals, and royal pavilions.
*   **Parashurama's Volcanic Pass:** A narrow, rocky mountain ridge engulfed in dust and lightning where the post-wedding party is ambushed.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: The Pinaka & Parashurama
*   **Boss Character:** The act features a dual boss structure:
    1.  *The Pinaka (Puzzle Boss):* The player must solve an active gravity puzzle. Aligns Rama's spiritual *Prana* nodes with the heavy bow. When aligned, the player holds down buttons to lift the bow and string it, leading to the cinematic snap.
    2.  *Parashurama (Combat Boss):* The legendary axeman. Parashurama is lightning fast and attacks with an unbreakable battleaxe. Players cannot block his strikes; they must rely on **Perfect Parries** and quick dodges.
*   **Phase 1 (Axe Flurry):** Parashurama launches sweeping physical strikes and leaps.
*   **Phase 2 (Earthquake Slams):** Parashurama slams his axe into the ground, creating volcanic fissures.
*   **Phase 3 (The Bow Duel):** Parashurama hands Rama his own divine bow (Vishnu's bow) and challenges him to draw it. Rama draws it and aims it at Parashurama's spiritual merits, forcing him to concede.
*   **Mythological Defeat:** Recognizing Rama as his successor avatar, Parashurama loses his anger, bows to Rama, and retires to the mountains.

---

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Janaka:** *"Princes and kings, many have tried to lift Shiva's wrath. If none succeed today, Mithila will remain without a son."*
*   **Vishwamitra:** *"Rama, step forward. Lift the bow, my child."*
*   **Parashurama:** *"Who has broken the bow of my master, Shiva? Draw your weapon, prince, for your royal blood will feed my axe!"*
*   **Rama (calmly):** *"I broken the bow in a test of union, not pride, noble Sage. If you seek my life, my hands are open. But if you seek the truth, behold who stands before you."*

---

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Rama & Lakshmana:** Bright white and yellow silk wedding garments embroidered with golden thread, crown, and flower garlands.
*   **Sita:** Gorgeous red and gold silk bridal saree, heavy emerald and gold jewelry, and a translucent veil.
*   **Parashurama:** Long dark dreadlocks, tiger-skin wrap, soot-smeared body, carrying a massive steel battleaxe.

---

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Royal Rama, Royal Lakshmana, Bridal Sita, King Janaka, Parashurama, The Pinaka (Colossal bow on iron cart).
*   **Environments:** Mithila Marriage Courtyard (floral decorations, red carpets), Volcanic mountain pass (cracked red soil, dust wind).
*   **Particle Effects:** Ethereal gravity waves around the bow, bow snapping shockwaves, ground fissure lava, golden dust trails for perfect parries.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* Triumphant shehnai, sitar, and classical temple bells for the wedding; terrifying thunderous cello and dark tribal drums for Parashurama's appearance.
*   *SFX:* Bow cracking like thunder, axe clashing on bow, earth-shaking footsteps of Parashurama.

### Level Design Elements
*   Active gravity-well mechanics (pulls player to the center).
*   Timing-based parry meter HUD element.
