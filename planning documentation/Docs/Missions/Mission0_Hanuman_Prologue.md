# Mission 0: Hanuman's Solar Leap (Prologue)

*   **Playable Protagonist:** Kid Hanuman (Playable Youth State)
*   **Reveals & Transitions to:** Prince Rama (Ayodhya Royal Chamber)
*   **Theme & Focus:** Playful innocence, divine limitlessness, and the tragedy of forgotten power.

---

## 1. Scene & Environment

*   **Primary Active Scene:** [Scene: Sumeru Stratosphere](../Scenes/Scene_0_Sumeru_Stratosphere.md) (`SCENE_SUMERU_STRATOSPHERE`)
*   **Key Environment Elements:** Vertical layers shifting from tropical Sumeru valleys up into the jet streams of the stratosphere, culminating in the golden solar gates.
*   **Key Interactive Objects:** [Object: Airavat's Tusks](../Objects/Object_Airavat_Tusks.md) (`OBJ_AIRAVAT_TUSKS`), floating temporary cloud pads, Deva solar chariot rails.

---

## 2. Basic Flow of Steps

1.  **Tutorial & Play:** Kid Hanuman starts in Sumeru's orchards. The player learns basic movement, climbing, and high-velocity double leaps.
2.  **Monster Hunt (Orchard Chase):** Capture forest imps stealing Anjana's sacred fruits.
3.  **Vertical Puzzle Ascent:** Navigate Mount Sumeru's high thermal updrafts to reach the clouds.
4.  **The Sun Pursuit:** Sprint across solid cloud pads in the stratosphere, chasing the rising Sun (believing it to be a giant golden mango).
5.  **Boss Battle 1 (Airavat):** Defeat/bypass the guardian elephant of Indra's gates.
6.  **Boss Battle 2 (King Indra):** Survive Indra's bullet-hell lightning barrage in the storm fields.
7.  **The Transition Nerf:** Endure the Vajra strike, fall to earth, receive boons, but get cursed to forget all powers.
8.  **Reveal:** Camera zooms out across the Sarayu river, revealing the royal cradle of Prince Rama in Ayodhya.

---

## 3. Fun Monster Hunting

### Chase of the Tyakshas (Forest Imps)
*   **Mechanic:** Forest imps are stealing sacred fruits from Mother Anjana's grove. They leap rapidly between high vines and rock arches, throwing prickly seeds.
*   **Combat Flow:** Kid Hanuman must use his high speed and tail-swing grapple mechanics to swing behind them. He cannot kill them (due to his playful nature); instead, he tickles and tackles them, forcing them to drop the fruits into his basket.
*   **Enemy Type:** *Tyakshas* — High-speed, small, green wood-sprites that double-jump and camouflage in leaves.

---

## 4. Fun Puzzle Solving

### Thermal Wind Updrafts (Vayu Gati)
*   **Mechanic:** To climb Sumeru's peaks, the player must navigate giant thermal wind columns generated by Hanuman's father, Vayu (Wind God).
*   **Puzzle Elements:** 
    *   The player must jump into a thermal column, using leaf glides to ascend.
    *   Rotate and align ancient stone windwheels on the cliffside to redirect the wind currents towards floating stone pads.
    *   Time leaps between moving cloud platforms that dissipate if Hanuman stays on them for more than 3 seconds.

---

## 5. Boss Encounters

### Boss 1: Airavat (Celestial Ivory Guardian)
*   **Phase 1 (The Tusks Climb):** Airavat charges across a cloud bridge, creating shockwaves. The player must dodge his stomps, grapple onto his ivory tusks, and scale his colossal neck to snatch the glowing golden mango key from his collar.
*   **Phase 2 (Trunk Lash):** Airavat sweeps the screen with water blasts and trunk whips. Hanuman must perform wall-jumps off Airavat's ears to plant a playful tickle behind his ear, putting the giant elephant to sleep.

### Boss 2: Lord Indra (King of Devas)
*   **Combat Arena:** Stormy sky-field filled with crackling blue lightning.
*   **Mechanics:** Indra rides his golden chariot, launching lightning spear strikes. The player must dodge expanding lightning rings on the cloud floor, using wind sprints to close the distance.
*   **Survival Check:** The battle is a high-speed survival fight. Once Hanuman reaches Indra's chariot and grabs his golden crown, a cinematic cutscene triggers.

---

## 6. The Character Nerf & Transition

### The Curse of Forgetfulness
*   **The Strike:** Furious at the mortal monkey's insolence, Indra fires his ultimate weapon, the **Vajra (Thunderbolt)**, striking Hanuman on the jaw (*Hanu*).
*   **The Fall:** Hanuman falls unconscious to earth. Vayu, enraged, withdraws the air from the cosmos, forcing the Devas to intervene.
*   **The Boons & Nerf (The Curse):** Lord Brahma restores Hanuman, and all deities grant him absolute boons (invincibility to weapons, fire, and death). However, due to his mischievous childhood antics of disturbing sages' sacrifices, the Sages lay a **heavy, spiritual lock (nerf) on him**:
    > *Hanuman will completely forget his divine strength, flight, and shape-shifting. His powers will remain dormant, locked under a deep spiritual seal, until he meets Lord Rama in the forests of Kishkindha.*
*   **Character Swap:** Hanuman's glowing orange aura fades, his model shrinks to a normal forest monkey, and he sits peacefully on Rishyamukha Hill. The camera pans upwards, flying over mountain ranges, across rivers, and enters a quiet, golden palace chamber in Ayodhya. A divine child crying is heard, and the screen reveals Prince Rama.

## 7. Dialogue & Narrative Scripts

### Dialogue Segment: The Vault of Sumeru
*   **Location:** Mount Sumeru peak.
*   **Characters:** Anjana (Mother), Kid Hanuman, Vayu (Voice in the wind).

```markdown
Anjana: (Calling out) "Hanuman! Do not climb so high, child! The sun is rising, and the sky belongs to the gods!"

Kid Hanuman: (Laughs, jumping on a floating cloud) "But look, Mother! The gods have hung a giant, golden mango in the sky! It is glowing, and my stomach rumbles!"

Vayu: (Deep wind echo) "Go, son! Leap upon the wind that I blow. The sky is your playground, but beware the thunder of the heavens!"

Kid Indra: (Appearing on his chariot, crackling with lightning) "Who is this forest child who dares to trespass into the solar chariot's path? Stand back, or feel the wrath of the Vajra!"
```

## 8. Weapons & Equipment Used

*   **Kid Fists (Melee):** Fast, non-lethal punching attacks that stagger minor targets.
*   **Primal Tail (Utility):** Used as a grappling hook to latch onto vines, ancient stone arches, and Airavat's tusks ([Object: Airavat's Tusks](../Objects/Object_Airavat_Tusks.md) / `OBJ_AIRAVAT_TUSKS`).
*   **The Mango Basket (Quest Object):** Holds the collected fruits, acting as a progress indicator.
