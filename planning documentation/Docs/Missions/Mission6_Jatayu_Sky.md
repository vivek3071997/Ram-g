# Mission 6: The Fall of the Vulture King

*   **Playable Protagonist:** Jatayu (Playable Celestial Vulture King State)
*   **Reveals & Transitions to:** Hanuman (Playable Prime State on Rishyamukha Hill)
*   **Theme & Focus:** Aerial velocity, titanic scale dogfights, sacrificial bravery, and completing the campaign loop to unlock dormant power.

---

## 1. Scene & Environment

### Setting: The Stormy Stratosphere & The Sacred Mound of Rishyamukha
*   **Environment Elements:**
    *   *The Cloud Canyons:* Colossal, towering columns of dark storm clouds crackling with purple lightning. The wind speeds are extreme, generating swirling air vortexes.
    *   *The Pushpaka Vimana:* A majestic, golden flying fortress chariot adorned with ivory carvings, mechanical sails, and glowing crystal shields, soaring at supersonic speeds.
    *   *Rishyamukha Hill:* A peaceful, green mountain peak rising above a sea of forest mist under a golden sunset sky, serving as the sanctuary of the Vanara exiles.
*   **Atmospheric Mood:** High-adrenaline, cinematic, stormy, and epic, shifting to quiet, sacred hope in the final sequence.
*   **Key Objects:** The Pushpaka Shield Cores, Chandrahasa (Ravana's Moon-Blade), fallen gold ornaments of Sita, and the sacred peak of Rishyamukha.

---

## 2. Basic Flow of Steps

1.  **Vimana Pursuit (Flight Tutorial):** Play as the giant vulture king Jatayu. The player learns high-speed flight mechanics, aerodynamic barrel-rolls, and wind-tunnel gliding while chasing the fleeing Pushpaka Vimana.
2.  **Air Shield Raid (Monster Hunt):** Destroy the escorts. Clear waves of flying demon guards (*Vimanadevas*) launched from the deck of the Vimana using razor-sharp talon sweeps.
3.  **Vortex Alignment (Puzzle):** Navigate through a series of violent sky vortexes. The player must align charging lightning clouds to form a super-speed slipstream, boosting Jatayu past the chariot's heavy turret defenses.
4.  **Boss Battle 1 (The Chariot Core Shields):** Land on the flying fortress's outer wings and destroy the three glowing crystal generators, breaking Ravana's primary shield.
5.  **Boss Battle 2 (King Ravana - The Sky Duel):** Duel Ravana on the main deck of the Pushpaka Vimana. Avoid his ten-headed visual illusions and massive dark fire bursts.
6.  **The Transition Nerf (Chandrahasa Wing Clip):** With his shield shattered, Ravana draws his ultimate weapon—**Chandrahasa (The Moon-Blade of Shiva)**. A cinematic plays where Ravana clips Jatayu’s wings, reducing his flight capability and health bar to zero.
7.  **The Final Message:** Jatayu plummets down through the clouds, crashing onto the forest floor. As Rama and Lakshmana run to his side, Jatayu uses his final breath to deliver the crucial news: *"Ravana, the king of Lanka, has stolen Sita and flown south."*
8.  **The Circle Completes (Reveal):** Jatayu dissolves into a peaceful spiritual light. The camera ascends from the forest, flying south across massive mountain peaks, and lands on the sunlit peak of Rishyamukha Hill. The camera focuses on the small, quiet forest monkey Hanuman. As he hears Rama's name carried by the wind, the curse shatters: his eyes glow gold, his body expands to prime height, and a colossal orange flame aura erupts around him. Prime Hanuman is unlocked!

## 3. Fun Monster Hunting

### Aerial Interception of Vimanadevas (Chariot Escorts)
*   **Mechanic:** Flying demon soldiers carrying celestial spears are surrounding the Pushpaka Vimana, firing tracking energy bolts to keep Jatayu away.
*   **Combat Flow:** Playing as Jatayu, the player must utilize **Vayu-Kata (Wind Dive)** to lock onto targets and execute high-speed talon slashes. Performing a barrel roll at the exact moment a spear is thrown deflects the projectile back, destroying adjacent escorts in chain reactions.
*   **Enemy Type:** *Vimanadevas* — Ranged aerial soldiers with high maneuverability, vulnerable to diving strike staggers.

---

## 4. Fun Puzzle Solving

### Storm Gate Slipstream (Vayu-Vyuha)
*   **Context:** The Pushpaka Vimana enters a dense storm barrier, creating a wall of lightning gates that will instantly vaporize any organic creature.
*   **Puzzle Mechanics:**
    *   The sky contains glowing positive (+) and negative (-) electrical clouds.
    *   Jatayu must fly through the clouds in a specific sequence (balancing the charges) to neutralize the lightning fields.
    *   *The Wind Tunnel:* Successfully balancing a gate generates a high-velocity wind tunnel slipstream, boosting Jatayu's flight speed by 300% and launching him through the storm wall.

## 5. Boss Encounters

### Boss 1: The Chariot Core Shields (Vimana Hull Raid)
*   **Combat Arena:** The moving, vibrating golden wings of the Pushpaka Vimana, with clouds rushing past at extreme speeds.
*   **Boss Mechanics:**
    *   *Hull Defense:* Three crystal generator cores are protected by automatic harpoon turrets that fire massive chains to pin Jatayu down.
    *   *The Destruction Flow:* The player must dodge the harpoons, grab the chains in their talons, and pull them into the crystal cores, forcing them to overheat. Once exposed, Jatayu must perform a **Talon Smash** to shatter the core.

### Boss 2: Demon King Ravana (The Lord of Lanka)
*   **Combat Arena:** The wide, golden ceremonial deck of the Pushpaka Vimana.
*   **Boss Mechanics:**
    *   *Ten-Headed Illusions:* Ravana creates multiple holographic copies of himself. The player must observe the shadow trails to identify the one true Ravana (the only one casting a solid physical shadow).
    *   *Asuric Firestorms:* Ravana launches massive, slow-moving balls of dark purple fire that cover the deck. The player must take flight to hover above the flames, performing descending dive-bomb attacks to break Ravana's spellcast.

## 6. The Character Nerf & Transition

### The Wing Clipping of Chandrahasa
*   **The Strike:** Enraged by the bird's persistence, Ravana draws the **Chandrahasa (The crescent-shaped sword gifted by Lord Shiva)**. The blade shines with a cold, blinding silver light that pierces the dark storm clouds.
*   **The Ultimate Nerf (Wing-Clipping Fall):**
    *   *The Wing Slice:* In a dramatic cinematic, Ravana executes a swift silver slash, severing Jatayu's primary wing joints.
    *   *The Flight Strip:* All flight mechanics are permanently deactivated. The flight control UI disappears.
    *   *The Crash:* Jatayu's health drops to 1%. The screen shakes violently as he plummets through 10,000 feet of clouds, crashing into a forest clearing near the Godavari River.
*   **The Final Report:** Prince Rama and Lakshmana discover the dying bird. Jatayu gasps out the vital intel: *"I fought him... Ravana... he took Mother Sita... to the south..."* before closing his eyes in peaceful death.
*   **The Circle Closes & Prime Reveal:**
    *   The camera pulls away from Jatayu's resting body. It sails over the dense forest canopy, crossing rivers and plains, and arrives at the rocky, tranquil peak of Rishyamukha Hill.
    *   Sitting in silent meditation is a quiet, normal forest monkey—Hanuman.
    *   As Rama's desperate call echoes through the wind, a spark of golden divine light enters Hanuman's heart.
    *   *The Curse shatters:* A majestic golden shockwave erupts. Hanuman's small frame grows to a towering, muscular giant, his eyes ignite with solar flame, and his divine mace (*Gada*) crystallizes in his hand.
    *   **The Loop is Complete:** The game displays: *"Hanuman's Power Restored. The Path to Lanka is Open."* The first half of the game concludes, unlocking Hanuman's prime state for the next act.

*   **Characters:** Jatayu, Ravana, Princess Sita.

Princess Sita: (Trapped inside the chariot's crystal cage, crying out) "Uncle Jatayu! Beware his sword! He is not a mere king—he is the master of ten directions!"

## 8. Weapons & Equipment Used

*   **Vajra Talons (Melee):** Colossal claws that deal crushing physical damage to shields and armor.
*   **Vayu-Paksha (Wind Blast):** Flapping wings to create localized hurricanes, pushing away minor enemies and deflecting incoming energy projectiles.
*   **Chandrahasa (Boss Weapon - Narrative Lock):** Ravana's ultimate sword that inflicts instant-nerf status upon hit during the final cinematic transition.
