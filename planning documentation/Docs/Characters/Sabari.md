## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **The tasted Berries (Bhakti Buffs):** Sabari acts as a specialized forest checkpoint merchant. She trades wild forest berries (*Jujube*) which she has tasted to ensure their sweetness. Eating these berries fully restores health, increases maximum health by 20%, and provides a passive protection against poison for 1 hour.
*   **Bhakti Sanctum (Safe Zone Checkpoint):** Sitting by Sabari's campfire creates an absolute safe zone. No enemies can spawn or attack within a 50-meter radius, acting as a crucial checkpoint before the Kishkindha trials.
*   **Rishyamukha Guidance:** Sabari reveals the path to Mount Rishyamukha and Jambavan, mapping key terrain choke points for the player.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Basket of Devotion:** A simple wicker basket containing forest berries and flowers, used to distribute healing items.
*   **Tapas Ash:** A small pouch of holy ash that can be thrown to instantly dissolve minor demon illusions.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Sabari is an ascetic from a low forest tribe, who lived in the ashram of her guru, Sage Matanga. When the sage passed away, he told Sabari that her patient devotion would be rewarded and that Lord Rama would personally visit her ashram. She waited for decades, sweeping the forest path every day, gathering fresh berries, and tasting each one to make sure only the sweetest were offered. During Rama's search for Sita in Act 6, he and Lakshmana visited her hermitage. Deeply touched by her pure love, Rama ate the half-bitten berries, attaining her liberation. She then advised Rama to seek the alliance of Sugriva on Mount Rishyamukha before self-immolating in a fire of pure light, ascending to the heavens.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 6 (The Ashram of Matanga):** Act as the core narrative checkpoint. Wash Rama's feet and present the tasted berries in a highly emotional, cinematic interaction.
*   **Act 6 (The Path Reveal):** Trade items, upgrade potions, and obtain the map coordinates for Mount Rishyamukha.
*   **Act 6 (The Ascending Flame):** Triggers the final cinematic where Sabari, having seen her lord, immolates in a column of gold light, leaving behind a permanent spiritual waypoint that heals the player.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Serene, deeply emotional, and devotional folk theme featuring a single-string Ektara, soft wooden hand-clappers (Kartal), slow bamboo flutes, and a gentle elderly female vocal chanting.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Very slow, peaceful tempo set in Raga Bhairavi (representing early morning devotion, victory over ego, and ultimate liberation), written in E Major.
*   **Integration:** Ashram exploration features quiet Ektara humming and ambient forest sounds; her ascension triggers a grand, soaring string choir that slowly fades into wind.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Incredibly slow, shuffling sandaled slides; the clicking of a simple wooden staff on dry leaves and soil.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Gentle, trembling elderly female voice; slow, peaceful breathing; soft devotional humming.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Throwing Tapas ash creates a soft, sweeping wind hiss followed by a gentle glass-chime sound; berry eating generates a crisp chewing sound.
