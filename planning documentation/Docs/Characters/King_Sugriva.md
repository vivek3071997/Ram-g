## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Vanara Command:** Sugriva blows his war horn, summoning a squad of 4 Vanara skirmishers to fight alongside the player. These scouts distract bosses, climb structures to drop obstacles, and throw rocks.
*   **Surya Gati (Solar Leap):** Leaps up to 15 meters, crashing down with fire-infused fists that deal area-of-effect heat damage.
*   **Grateful Vow:** Passive buff. When fighting alongside Lord Rama, Sugriva's defense is increased by 30%, and his companion cooldowns recover twice as fast.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Sun-Mace (Surya Gada):** A copper-reinforced mace blessed by his father Surya (the Sun God). Each heavy hit inflicts minor fire damage, burning targets for 3 seconds.
*   **Boulder Throw:** Sugriva rips a massive stone block from the ground and hurls it at target barriers, breaking enemy shields.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Sugriva is the younger son of the sun god Surya and the brother of Vali. After being falsely accused of treason by his brother Vali, Sugriva was beaten, exiled, and had his wife Ruma abducted. He lived in terror on Mount Rishyamukha (where Vali could not enter due to a sage's curse). Hanuman introduced him to Lord Rama, who forged a sacred pact of friendship over fire. Rama shot Vali, restoring Sugriva to his throne. Although Sugriva temporarily forgot his vow due to royal luxuries, Lakshmana's stern warning reminded him. Sugriva immediately mobilized the entire global Vanara army, leading them to Lanka and fighting heroically in the frontlines.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 6 (Mount Rishyamukha Alliance):** Forge the alliance with Lord Rama, completing the sacred fire ritual and outlining the search boundaries.
*   **Act 7 (The Kishkindha Duel):** Duel Vali in the palace courtyard, acting as the decoy while Rama positions himself for the sniping shot.
*   **Acts 8 - 10 (The War Campaign):** Act as the Commander-in-Chief of the Vanara army, coordinating siege assaults on Lanka's gates and dueling Rakshasa bosses (slaying Praghasa and Kumbha).

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Energetic, rhythmic, and triumphant tribal theme featuring upbeat wooden percussion (Mrida), soaring flutes, traditional folk bagpipes, and rapid group vocal chants.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Double-tempo, high-energy theme set in Raga Bhupali (bright, pentatonic scale representing joy and heroic resolution), written in D Major.
*   **Integration:** In-game companion combat features rhythmic drum loops; commander segments trigger triumphant brass and drum fanfares as he commands his troops.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rapid, scraping monkey claw thuds on soil; metallic jangling of chest-armor plates.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Sharp, energetic commanding barks; high-pitched battle calls; gravelly, resolute breathing under exhaustion.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Mace swings produce whistling wind-shears; boulder impacts sound like massive rock slides; summoning scouts triggers a booming wooden war-horn blow.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Glowing golden solar vapors rising from his shoulders when his health drops below 30%, increasing his attack speed.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Footsteps leave small dusty clouds; dirt particles rise in small circles when he crouches.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding circles of bright orange solar light expanding outwards; fire trails coiling around his mace during heavy hits.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of orange sparks and volcanic dust particles upon mace impact, leaving burning ground cracks.
