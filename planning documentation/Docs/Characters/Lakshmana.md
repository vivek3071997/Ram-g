## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Vigilant Stance (No-Sleep Buff):** Lakshmana vowed not to sleep for 14 years to protect Rama and Sita. In-game, this translates to a passive immune to sleep-inducing spells and stun effects.
*   **Lakshmana Rekha (Protective Ward):** Creates a burning, glowing blue barrier on the ground. Enemies crossing it take massive fire damage and are blown back. (Used to defend static points or escort sequences).
*   **Shesha Siphon:** Passive resistance to all venom and poison-based attacks (due to being the avatar of Adi Shesha, the cosmic serpent).

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Twin Blades (Kripana):** Hand-to-hand dual-wield daggers used for fast melee combos when enemies breach the bow range.
*   **Saumitra Bow:** A lightweight wooden recurve bow designed for high fire rate and multi-shot volleys (fires 3 arrows simultaneously).
*   **Saumitrastra (Lethal Bolt):** An concentrated energy shot that pierces through a single line of enemies, dealing massive impact damage (used to defeat Indrajit).

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Lakshmana is the son of King Dasharatha and Queen Sumitra, and the twin brother of Shatrughna. He is the earthly incarnation of Adi-Shesha, the multi-headed cosmic serpent upon whom Lord Vishnu rests. From childhood, Lakshmana was inseparable from Rama. When Rama chose exile, Lakshmana insisted on following him, leaving behind his wife Urmila. Throughout the exile, he served as the builder of their cottages, gatherer of food, and guardian. During the war in Lanka, he fought heroically, surviving a near-fatal wound from Indrajit's Shakti lance, and eventually slayed Indrajit.

### Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 2 (Youth):** Support Rama in the gurukul, defeat the minor demon armies, and guard the marriage ceremony in Mithila.
*   **Acts 3 - 5 (Forest):** Construct the ashrams (Chitrakoot, Panchavati), gather resources, defend the perimeter, and cut Shurpanakha's nose when she attacks Sita.
*   **Acts 6 - 8 (Alliance & March):** Help forge the alliance with the Vanaras, track Sita's captor, and lead the charge across the Rama Setu.
*   **Act 9 - 10 (War & Victory):** Fight and slay Indrajit, recover from the Shakti lance using the Sanjeevani herb, protect Rama's flank in the final battle, and return to Ayodhya.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Fast, dynamic acoustic-electric hybrid featuring energetic Sitar, aggressive hand-drums (Tabla, Dholak), and sweeping string sections that convey swift motion.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** High-tempo, intense, set in Raga Bhairav (representing vigilance and fierce energy), played in E minor.
*   **Integration:** In-game companion battles feature rhythmic Sitar riffs; during boss combat (e.g., against Indrajit), his theme swells with high-tempo percussion and string movements to highlight his fast-paced playstyle.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rapid, light leather-slipper clicks, producing brief bursts of wind rustle and tiny static crackles when sprinting.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Sharp, high-energy battle grunts; fierce shouts during dual-blade combat strikes; intense, breathy war cries when summoning the Lakshmana Rekha.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Twin-blade sweeps produce high-pitched metal-slitting swooshes; arrow firing sounds like high-tension whip cracks; lightning crackles upon dual-blade strikes.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A flickering, electric light-green speed aura surrounding his limbs, with faint crackles of emerald static discharging when standing idle.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Wind leaves and green sparks swirl upward behind his steps, leaving temporary glowing green speed-trails during sprints.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Blinding burst of green sheet lightning emanating from his weapons, accompanied by a spinning geometric Sanskrit ward (the Rekha) scorched into the soil in brilliant azure-blue flames.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Sharp, electric-green jagged sparks ejecting upon physical or ranged impact, leaving briefly sizzling lines in the air.
