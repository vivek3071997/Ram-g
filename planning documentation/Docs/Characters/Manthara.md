## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Whisper Poison (Intrigue Mechanic):** Manthara does not engage in direct combat. Instead, she is an active environmental shadow agent. Her whispers silently corrupt relationships, raising the "Suspicion" meter in palace dialogs. This unlocks hidden, high-corruption dialogue paths for Kaikeyi.
*   **Shadow Infiltration:** Can move through palace hallways completely undetected, bypassing royal guards to gather intelligence or plant items.
*   **Intrigue Focus:** Passive buff. When active in a palace sector, player dialogue options are limited, forcing more defensive or hostile choices.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Crooked Cane:** A heavy, gnarled dark wood walking cane used to tap the floor, sending out small waves of vibration that reveal hidden passages.
*   **Poisoned Whispers:** Concentrated dialogue tags that inflict "Doubt" on targets, draining their confidence gauge.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Manthara is the wicked, hunchbacked wet-nurse and maid of Queen Kaikeyi, whom she accompanied from the kingdom of Kekeya to Ayodhya. Possessing deep political cunning and carrying personal resentment toward Prince Rama, she watched with jealousy as the city prepared for Rama's coronation. Approaching a sleeping Kaikeyi, she used absolute deceit, poison whispers, and worst-case scenarios to convince the queen that Rama's coronation would lead to Bharata's exile and death. Her manipulation succeeded in turning the queen against Rama, triggering the exile and the subsequent collapse of the royal family. She was later beaten and dragged by Shatrughna before Bharata pardoned her.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 2 (The Poisoning of the Mind):** Act as the shadow catalyst. The player must intercept and track Manthara through the palace gardens as she whispers to Kaikeyi, completing a high-difficulty stealth tailing quest.
*   **Act 3 (The Confrontation):** Triggers the cinematic where Shatrughna discovers her boasting of her success, dragging her through the court until Bharata intervenes to preserve family honor.
*   **Act 3 (Palace Exit):** Banished from the court, withdrawing into the shadow corridors of Ayodhya.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Sinister, quiet, and unsettling theme featuring crawling Esraj strings, low-pitched dry bamboo shakers, slow-tempo Santoor hits, and whispering dry wind soundscapes.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Very slow, unstable tempo, set in Raga Jog (evoking shadows, greed, and dark intent), written in a dark F# minor scale.
*   **Integration:** Stealth and tailing sequences feature highly ambient, whisper-heavy loops; her dialogue drops out all melodic instruments, leaving only the dry tapping of her cane.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rhythmic, slow tapping of a wooden cane on polished marble floors, accompanied by quiet, shuffling sandalled slides and wheezing breath.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Raspy, wheezing elderly female voice; quiet, calculating giggles; sharp, frightened shrieks when dragged.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Cane taps generate low-frequency hollow woody thuds; whispers sound like dry leaves coiling in a drafty hallway.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A thin, coiling trail of dark purple and grey shadow threads rising from her hunch and coiling around her cane when walking.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Floor reflections look distorted near her path; dust devils swirl briefly around her feet.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding circles of dark purple smoke and green poison ribbons coiling outwards; glowing silver runes of "Doubt" coiling around target queens.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of dark purple ash and grey dust upon impact, draining the player's confidence gauge.
