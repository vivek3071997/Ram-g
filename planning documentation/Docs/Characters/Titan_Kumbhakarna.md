## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Colossal Invincibility (Shadow Weak-Spots):** Kumbhakarna's thick skin is immune to direct damage from standard attacks. The player must scale his colossal limbs using Lord Rama's *Grapple Arrow* or Hanuman's support throws to target highlighted weak spots (ankles, chest scars, throat, and eyes) while dodging giant sweeps.
*   **Tamas Sleep-Fog:** When forced to wake up early, Kumbhakarna releases dark blue sleep vapors from his chest. Any player caught in this fog has their stamina recovery reduced by 80% and is put to sleep if they stay inside for over 5 seconds.
*   **Siege-Sweep:** Sweeps his giant iron trident across the battlefield in a 180-degree arc, shattering all environmental cover blocks (rocks, towers) and knocking back the player.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Titan Trident:** A massive, 40-meter iron spear used to execute ground pierce attacks, causing geysers of stone and dirt to erupt from the ground.
*   **Rock-Crusher Fists:** Colossal hand strikes that pulverize anything in their impact zones.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Kumbhakarna is the younger brother of Ravana and Vibhishana. Possessing immense power, he performed intense penance to Lord Brahma. Fearing his appetite and strength, the gods manipulated his tongue when he asked for a boon. Instead of *Indrasana* (throne of Indra), he asked for *Nidrasana* (bed of sleep). Brahma granted him the boon of sleeping for six months at a time, waking for only one day to eat. During the siege of Lanka, Ravana forced his servants to wake Kumbhakarna early using thousands of drums and trampling elephants. Despite severely warning Ravana about the unrighteous nature of his actions, Kumbhakarna marched out of family duty, crushed the Vanara armies, and was eventually slain by Rama in a dramatic combat sequence.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 9 (The Awakening Cinema):** Complete a puzzle-action sequence inside the Lanka vault to wake Kumbhakarna up, defending the perimeter from demon guards while the giant shifts in his sleep.
*   **Act 9 (The Colossal Siege Boss):** Fight Kumbhakarna at Lanka's main gates. The player must coordinate with the Vanara army, using catapults, grapple arrows, and aerial leaps to climb his colossal frame.
*   **Act 9 (Sacrificial End):** Triggers a final emotional dialogue cinematic as his severed limbs fall, with the giant expressing gratitude to Rama for liberating his soul.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Monumental and tragic orchestral theme featuring colossal war-drums (Dundubhi), screaming bronze horns, resonant low cellos, and a massive, slow-tempo male choir chanting funeral mantras.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Heavy, slow, crushing tempo, set in Raga Bhairav (Tandava style), written in a dark C# minor key.
*   **Integration:** The raid boss duel features deep, rolling percussion loops that shake the audio field; his sweeping attacks trigger explosive brass crescendos that amplify the threat level.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Earthquake thuds that shake the player's screen and rumble low frequencies; deafening metallic armor plates grinding together.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Mountain-shaking throat growls; thunderous snores and groans; a sad, resonant voice during his final meeting with Vibhishana.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Trident sweeps produce screaming wind-shears; fist impacts sound like massive rock collapses.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Swirling columns of thick dust, mountain rock particles, and blue sleep vapors rising from his massive chest and limbs.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Trees splinter and ground cracks open under his steps; high winds swirl leaves and sand outward from his sweeping blows.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding shockwave rings of blue and grey energy expanding outwards; glowing red weak-spot runes flashing on his limbs.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of rock shards and gold sparks upon hitting weak spots, leaving trailing trails of red energy that bleed out.
