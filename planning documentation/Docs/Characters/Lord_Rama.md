## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Shabda-Bhedi Vidya (Sound-Targeting):** A passive focus mechanic. Rama can slow down time and pinpoint invisible or hidden targets purely by their acoustic footprints (used in Tataka and Maricha fights).
*   **Dharmic Focus (Aura Buff):** When fighting high-corruption bosses, Rama's spiritual gauge charges faster. Increases resistance to dark elemental attacks.
*   **Grapple Arrow:** Utilizes specialized arrows to grapple onto high structures, cliff faces, or giant bosses (e.g., Kumbhakarna).

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Kodanda Bow:** Rama's primary physical weapon. It has three upgrade tiers:
    1.  *Forest Bow (Valkala Arch):* Made of dense wood, fast fire rate, moderate damage.
    2.  *Royal Recurve (Ayodhya's Grace):* Sandstone reinforced, increased range and penetration.
    3.  *Suresha Kodanda:* Divine state, shoots celestial energy arrows, unlimited physical ammo.
*   **Brahmastra (Brahma's Missile):** Rama's ultimate Astra. Requires a full spiritual gauge, locks the player in an invocation stance, and fires a beam of pure light that bypasses all boss immunities.
*   **Agneyastra (Fire-Arrow):** Shoots explosive fire arrows that burn groups of minor demons.
*   **Varunastra (Water-Arrow):** Launches a heavy downpour arrow, freezing fire shields and extinguishing burning areas.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Born as the eldest son of King Dasharatha and Queen Kausalya in Ayodhya, Rama is the seventh incarnation of Lord Vishnu. Masked in human form, his life is a testament to the pursuit of Dharma despite crushing personal tragedies. From defending sages in his youth to enduring a fourteen-year forest exile and waging a cosmic war on Lanka to rescue his beloved wife Sita, Rama remains steady, compassionate, and unshakeable in his morals.

### Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 2 (Youth):** Master his training, protect the sacred sacrifices of Sage Vishwamitra, and string the Pinaka in Mithila to win Sita's hand.
*   **Acts 3 - 5 (Exile):** Maintain honor by accepting exile, protect the sages of Dandaka forest, and pursue the golden deer, leading to Sita's abduction.
*   **Acts 6 - 7 (Alliance):** Search for Sita, forge a pact with Sugriva and Hanuman, and defeat the tyrant Vali.
*   **Acts 8 - 10 (War):** Construct the ocean bridge, lead the Vanara army, vanquish Ravana, and return to Ayodhya to establish Ramarajya (the righteous reign).

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Epic ancient orchestra featuring classical Veena, soaring bamboo flutes (Bansuri), and resonant temple drums (Mridangam) elevated by a celestial Sanskrit choir.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Majestic, medium-slow tempo, set in Raga Bhairavi (traditional scale representing sunrise and spiritual victory), centering on the "Aditya Hrudaya Stotra" chant.
*   **Integration:** Exploration theme features gentle solo bamboo flutes; boss battle phases build into a full-scale orchestral and choir arrangement, culminating in triumphant choral chants upon victory.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Crisp, metallic ring of golden ankle bells layered with firm, resonant leather sandaled thuds upon stone or soil.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Calm, resonant baritone grunts; rhythmic meditative breathing during archery draws; clear, ringing vocalization of the sacred "OM" syllable during celestial weapon invocation.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** The deep, booming resonance of the Kodanda bowstring (like a thunderclap); sharp whistling wind shears for flying arrows; a singing, high-frequency hum when invoking astras.
