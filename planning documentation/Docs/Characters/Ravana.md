## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **The Ten Heads of Vice:** Ravana's ten heads represent his mastery over the six shastras and four Vedas, but in-game they manifest as the **Ten Vices** (Pride, Anger, Lust, Greed, Envy, Delusion, etc.). Each head casts a different school of magic:
    -   *Anger Head:* Casts fire storms.
    -   *Delusion Head:* Creates perfect shadow mirrors of the player.
    -   *Pride Head:* Reflects 20% of incoming physical damage.
*   **Absolute Boon Shield:** An active passive barrier. Ravana is 100% immune to damage from celestial beings, demigods, and monsters (due to his boon from Brahma). Only a mortal human (Rama) and normal forest beasts (Vanaras) can pierce this shield.
*   **Nabhi Core (Vulnerability):** A pool of divine nectar (*Amrita*) lies hidden in his navel, granted by Brahma. If the navel is intact, his severed heads and limbs regenerate instantly in 3 seconds.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Chandrahasa (The Moon-Blade):** A curved glowing sword gifted by Shiva. Launches crescent-shaped energy blades that slice through environment obstacles.
*   **Trishula (Demon Spear):** A heavy throwing spear that splits into three homing lightning bolts.
*   **Maya-Astra (Illusion Storm):** A spell that covers the combat arena in dark purple fog, swapping the player's controls and spawning phantom attackers.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Ravana was born to the sage Vishrava and the Rakshasa princess Kaikesi, giving him a unique mix of high Brahmin intellect and demonic strength. After performing intense penances to Lord Brahma, he secured a boon of invincibility against gods and demons, foolishly omitting humans out of contempt. He conquered Lanka from his stepbrother Kubera, establishing a golden empire. His kidnapping of Sita, driven by revenge for his sister Shurpanakha and his own lust, sparked the epic war. Despite countless warnings from his brother Vibhishana, his hubris led him to fight to the bitter end.

### Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 4 (Preparation):** Build his empire, enslave the demigods, and guard his northern forest outpost under Khara and Dushana.
*   **Act 5 (The Plot):** Execute the plan to kidnap Sita, using Maricha as bait, and fly her to Lanka on the Pushpaka Vimana.
*   **Acts 6 - 9 (Defenses):** Coordinate Lanka's siege defenses, unleash his titan brother Kumbhakarna, and send his son Indrajit to wipe out the invaders.
*   **Act 10 (Final Stand):** Duel Lord Rama in the ruins of Lanka's golden courtyard, utilizing his ultimate forms, only to be destroyed by a Brahmastra shot straight into his navel.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Terrifying, dark operatic orchestra featuring thunderous war drums, dissonant low brass (trombones, tubas), screeching violin sections, and a massive, menacing male choir chanting the "Shiva Tandava Stotra."
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Moderate, driving, aggressive tempo set in Raga Bhairav (Tandava style of Lord Shiva's destructive dance), written in a dark C# minor key.
*   **Integration:** In-game cutscenes feature deep Sanskrit chants and low brass hums; the Act 10 multi-phase boss fight builds in tempo and vocal intensity, reaching a chaotic, apocalyptic crescendo as he manifests all ten heads and twenty arms.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Heavy, metallic clanging sandaled steps layered with low-frequency mechanical earth-thuds, vibrating the player's audio field.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Incredibly arrogant, multi-toned resonant laughs; rumbling demonic throat-growls; terrifying dual-vocal layering during powerful spellcasting as multiple heads vocalize in unison.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** The Chandrahasa Moon-Blade creates screaming, high-velocity metal-shearing swooshes; Trishula lightning bolts produce explosive crackles and dry electrical thunderclaps.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A thick, swirling shroud of dark purple and copper-bronze demonic energy (Asuric mist) that coils around his massive frame.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** The ground beneath his feet scorches and blackens; plants wither instantly, and small dust tornadoes whirl upwards near his hands.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant dark-red and purple shockwaves expanding outward; manifestation of ten heads creates holographic red energy projections of the heads floating behind him in a fan-like array.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Jagged copper sparks and bursts of dark violet shadow flames expanding from the point of impact, trailing black smoke trails that dissolve into embers.
