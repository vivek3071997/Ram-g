## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Maya Invisibility (Stealth Phase):** Indrajit vanishes into dark thunderclouds, becoming 100% invisible and untargetable. The player must switch to Lord Rama's *Shabda-Bhedi Vidya* or follow Lakshmana's direction indicators to track his footsteps and sword swing footprints purely by their audio-visual cues before striking.
*   **Meghnad (Thunder Roar):** Emits a terrifying sonic thunder blast that blinds and stuns all players in a 360-degree radius for 4 seconds, dealing moderate sonic damage.
*   **Nagapasha Bind:** Shoots a venomous serpent arrow that binds the target in heavy snake loops, causing a continuous damage-over-time poison tick and restricting movement. (Can only be purged by summoning Garuda).

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Chandrahasa Daggers:** Heavy curved dual daggers that slash with rapid speeds, dealing armor-shredding damage and inflicting poison status effects.
*   **The Storm Bow:** A dark iron bow that shoots high-frequency red lightning arrows.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Indrajit, originally named Meghanada because his birth cry sounded like thunder, is the eldest son of Ravana and Mandodari. He is widely considered the greatest mortal warrior in the epic, possessing absolute mastery over celestial weaponry, illusions (*Maya*), and stealth. He conquered heaven, defeated and bound the king of gods Indra (earning his title Indrajit), and was blessed by Brahma with a boon: before any battle, if he performs a fire ritual (*Nikumbhila Yajna*), he becomes completely invincible. During the war in Lanka, he twice defeated Rama and Lakshmana, binding them with the Nagapasha and knocking them unconscious with the Brahmashira weapon. He was eventually slain by Lakshmana, who disrupted his secret fire ritual with the help of Vibhishana.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 9 (The Serpent Bind Crisis):** Act as the main boss of the Lanka sky-fields. Duel Rama and Lakshmana, binding them with Nagapasha and forcing the player to initiate the Garuda summon sequence.
*   **Act 9 (Disruption of Nikumbhila):** Lead the stealth raid campaign into the secret temple of Nikumbhila. The player, controlling Lakshmana, must fight through waves of demon guards to disrupt Indrajit's fire ritual before he achieves invincibility.
*   **Act 9 (The Final Stand):** Duel Lakshmana in a high-speed dual-wield melee fight in the ruins of the temple, ending in a dramatic parry-counter sequence where Lakshmana slays him.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Terrifying, ultra-fast orchestral theme featuring frantic, aggressive drums, dissonant brass, sharp Sitar riffs, and high-pitch male choral shouts of thunder.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Double-tempo, chaotic tempo set in Raga Bhairav (martial Rudra scale), written in a dark D minor scale.
*   **Integration:** His stealth phases drop the music into a quiet, tense ambient track filled with whispering wind, crackling electricity, and soft, coiling snake soundscapes; his combat strikes burst with heavy percussion.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rapid, silent leather-boot slides accompanied by static crackling; rustle of scales in the wind.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Arrogant, echo-layered throat growls; sinister, whispering laughs that seem to come from all directions during invisibility.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Twin daggers produce screaming metallic sweeps; lightning arrows strike with high-frequency electricity snaps; conch blasts create crackling sonic booms.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Thick, coiling clouds of dark purple shadow mist and jagged red static electricity coiling around his weapons.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Grass and sand beneath him burn and scorch; lightning storms gather overhead when he enters his stealth phase.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding spheres of dark purple mist and red lightning coiling outwards; rotating serpentine shadows wrapping around bound targets.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of red lightning sparks and black shadow feathers upon impact, leaving sizzling trails in the air.
