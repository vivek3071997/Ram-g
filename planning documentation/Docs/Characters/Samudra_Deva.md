## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Tidal Barrier:** Can summon 30-meter wave walls that block passage. During the bridge-building sequence, he actively calms these tides to allow the player to drop stones without losing them to the current.
*   **Primordial Water Form:** Samudra Deva is immune to all physical strikes, cuts, and blunt forces. Physical arrows pass right through him. However, he is highly vulnerable to thermal energy ( Rama's fire-infused arrows).
*   **Setu Buoyancy (Floating Stone Mechanic):** Grants a passive buff to the stones inscribed with "RAM," allowing heavy mountain rocks to float on the water surface without sinking.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Trident of Tides:** A giant golden spear that channels high-pressure water lasers, sweeping across the arena and knocking back invaders.
*   **Varunastra Wave:** Invokes a crushing circular tidal wave that deals water damage and inflicts a "Soaked" debuff, doubling lightning damage taken.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Samudra Deva is the personification of the ocean. During the campaign to rescue Sita, the Vanara army reached the Southern Shore but found their path blocked by the vast, impassable ocean. Rama prayed and fasted for three days, asking Samudra Deva to pave a path. When the Ocean God did not respond, a furious Rama invoked a celestial weapon (the Brahmastra, aiming to boil the ocean and destroy all marine life). Terrified, Samudra Deva rose from the waves with folded hands, adorned in ocean gems. He explained that he is bound by the laws of nature and cannot simply divide, but revealed the secret: Nala, the Vanara architect, possesses the touch of floating stones, and if they build a bridge, he will keep the stones stable and float them to Lanka.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 8 (The Confrontation):** Act as an environmental narrative boss. The player must charge Rama's ultimate arrow to 100% to force Samudra Deva to emerge from the ocean.
*   **Act 8 (Bridge-Building Guide):** Interact with Nala and Samudra Deva to coordinate the floating stone placement, protecting the bridge under construction from deep-sea monster attacks.
*   **Act 8 (Bridge Complete):** Provide a stable walkway across the sea, ensuring the Vanara army can march safely.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Deep, booming, and ambient oceanic theme featuring heavy brass drones, resonant sea-shell horns (Shankha), low-frequency percussion thuds, and crystal-clear chimes.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Very slow, rolling tempo, set in F minor, creating a sense of crushing depth and primordial weight.
*   **Integration:** The confrontation features roaring ocean soundscapes with tense low strings; when Samudra Deva emerges, the music shifts to a grand, awe-inspiring choral chant with sweeping, fluid brass chords.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Churning, splashing water torrents; thunderous crashes of waves breaking on rocks; deep underwater bubbling.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Extremely deep, echo-layered baritone voice that sounds like a roaring tide; fluid, bubbly sighs under stress; resonant Sanskrit apologies.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Water jets generate high-velocity splashing whooshes; steam sizzles aggressively when Rama targets him with fire-infused arrows.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Falling sheets of translucent water and rising mist columns coiling around his colossal body; schools of glowing blue jellyfish and fish swimming inside his liquid torso.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Waves swell and crash in synchronization with his hand gestures; glowing sea-green footprints float on the water's surface before dissolving.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Blinding white water sprays and bubbling blue aura rings expanding outwards; glowing blue marine runes rotating on the ocean surface when Nala's stones are placed.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding splashes of clear water and steam clouds when struck by fire, accompanied by boiling water bubble rings.
