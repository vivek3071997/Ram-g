# Kaumodaki Gada (The Mace of Hanuman)

*   **Weapon Class:** Heavy Warhammer / Mace (Melee)
*   **Wielder:** Hanuman (Anjaneya)
*   **Mythological Origin:** Associated with the divine strength of Lord Vishnu (Kaumodaki), this massive golden mace represents absolute crushing force, protection, and earth-attunement. It is gifted to Hanuman, matching his diamond-bodied strength.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Kaumodaki Gada is designed for heavy, area-of-effect (AoE) melee combat, crushing shields and launching enemies:

### Base Combat Attributes
*   **Base Melee Damage:** 110 per swipe.
*   **Posture Break Rate:** 150% (Shatters minor enemy blocks instantly, staggering elite bosses).
*   **Attack Speed:** 0.8 swings/second (Slow, high-inertia swings).

### Special Combat Attacks
*   **Bhumipa Kampan (Earthquake Slam):** Hanuman leaps high into the air and slams the mace down, creating a 10-meter circular shockwave. minor enemies are launched into the air, and bosses are staggered for 2 seconds.
*   **Gada Whirlwind:** Hanuman spins with the mace, creating a vacuum that draws in nearby enemies, dealing multi-hit physical damage.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Wielding
*   **Polished Gold Flare:** The mace has a polished golden metallic texture, reflecting sunbeams with a warm, bright golden glare.
*   **Spiritual Weight:** The head of the mace constantly drips golden sparkles, representing concentrated spiritual force (*Prana*).

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ground Fracturing:** Ground slams visibly crack the stone floor, sending up dirt fragments and glowing golden fault lines.
*   **Swing Blur:** Slashes leave a wide, glowing golden circular ribbon of light, indicating the heavy momentum of the swing.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Loud, triumphant copper horns, fast-paced clay drums (*Mridangam*), and booming male choir chants (*"Jai Bajrangbali"*).
*   **Melodic Key:** C major (uplifting, unbreakable, powerful).
*   **Integration:** Heavy mace swings trigger brass accent beats in the background combat music.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A low, vibrating vocal chant mixed with the crackle of gathering electricity.
*   **Swing/Release SFX:** A deep, vacuum-like whoosh (*"WHOO-OOM"*), showing massive displacement of air.
*   **Impact SFX:** A thunderous, metallic dome strike (*"CLANGG-BOMM"*) followed by the crunch of breaking stone.
