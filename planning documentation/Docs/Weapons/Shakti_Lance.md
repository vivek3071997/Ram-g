# Shakti Lance (The Spear of Indrajit)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ranged / Throwable Magic Lance (Spell & Projectile)
*   **Wielder:** General Indrajit
*   **Mythological Origin:** An invincible divine weapon gifted to Indrajit by Brahma or Shiva through dark, sacrificial rituals. Wielded in Act 9, this spear delivers a near-fatal wound to Lakshmana, which can only be cured by the Himalayan *Sanjeevani* herb.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Shakti Lance is Indrajit's high-damage, phase-ending throw attack:

### Combat Attributes
*   **Base Damage:** 800 (Designed as a high-damage tactical strike).
*   **Special Trait:** *Near-Fatal Wound:* Direct hit inflicts a permanent "Mortal Bleed" debuff, reducing the target's maximum health pool by 5% every 3 seconds until a specific healing trigger (Sanjeevani) is activated.
*   **Stun Potential:** 100% (Stuns and locks the target in a downed state for 5 seconds upon impact).

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Charging Stance
*   **Sacrificial Vortex:** Dark crimson and purple energy spirals inward to the lance shaft, drawing light out of the immediate arena.
*   **Glowing Spearhead:** The tip of the lance shines with a blinding, toxic violet light that leaves screen-space glare lines.

### Throw & Impact
*   **Sonic Projectile:** The lance travels faster than all other projectiles, leaving a trail of sonic wind rings and purple electric static.
*   **The Shattered Soul:** Impact creates a circular burst of violet electric veins expanding outward on the ground, leaving a charred crater.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Apocalyptic brass, screeching strings, rapid war bells, and a terrifying, low chanting chorus.
*   **Melodic Key:** A# minor (dreadful, tragic, high-stakes).
*   **Integration:** Impact triggers a complete collapse of all high-tempo drums, leaving only a lingering, tragic solo female vocal lament as the wielder falls.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A high-speed, charging energy build hum (*"hummm-VREEE"*) that grows in pitch.
*   **Release/Throw SFX:** A sonic-boom crack (*"KA-CRACK"*) that echoes throughout the canyon walls.
*   **Impact SFX:** A sickening, electric energy blast (*"ZAP-CRUNCH"*) followed by a metallic ringing hiss that slowly fades out.
