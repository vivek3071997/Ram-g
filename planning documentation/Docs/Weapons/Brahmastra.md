# Brahmastra (The Absolute Missile of Brahma)

*   **Weapon Class:** Supreme Celestial Astra / Ultimate Finisher (Spell/Ranged)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama \| Sage Parashurama \| General Indrajit
*   **Mythological Origin:** Crafted by Lord Brahma, containing the supreme creative and destructive energy of the entire cosmos. It is the ultimate weapon of the epic, capable of incinerating worlds, and can only be invoked as a last resort to preserve cosmic order.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Brahmastra serves as the absolute "Ultimate Finisher" of the campaign, used to end the final boss fight in Act 10:

### Combat Attributes
*   **Spiritual Prana Cost:** 100% Aura Meter (Requires a fully charged gauge).
*   **Invocation Time:** 5.0 seconds (The wielder stands completely stationary, surrounded by a protective barrier, chanting the supreme mantra).
*   **Base Damage:** 10,000 (Absolute, non-mitigatable damage).
*   **Post-Impact Debuff:** *Disintegration:* Instantly disintegrates boss armor, shields, and regeneration systems.

### Usage Restrictions
*   **Cooldown:** One cast per mission.
*   **Intervention Check:** Wielding this weapon drains the player's stamina to 0 after impact, leaving them in a temporary fatigued state.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Invocation Stance
*   **The Pillar of Creation:** During the 5-second chant, a massive cylinder of white and gold light descends from the clouds, shielding the wielder.
*   **Orb of Energy:** A small, miniature sun of concentrated white-hot plasma floats at the center of the bow string.

### Release & Impact
*   **Solar Beam:** The arrow releases as a solid, high-intensity beam of solar fire that vaporizes the atmosphere in its path, creating heat waves.
*   **The Supernova Impact:** On impact, the screen flashes white for 0.5 seconds, followed by a massive, golden dome expansion of energy that disintegrates the target into gold dust.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** High-operatic choir, chanting Vedic hymns (*"Om Brahmane Namaha"*), backed by a massive symphonic orchestra with roaring timpani, crashing cymbals, and screaming trumpets.
*   **Melodic Key:** D minor (grand, apocalyptic, absolute).
*   **Integration:** The music completely overpowers all other sound channels during the 5-second invocation, building to a deafening operatic peak at the moment of release.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A roaring, vacuum-like hum of solar wind mixed with high-frequency choir voices and a crackling nuclear reaction.
*   **Release SFX:** A massive, explosive launch boom (*"KRA-THRUUUM!"*) that silences all ambient noise.
*   **Impact SFX:** A rolling, cosmic thunderous crash (*"VRA-RAAAAH"*) representing the destruction of the old and creation of the new.
