# Kodanda (The Arch of Rama)

*   **Weapon Class:** Longbow (Ranged)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama
*   **Mythological Origin:** Crafted by the divine architect Vishwakarma, later gifted to Lord Rama. The bow is synonymous with absolute precision, righteousness, and cosmic defense.

---

## 1. Combat & Upgrade Profiles

The Kodanda features three distinct combat tiers unlocked as the GDD campaign progresses:

### Tier 1: Forest Valkala Arch (Act 1 - 4)
*   **Base Damage:** 45 per normal arrow.
*   **Fire Rate:** 1.2 arrows/second.
*   **Effective Range:** 45 meters.
*   **Special Trait:** *Silent Drawing:* Drawing does not alert nearby minor Asura patrols.

### Tier 2: Royal Recurve (Act 5 - 7)
*   **Base Damage:** 85 per normal arrow.
*   **Fire Rate:** 0.9 arrows/second.
*   **Effective Range:** 80 meters.
*   **Special Trait:** *Shattering Draw:* Arrows pierce minor wooden shields and armor plates.

### Tier 3: Suresha Kodanda (Act 8 - 10)
*   **Base Damage:** 150 per spirit arrow.
*   **Fire Rate:** 1.5 arrows/second.
*   **Effective Range:** 150 meters (Infinite trajectory falloff).
*   **Special Trait:** *Sun-Core Focus:* Fires arrows made of pure solar energy. Arrows auto-target designated *Marma* (vital) weak points.

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Drawing & Charging
*   **Draw Line:** A bright, golden thread of light connects the bowtips, representing the string of spiritual *Prana*.
*   **Spiritual Condensation:** Shimmering gold particles draw from the air to condense into a crystalline arrow at the center.

### Arrow Release & Trajectory
*   **Missile Trail:** The arrow flies leaving a solid, glowing golden ribbon of light that fades after 1.5 seconds.
*   **Wind Displacer:** Creates minor cone-shaped wind rings surrounding the arrow shaft in flight, reflecting high speed.

### Impact Effects
*   **Friction Blast:** Impact sparks a small, sun-colored flash with radiating golden sparkles.
*   **Solar Sear:** Targets hit by the *Suresha Kodanda* burn with a faint, warm yellow aura, taking damage over time.

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Majestic, high-register wooden flute (*Bansuri*) accompanied by a solo classical *Veena* and slow temple *Dhamru* hand drums.
*   **Melodic Key:** E major (bright, heroic, stable).
*   **Integration:** Melodic runs build in frequency as the player draws the bow, peaking at full draw.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A low, vibrating spiritual chant (*OM*) mixed with a rising celestial chord as Rama draws the string.
*   **Release SFX:** A high-speed metallic hum and string snap (*"Twang-VRAAM"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A solid wood-splitting thud (*"TOCK-shhh"*) accompanied by a high-frequency spiritual chime on critical hits.
