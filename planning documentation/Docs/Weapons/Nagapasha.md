# Nagapasha (Serpent Noose of Indrajit)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ethereal Snare / Crowd Control (Spell/Astra)
*   **Wielder:** General Indrajit
*   **Mythological Origin:** A celestial weapon associated with Varuna (and later obtained by Indrajit through dark rituals), which manifests as thousands of venomous snakes that tightly bind, poison, and immobilize targets.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Nagapasha is used as an elite crowd-control snare during the Act 8 boss battle against Indrajit:

### Combat Attributes
*   **Astra Casting Cost:** 100% Sorcery Gauge (locks Indrajit in a 3-second chanting phase).
*   **Debuff Duration:** 60 seconds (or until purged by summoning *Garudastra*).
*   **Damage Profile:** 5 Poison Damage/second directly to player's health, ignoring all armor defenses.
*   **Immobilization Rate:** 100% (The player is locked in place; all attack skills and movements are disabled except *Spiritual Summon* commands).

### The Escape Mechanism
To survive the Nagapasha, the player must:
1.  *Hold Stance:* Use defensive breath controls to reduce poison damage by 50%.
2.  *Channel Garuda:* Direct the spiritual Prana meter to summon the divine eagle **Garuda**, who instantly devours the serpents, clearing the debuff.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Invocation & Launch
*   **Chanting Sigil:** A glowing, dark-green circular Sanskrit sigil appears under Indrajit's feet during invocation.
*   **Launch Trail:** A glowing green arrow splits into thousands of writhing, emerald-green light-trails that home in on the player.

### Constriction State
*   **Serpent Coils:** The player's character model is visibly wrapped in glowing green, semi-translucent ethereal cobras that slide and hiss, constricting when the player moves.
*   **Poison Vapor:** A faint green mist rises from the player's body, indicating the active poison status.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** High-frequency, rapid shaker instruments (mimicking rattlesnakes), tense, discordant violin screeches, and a low, whispering Asuric chant (*"Naga-Vasa, Naga-Vasa"*).
*   **Melodic Key:** F# minor (discordant, suffocating, tense).
*   **Integration:** The music instantly drops all triumphant brass, replacing it with a claustrophobic, silent track dominated by snake rattles and whispering flutes.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A low, slithering sound mixed with thousands of whispering voices and a green magic crackle.
*   **Launch/Binds SFX:** A whip-like snap (*"whip-SHH"*), followed by the sounds of tightening chains and hissings (*"HISS-sss"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A tight, constricting squeeze sound (*"CRUNCH-hiss"*) indicating venom injection.
