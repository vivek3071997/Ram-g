# Vayavyastra (The Celestial Wind Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ranged Elemental Astra (AoE / Wind / Utility)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama
*   **Mythological Origin:** Associated with Vayu, the Lord of Wind and father of Hanuman, this weapon summons the absolute power of gale winds, cyclonic storms, and tornadoes, capable of dispersing dark clouds, gases, and lifting light enemies.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Vayavyastra is a tactical utility arrow used to disperse dark clouds (such as in Act 8 against Indrajit's invisible cloaking):

### Combat Attributes
*   **Spiritual Prana Cost:** 25% Aura Gauge.
*   **Invocation Speed:** 1.0 second.
*   **Splash Radius:** 10-meter circular cylinder of wind.
*   **Base Damage:** 50 physical damage + *Gale Blow* (instantly blows away dark mist, toxic gases, and throws minor Asura infantry into the air, keeping them in a juggled state for 3 seconds).
*   **Special Trait:** *Disperser:* Instantly cancels all cloud-based or concealment abilities of bosses, forcing them to reveal their positions.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Drawing & Charging
*   **Vortex String:** Wind rushes into the bow, creating small dust eddies around Rama's feet.
*   **Condensation Sphere:** A spinning white mini-tornado forms at the arrowhead.

### Release & Impact
*   **Storm Streak:** The arrow leaves a spiralling, translucent white wind tunnel trail in the sky.
*   **Tornado Columns:** On impact, a glowing white cyclonic column of wind (height: 8 meters) shoots up, spinning rapidly, drawing in autumn leaves and loose debris.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Whistling high flutes, rapid string sweeps, and a sweeping, gusty wind background wash.
*   **Melodic Key:** F major (sweeping, light, unstable).
*   **Integration:** String instruments execute fast, upward arpeggios that climb in pitch as the wind cyclone lifts enemies.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A rising whistling wind, mixed with the sound of leaves rustling rapidly.
*   **Release SFX:** A sharp, whipping whistle (*"FWHIIISH"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A roaring tornado roar (*"SHHH-WOOSH-rrrm"*) followed by the thuds of falling objects.
