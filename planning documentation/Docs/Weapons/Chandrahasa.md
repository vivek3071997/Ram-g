# Chandrahasa (The Moon-Blade of Ravana)

*   **Weapon Class:** Greatsword / Scimitar (Melee)
*   **Wielder:** Emperor Ravana
*   **Mythological Origin:** A curved, glowing divine sword gifted to Ravana by Lord Shiva, pleased by Ravana's intense penance (when Ravana cut off his own heads to offer them as sacrifice). The name means "the laughter of the moon," reflecting its bright, crescent shape.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Chandrahasa is a heavy, curved melee blade designed to dominate close-range combat during boss encounters:

### Base Combat Attributes
*   **Base Melee Damage:** 180 per sweep.
*   **Block Penetration:** 100% (Attempts to block this weapon physically will cause the player's posture to shatter instantly. Attacks must be parried with *Perfect Timing* or dodged).
*   **Swing Speed:** 0.6 swings/second (Heavy, deliberate, high-impact swings).

### Special Combat Attacks
*   **Crescent Void Wave:** Ravana swings the sword, launching a curved, horizontal crescent projectile of silver energy that travels across the arena, cutting through stone pillars.
*   **Lunar Bind:** Slamming the blade into the ground creates a radial cold wave that slows down the player's movement speed by 40% for 5 seconds.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Wielding
*   **Blade Glow:** The blade emits a constant, cold, silver-white glow, resembling the light of a crescent moon in a dark night.
*   **Shadow Trace:** As Ravana moves, the sword leaves a trailing trail of dark purple smoke and silver stardust.

### Combat Slashes
*   **Crescent Arc:** Normal attacks leave a highly defined, glowing crescent-moon arc in the air that lingers for 0.8 seconds.
*   **Impact Splash:** Impact on player blocks or environment stone creates a burst of dark obsidian shards and silver sparks.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Terrifying, low-pitch cello runs, rapid dark violins, and a dramatic Asuric chorus chanting Sanskrit stanzas (*"Shiva Tandava Stotra"*).
*   **Melodic Key:** G# minor (tense, aggressive, royal but corrupted).
*   **Integration:** Wielding this sword triggers the high-tempo final boss battle music, where low cello sweeps mirror the sword's crescent slashes.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A high-frequency, vibrating hum like cold metal on ice, mixed with a low, menacing whisper.
*   **Release/Swing SFX:** A heavy, rushing wind slice (*"WHOOSH-shhh"*), ending in a metallic bell-like echo (*"TING"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A heavy, bone-crushing clang (*"CLANGG-shiiin"*) representing the sheer weight of Shiva's blessing.
