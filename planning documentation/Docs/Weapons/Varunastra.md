# Varunastra (The Celestial Water Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ranged Elemental Astra (AoE / Water / Purification)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama
*   **Mythological Origin:** Associated with Varuna, the Lord of the Oceans, this weapon summons the absolute purifying, cooling force of the deep sea and rainstorms, neutralizing fire traps and cleansing demonic corruption.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Varunastra is a tactical arrow used to counter fire-based boss mechanics (such as Ravana's fire heads in Act 10):

### Combat Attributes
*   **Spiritual Prana Cost:** 25% Aura Gauge.
*   **Invocation Speed:** 1.0 second.
*   **Splash Radius:** 8-meter circular area.
*   **Base Damage:** 60 impact damage + *Purify* status (instantly extinguishes burning areas, melts fire shields, and slows demon movement by 30% due to mud).
*   **Special Trait:** *Sustained Rain:* Spawns a localized downpour for 5 seconds that heals active companions by 5 HP/second.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Drawing & Charging
*   **Liquid String:** The bow string turns into a flowing thread of glowing blue water.
*   **Water Vortex:** A swirling sphere of clear, glowing blue water condenses at the tip of the arrow.

### Release & Impact
*   **Torrential Spear:** The arrow flies as a spear of pure, high-velocity water, leaving a trail of blue splashing droplets.
*   **The Rain Dome:** On impact, it erupts into a circular dome of falling rain, complete with glowing blue water rings expanding outward on the ground.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Serene, flowing classical bamboo flute (*Bansuri*), gentle glass chimes, and a soft, chanting female choir.
*   **Melodic Key:** A minor (peaceful, flowing, purifying).
*   **Integration:** Invocations introduce a tranquil, refreshing woodwind melody that cools the intense combat percussion.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** The sound of a rushing river or mountain waterfall, mixed with light, sparkling chimes.
*   **Release SFX:** A high-pressure water jet hiss (*"SQUISH-splash"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A heavy splash (*"SHH-SPLASH"*) followed by the continuous, relaxing patter of rain.
