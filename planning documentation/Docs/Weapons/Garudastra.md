# Garudastra (The Celestial Eagle Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ranged Summon Astra (Purge / Anti-Serpent)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama
*   **Mythological Origin:** Associated with the divine eagle deity Garuda, the eternal enemy of all serpents. This weapon summons the spirit of Garuda, instantly neutralizing snake-based bindings, traps, and poison fields.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Garudastra is a specialized tactical purge weapon used to break serpent bindings (such as Indrajit's Nagapasha in Act 8):

### Combat Attributes
*   **Spiritual Prana Cost:** 50% Aura Gauge.
*   **Invocation Speed:** 1.5 seconds.
*   **Splash Radius:** 15-meter circular area centered on the wielder.
*   **Purge Rate:** 100% (Instantly removes all *Nagapasha* bindings, cures poison status effects on the team, and dissolves active serpent enemies).
*   **Summon Damage:** Spawns a flock of celestial eagles that dive-bomb nearby ground targets, dealing 120 physical damage per target.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Drawing & Charging
*   **Feather String:** Feathers of light fall from the bow string during drawing.
*   **Avian Shape:** A glowing, golden holographic bird head appears over the bow grip, with wings extending along the bow limbs.

### Release & Impact
*   **Eagle Projectile:** The arrow morphs into a soaring, golden-light eagle that flies rapidly, shrieking as it travels.
*   **Celestial Cleansing:** On impact, a massive golden crest of Garuda expands, with spectral wings sweeping the ground, vaporizing green poison pools and snakes in a burst of yellow light.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** High-pitched, piercing woodwinds, soaring symphonic violins, and a triumphant, celestial brass chorus.
*   **Melodic Key:** B major (majestic, liberating, soaring).
*   **Integration:** The activation triggers a sudden, dramatic upward shift in the soundtrack, shifting from a dark, whispering theme to an open, soaring avian melody.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** The beating of heavy wings, mixed with a rising wind whoosh and celestial chimes.
*   **Release SFX:** A piercing, high-frequency eagle shriek (*"SKREEE-eee"*) as the arrow launches.
*   **Impact SFX:** A resonant, glass-like shattering chime (*"CHIME-dingg"*) representing the clean purge of all poison.
