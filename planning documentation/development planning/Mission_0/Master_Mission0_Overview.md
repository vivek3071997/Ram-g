## 1. Mission Summary

Kid Hanuman begins in Sumeru's sacred orchards, learns core movement mechanics while chasing forest imps (Tyakshas) stealing sacred fruits. He then ascends Mount Sumeru via thermal wind puzzles, crosses the Troposphere cloud deck, reaches the upper stratosphere where he battles Airavat (Celestial Elephant Guardian) and Lord Indra (King of Devas). After receiving the Vajra strike, divine boons are bestowed but the Sage council places the **Curse of Forgetfulness** — locking all his powers until he meets Rama. The camera pans across mountains and rivers to Ayodhya, revealing baby Prince Rama — transitioning the player to Mission 1.

## 7. Risk Register

| # | Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|------|------------|--------|------------|
| R01 | Kid Hanuman tail physics causing clipping issues in tight spaces | High | Medium | Allocate extra RIG sprint (Week 2–3), physics LOD fallback plan |
| R02 | Airavat boss (colossal scale) causing performance drops below 60fps | High | High | Early GPU profiling at M0-Alpha; set LOD thresholds before texture pass |
| R03 | Vayu Wind system interacting incorrectly with character controller at zone boundaries | Medium | High | Dedicated wind physics integration test in Week 4 |
| R04 | Sanskrit voice acting difficult to cast with correct pronunciation | Medium | High | Engage cultural consultant early; prepare phonetic guide for VAs |
| R05 | Cloud platform timer (3 sec) feeling unfair at different frame rates | Medium | Medium | Frame-rate independent timer using DeltaTime from day one |
| R06 | Indra boss bullet-hell pattern too difficult for casual players on first play | Medium | Medium | Difficulty-scaled ring speed; extensive playtesting at M0-Beta |
| R07 | Transition cinematic (Hanuman → Rama) causing scene load hitch | Medium | High | Pre-load Ayodhya assets during Boon Sequence cinematic |
| R08 | Volumetric cloud layer causing VRAM overrun on target hardware | Medium | High | Budget cloud volume resolution early; cloud texture streaming |
| R09 | Localization (Tamil/Telugu) text overflow in UI elements | Low | Medium | Design UI with 40% text expansion buffer from start |
| R10 | Cultural inaccuracy flagged post-release causing reputational damage | Low | Very High | Cultural consultant sign-off gate before M0-Gold-2 |
