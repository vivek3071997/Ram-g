# Mithila (The Terracotta Capital)

*   **World Region:** Kingdom of Videha (Act 2)
*   **Aesthetic Direction:** Neo-Traditional Terracotta Brick & Polished Marble. Symmetrical brick temples, intricate geometric carvings, and crystalline optical observatories built above clean underground research vaults.
*   **Acoustic Theme:** **Raga Yaman Kalyan** (Dawn temple state - peaceful, highly devotional, clear, and resonant).

---

## 1. Architectural & Subterranean Layout

Mithila blends ancient terracotta clay structural construction with advanced optical crystal research. Symmetrical towers house giant prism systems used to harvest solar energy.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Architectural / Scientific Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Material Profile** | Sintered Terracotta Clay + White Marble | High thermal mass, naturally cooling the city interior. |
| **Observatory Arrays** | Sphatika Crystalline Heliostat Towers | High-precision mirrors that track the sun, focusing rays into labs. |
| **Sanctuary Vault** | Subterranean Basalt Pinaka Chamber | Located `30 meters` underground; high-tensile stone vaults. |
| **Static Winch Rig** | Ashtapada Mechanical Calibration Platform | Heavy concrete and steel structure housing the *Pinaka* bow winch. |
| **Puzzle Interfaces** | Light-Refraction Optical Gates | Light beams must be directed through crystal lenses to unlock vaults. |

---

## 2. Dynamic Traversal & Optical Puzzles

*   **Observatory Climbing Tracks:** Symmetrical terracotta bricks feature deep decorative grooves, providing climbing handholds for rapid vertical traversal.
*   **The Sphatika Light Puzzle:** To access the underground *Pinaka* Sanctuary, players must rotate three large, ground-mounted crystalline Sphatika prisms. Aligning the prisms focuses a beam of concentrated solar light onto a thermal expansion lock on the vault door, triggering the mechanical counterweights to lift.
*   **The Subterranean Vault:** Dark, cool basalt cavern with low ceiling heights, requiring players to track targets using acoustic footprints.

---

## 3. Visual & Audio Design

### Visual Particles
*   **Volumetric Solar Beams:** Thick, glowing gold shafts of concentrated sunlight cutting through the dark subterranean mist, illuminating floating dust particles.
*   **Terracotta Surface Normal Maps:** Highly detailed normal maps showing individual clay brick textures, micro-fissures, and geometric Vedic relief carvings.

### Sound SFX
*   **Temple Bells:** High-frequency, clear brass bell rings that reverberate across stone plazas; the low-frequency drone of temple chants.
*   **Prism Rotation:** The heavy, grinding crunch of massive stone gear assemblies rotating on gravel beds.
*   **Vault Door Open:** A colossal, low-frequency stone-scraping roar, followed by the high-pressure hiss of ancient hot air venting.
