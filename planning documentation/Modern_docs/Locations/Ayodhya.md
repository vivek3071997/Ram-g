# Ayodhya (The Solar Metropolis)

*   **World Region:** Royal Capital of Kosala (Act 1, 3, and 10)
*   **Aesthetic Direction:** Pristine Neo-Classical Solarpunk. Clean, polished white-marble towers, golden solar panel sheets integrated into roofs, and lush green terrace gardens.
*   **Acoustic Theme:** **Raga Yaman** (Daytime palace - tranquil, majestic, golden) and **Raga Darbari** (Royal Hall - deep, solemn, resonant).

---

## 1. Urban Layout & Biomechanical Defenses

Ayodhya is designed with absolute geometric symmetry, utilizing the ancient **Ashtapada** (8x8 chessboard grid) street layout to optimize solar exposure, wind circulation, and defensive logistics.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Engineering / Scientific Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Grid Layout** | 8 x 8 Symmetrical Ashtapada Blocks | Maximizes line-of-sight safety and simplifies transit routes. |
| **Transit System** | Ground-Induction Electric Tramways | Silent, clean-energy passenger transport across the capital grid. |
| **Defensive Walls** | 12m Elevated White Granite Fortifications | Heavy mass blocks physical breaches; sloped profiles scatter impacts. |
| **Shataghni Batteries**| Automated Projectile Launchers (Wall-Mounted) | "Hundred-killers"; mechanical, pressurized air launchers firing heavy kinetic iron darts. |
| **Water Utility** | Sarayu Gravity Aqueduct Towers | Siphons and filters clean river water using sand-charcoal beds. |
| **Energy Grid** | Gold-Anodized Photovoltaic Roof Arrays | Powers the city's water filtration and night lighting grids. |

---

## 2. Dynamic Traversal & Environmental Hazards

*   **The Ashtapada Highways:** Wide, paved marble lanes designed for smooth, high-speed [Ratha Buggy](file:///../Vehicles/Ratha_Tactical_Rover.md) maneuvering. Players can execute seamless drift turns around the city's circular fountains.
*   **Elevated Aqueduct Paths:** High-level stone structures that players can climb using parkour skills, providing a stealthy, high-altitude traversal route over city walls.
*   **The Shataghni Defense Grid:** During defensive acts, players must coordinate wall-mounted projectile batteries, aligning mechanical gears to aim and fire kinetic dart swarms into massive charging wild beasts.

---

## 3. Visual & Audio Design

### Visual Particles
*   **Golden Solar Glare:** High-contrast PBR lighting showing brilliant sunlight reflecting off gold-anodized roofs; soft green leaf shadows falling across pristine white marble squares.
*   **Aqueduct Mist:** Micro-droplets of water vapor condensing around aqueduct outlets, creating localized rainbow refractions.

### Sound SFX
*   **City Ambience:** The peaceful, quiet hum of electric ground-induction trams; the clean rush of flowing aqueduct water; distant melodious bamboo flutes.
*   **Shataghni Firing:** The heavy, pneumatic hiss and massive metallic *CLANG* as the pressurized launch chambers release the kinetic dart swarms.
*   **Footsteps on Marble:** Crisp, echoing sandaled footsteps vibrating across high-ceiling polished granite halls.
