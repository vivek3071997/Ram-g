# Agneyastra (The Thermobaric Combustion Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** Thermobaric Aerosol Combustion Arrow (Elemental Modification)
*   **Primary Wielder:** Lord Rama
*   **Ammunition Cost:** Consumes `15` units of thermal compound energy.

---

## 1. Technical & Chemical Specifications

The Agneyastra is a highly efficient tactical combustion projectile designed to clear cluster targets and destroy wooden defensive barricades through rapid thermodynamic expansion.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Chemical / Thermodynamic Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Arrow Tip Type** | Pressurized Thermobaric Fuel Capsule | Stores volatile liquid fuel that vaporizes instantly on structural impact. |
| **Aerosol Compound** | Ethylene Oxide + Powdered Aluminum | Delivers high combustion velocity and intense thermal radiance. |
| **Ignition System** | Kinetic Impact Piezo-Electric Sensor | Triggers immediate spark upon physical contact with surface. |
| **Thermal Output** | 1,200 °C (Peak Flame Temperature) | Incinerates organic armor and wooden structures in seconds. |
| **Explosion Radius** | 4.0 meters (Radial Sphere) | Displaces oxygen, creating a vacuum that draws nearby minor units inward. |
| **Burn Duration** | 6.0 seconds (Persistent Flame) | Inflicts continuous damage-over-time (DOT) to burning units. |
| **Blast Pressure** | 0.2 MPa (Megapascals) | Generates a physical shockwave that staggers nearby medium-weight enemies. |

---

## 2. Tactical Gameplay Utility

*   **Barricade Demolition:** Instantly incinerates wooden fortifications, doors, and vine screens in Dandakaranya.
*   **Lanka Firestorms:** In Lanka's high-tar environments (docks, wooden balconies), firing an Agneyastra triggers chain-reaction palace fires, spreading continuously across adjacent wooden structures.
*   **Asuric Deficit:** Explodes fire shields and deals double damage to units with wet mud coverings.

## 3. Visual & Particle Render (VFX)

### Arrow Trajectory
*   **Thermal Combustion Trail:** The arrow flies leaving a dynamic, turbulent trail of bright orange fire and black soot particles, accompanied by local air heat-shimmering.

### Impact Explosion
*   **Thermobaric Fireball:** A dense, volumetric orange fireball expands rapidly in a `4m` sphere, dissolving into rising black smoke clouds with realistic buoyancy.
*   **Secondary Embers:** High-velocity burning sparks bounce off rock surfaces, casting dynamic flickering point-lights.

---

## 4. Acoustic Profile & Sound Design

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Arrow Travel:** A loud, rushing fire roar (*"whoooooosh"*) as the projectile travels.
*   **Piezo Ignition:** A sharp, metallic click immediately preceding the explosion.
*   **Thermobaric Explosion:** A deep, thudding bass blast (*"BOOOMPH"*) rather than a sharp click, followed by the crackling roar of persistent flames.
