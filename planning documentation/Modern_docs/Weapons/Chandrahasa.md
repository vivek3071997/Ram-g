# Chandrahasa (The Dark-Alloy Obsidian Blade)

*   **Weapon Class:** Colossal High-Carbon Obsidian-Steel Broadsword (Melee)
*   **Primary Wielder:** Ravana (Warlord of Lanka)
*   **Biomechanical Wielding:** Swung using high-torque shoulder rotation and absolute core-mass leverage, generating up to `8,000 N` of forward kinetic sweep force.

---

## 1. Technical & Metallurgical Specifications

The Chandrahasa is a massive, high-contrast dark broadsword crafted from a specialized high-carbon steel alloy infused with volcanic obsidian minerals, creating a blade of diamond-like hardness and extreme mass.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Metallurgical / Mechanical Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blade Length** | 1.6 meters | Offers colossal reach and extreme kinetic sweep radius. |
| **Total Weight** | 8.5 kg | High physical mass generates devastating kinetic momentum. |
| **Alloy Composition** | High-Carbon Steel + Volcanic Obsidian Minerals | Achieves a hardness rating of `68 HRC` (harder than standard steel). |
| **Edge Geometry** | Molecular-Scale Vacuum-Treated Bevel | Cuts through standard steel plates and carbon composites like butter. |
| **Hilt Construction** | Solid Bronze Core with Textured Ray-Skin Wrap | Absorbs heavy impact vibration, preventing grip slippage during sweeps. |
| **Swing Inertia** | High ($I = 2.17 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m}^2$) | Devastating damage, but leaves the wielder highly vulnerable to quick counters if parried. |

---

## 2. Combat Mechanics & Balance Constraints

Because of its colossal mass, the Chandrahasa operates under strict laws of physical inertia:
*   **Kinetic Cleave:** Hits ignore standard player parry boxes unless the player utilizes a specialized composite shield or the high-strength *Kodanda* riser.
*   **Anger-Induced Overclock:** When Ravana's rage is active, his swings generate high-pressure shockwaves of air (*Wind Shears*), knocking back players within `3m`.
*   **Lactic Recovery Cost:** If a player successfully dodges a heavy swing, the sword's immense momentum carries Ravana forward. It takes `1.5 seconds` for him to reverse the blade's vector, leaving his side torso fully exposed.

## 3. Visual & Particle Render (VFX)

### Blade Texture & Lighting
*   **Matte Obsidian Material:** The blade body uses a highly detailed, light-absorbing PBR material with high roughness, while the vacuum edge is highly polished, reflecting high-contrast gold and crimson highlights.
*   **Air Distortion Shimmer:** During rapid swings, a clear atmospheric refraction pattern (air-pressure wave) trails the edge, representing extreme kinetic velocity.

### Impact Effects
*   **Heavy Metal Sparks:** Clashing with metal blocks triggers bright, high-velocity orange sparks that bounce realistically off ground surfaces conforming to gravity.
*   **Structural Ruptures:** Ground impacts shatter stone tiles, displacing small debris particles in a radial vector.
