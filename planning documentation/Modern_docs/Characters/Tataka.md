## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Feral Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Corrosive Submandibular Acid Spray:** Tataka possesses specialized glands in her lower jaw that synthesize a highly acidic organic sulfur compound. By compressing her throat muscles, she sprays this chemical in a `60-degree` forward arc (`6m` range). The acid melts high-strength carbon-composite clothing, degrades armor durability, and creates toxic environmental pools that damage players.
*   **Dermal Calcium Bone-Plating:** Possesses hyper-dense calcium carbonate and keratin bone plates along her forearms, chest, and skull. These thick dermal plates act as natural armor, deflecting low-velocity sporting arrows and reducing incoming physical damage by `60%`. Players must target exposed joints to inflict critical hits.
*   **High-Inertia Feral Charge:** Tataka launches her `350 kg` body forward, reaching a speed of `35 km/h` in under `2 seconds`. If she impacts a structural column or wall, she generates up to `12,000 N` of kinetic force, collapsing wooden scaffolding. However, if she misses, her high momentum carries her into obstacles, causing a `3-second` self-stun due to brain deceleration.

### Primal Biology
*   **Natural Weaponry:** Relies on massive claws, heavy stone throws, and acid spray to defend her jungle boundaries.
*   **Flesh Textures:** hard, calloused gray skin mapping thick veins, scar tissue, and coarse biological mud layers.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
A beautiful princess cursed by Sage Agastya into a towering, feral forest predator, Tataka resides in the dense, dark woods along the southern trade borders. Driven by intense biological hunger and primal rage, she has completely destroyed the regional agricultural balance, turning a fertile valley into a desolate, toxic jungle. As Rama's first major trial, defeating Tataka requires the solar prince to overcome his hesitation to engage a female opponent and deploy highly calculated, precision archery to penetrate her dense bone-plating.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Act 1 (Border Patrol):** Block trade lanes, destroy regional water filtration setups, and terrorize local agricultural borders.
*   **Act 2 (The Trial):** Lure Rama and Lakshmana into her dark forest canopy, launch high-velocity boulder throws and acid sprays, and engage in the final feral clearing duel.

## 5. Visual Render & Particle Systems (VFX)

### Material & Shader Settings
*   **Keratin Dermal Plates:** PBR material shader with high roughness, low reflectivity, and complex normal maps to simulate cracked, ancient bone armor.
*   **Corrosive Acid VFX:** Bright yellow-green volumetric fluid particles with realistic viscosity, dynamic surface tension, and boiling surface bubble meshes.

### Dynamic Spiritual Aura (Flow State Particles)
*   **Feral Saliva Spills:** Constant dripping of micro-acid droplets from her jaw, causing tiny puffs of steam when they hit the damp soil, emphasizing real chemical reactions.
*   **Soil & Bark Splinters:** Her charging sweeps displace massive clouds of damp forest soil, wood chips, and leaf splinters, conforming to realistic physical friction vectors. No magical neon particles are used.
