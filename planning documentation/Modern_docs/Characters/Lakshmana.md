## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Kinetic Momentum Redirection (Parkour Jump Boost):** Lakshmana is a master of spatial mechanics and momentum conservation. By converting forward sprinting kinetic energy into elastic energy in his Achilles tendons and quadriceps, he can perform rapid, horizontal wall-runs and `3.5m` leaps across forest branches without requiring supernatural powers.
*   **Adrenaline-Gated Pain Suppression:** When severely wounded or under heavy suppression, Lakshmana triggers a massive sympathetic nervous system override. This blocks physical pain receptors (nociceptive signals) for `12 seconds`, maintaining maximum sprint speeds and preventing aim wobble. However, when the surge ends, he suffers a severe lactic acid crash (stamina recovery speed reduced by `50%` for `10 seconds`).
*   **Dual-Wielding Defensive Deflection:** Utilizing his high-frequency coordination (`160ms` latency), Lakshmana can track incoming projectile paths (such as Asuric throwing spears or arrows) and perform rapid mechanical deflections using dual high-carbon steel blades.

### Composite Archery & Close-Quarters Gear

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
The twin brother of Shatrughna and younger half-brother of Rama, Lakshmana is the absolute embodiment of fierce fraternal loyalty and active guardianship. Refusing to remain behind in Ayodhya, he willingly abandons his royal comforts to serve as Rama and Sita's primary defender in the wild forest zones of Dandakaranya. Bound by mortal limits, his combat acts are marked by raw human grit, scars, physical fatigue, and defensive rage, serving as the fiery tactical blade to Rama's cool, calculated solar focus.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 2 (Youth):** Safeguard local agricultural boundaries with Sage Vishwamitra, clear wild beast lanes, and defend the perimeter of the Mithila research facility.
*   **Acts 3 - 5 (Exile):** Construct the secure timber cottage at Panchavati, set up active physical tripwires (*Lakshmana Rekha* boundary markers), and defend Sita from early Asuric scouts.
*   **Acts 6 - 7 (Search):** Traverse the high cliffs of Kishkindha using mechanical climbing hooks, help forge the Sugriva alliance, and coordinate the assault on Vali's stronghold.
*   **Acts 8 - 10 (Siege):** Command ground combat units on the Lanka bridge, survive a near-fatal spear puncture from Indrajit through emergency medical triage, and neutralize Indrajit's optical stealth tactics.
