## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Avian Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **High-Velocity Wing-Lash (Aerodynamic Shockwave):** By locking his shoulder joints and flapping his massive `15m` wingspan at precise downward angles, Jatayu generates concentrated aerodynamic force vectors of up to `3,500 N`. This creates localized high-pressure wind tunnels that can knock over heavy ground targets or destabilize the private glider jet of Ravana.
*   **Thermal Updraft Navigation (Thermal Gliding):** Jatayu uses his hyper-acute sensory feathers to map thermal air columns (updrafts). By gliding within these columns, he generates passive lift that allows him to remain aloft at sub-orbital heights for hours with near-zero calorie consumption, tracking long-range coordinates.
*   **Serrated Talon Compression:** Biomechanically locks his massive talons using a tendon-latching mechanism that requires zero active muscle tension. This generates a crushing force of up to `4,500 N`, capable of crushing thick composite metal frames or tearing through heavy leather armor.

### Natural Flight Mechanics
*   **Natural Arsenal:** Relies on his powerful beak, massive claws, and dynamic aerodynamic wind pressure to engage aerial targets.
*   **Physical Protection:** Sleek, dense, waterproof golden-brown feathers that act as a natural PBR thermal barrier and distribute impact forces evenly across his skeletal chest grid.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
An ancient king of vultures and eagles, Jatayu is a noble biological beast who was a close companion of King Dasharatha. Residing in the primeval forests of Dandakaranya, he acts as a regional aerial guardian. When he witnesses Ravana abducting Princess Sita in his high-speed private solar jet, Jatayu does not hesitate. Despite his fragile, hollow-boned biological structure, he engages the hyper-armed Ravana in a legendary high-altitude dogfight, sacrificing his life to slow down the tyrant and alert Rama.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 4 (Guardian):** Survey regional forest boundaries from cloud heights, alert wilderness settlements of Asuric scout incursions, and maintain air superiority.
*   **Act 5 (The Dogfight):** Intercept Ravana's high-speed *Pushpaka Vimana* glider jet in the stormy stratosphere, tear through the visual light-refractive chameleon camouflage skin, and force the jet to drop altitude.
*   **Act 6 (Tragic Relay):** Survive severe structural wing injuries long enough to transmit Ravana's flight path coordinates and Lanka landing vectors to Rama, ensuring the mission continues.
