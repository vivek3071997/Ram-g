## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Somatic Volume Scaling (Mahima & Anima):** Hanuman controls his cellular size and interstitial tissue expansion. By hyper-dilating his skeletal-muscle cells and expanding his lung capacity through deep yogic breathing, he can scale his physical height from `2.1m` to `15m`. This physical mass expansion scales his physical kinetic punches by up to `100x`, enabling him to shatter heavy stone walls or lift massive timber obstacles.
*   **Aerodynamic Gravity-Assisted Leap:** Hanuman does not fly; he leaps. By compressing his massive leg muscles like high-tension coils and releasing up to `50,000 N` of kinetic force, he launches himself at precise aerodynamic vectors. During descent, he expands his chest volume and utilizes a broad woven sash to catch natural thermal updrafts (similar to a wingsuit), gliding hundreds of meters.
*   **Resonance Vocal Shockwave (Vajra Roar):** By hyper-compressing air within his massive lung capacity and forcing it through his vocal cords, Hanuman releases a focused, high-decibel acoustic sound wave (`130 dB` at `15m` range). This shatters local glass and stone structures, and temporarily disrupts the equilibrium of Asuric guards.

### Heavy-Duty Tactical Gear
*   **Combat Gear:** Wields no bladed weapons, relying instead on a heavy, solid bronze mace (*Kaumodaki Gada*) to crush armored barricades, or using his bare fists.
*   **Equipped Costume:** [Maruti Kevlar Sash](file:///../Clothing/Materials_Textures.md). A flexible, highly durable, and elastic Kevlar-blend chest wrap and dynamic loin-sash that stretches seamlessly during his somatic scale changes.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
Born to Anjana and the wind deity Vayu, Hanuman is a celestial being walking in a physical biological form of supreme power. Gifted with incredible strength, he is also a highly educated master of Sanskrit grammar, diplomacy, and science. His tragic childhood power-seal is lifted upon his faithful encounter with Lord Rama. Combining unmatched physical mass and brawling capability with absolute humility and deep strategic intellect, Hanuman serves as the ultimate tactical link between Rama's forces and Lanka's volcanic island.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Mission 0 (Prologue):** Discover his immense physical leaps as kid Hanuman, navigate the thermal drafts of the stratosphere to reach solar atmospheric heights, and manage his power-sealing crash.
*   **Acts 6 - 7 (Alliance):** Serve as Sugriva's chief diplomat, track Rama's physical coordinates, resolve the high-altitude conflict between Rama and Vali, and coordinate the forest army.
*   **Act 8 (Leap of Faith):** Execute the epic long-distance aerodynamic leap across the Southern Ocean, bypass Lanka's atmospheric jet streams, and infiltrate Ashoka Vatika undetected.
*   **Act 9 (Infiltration & War):** Deliver Sita's active beacon key, allow himself to be captured to survey Lanka's geothermal infrastructure, set Lanka's outer wooden/tar docks ablaze, and lead the heavy breach vanguard.
