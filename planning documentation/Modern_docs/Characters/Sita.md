## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Cognitive Fear Dampening (Satyagraha):** Sita possesses an exceptional ability to self-regulate her autonomic nervous system. Under extreme psychological pressure (such as Ravana's threats in Ashoka Vatika), her brain suppresses amygdala hijack, reducing her heart rate to a resting `60 BPM` and granting complete immunity to hallucinogens, Asuric psychological debuffs, and environmental panic effects.
*   **Light-Refracting Camouflage:** By remaining completely motionless and controlling her respiratory cycles, Sita aligns the micro-weave of her specialized silk garment to refract ambient sunlight. This reduces her optical signature by `85%`, allowing her to hide in plain sight from patrolling guards in Ashoka Vatika.
*   **Bio-Electromagnetic Shielding (Purity Aura):** Through intense meditative concentration, Sita projects a localized bio-electromagnetic field (`0.8m` radius). This field repels low-velocity organic hazards, such as swarms of toxic insects (*Asuric Locusts*), swamp flies, and airborne fungal spores in Dandakaranya.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
The princess of Mithila and daughter of King Janaka, Sita is a divine avatar born of the Earth, walking in a mortal form of supreme dignity. Far from a passive captive, her role in the epic represents ultimate psychological resilience, moral steel, and tactical signaling. Choosing to accompany Rama into exile, she survives dense forests, a terrifying aerial abduction, and months of solitary confinement under Asuric prison guards in Lanka's volcanic ash fields, never breaking her mental composure.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 2 (Youth):** Master botanical science and optical crystal arrays in Mithila, and select her life partner through the trials of the subterranean vault.
*   **Acts 3 - 5 (Exile):** Manage off-grid shelter logistics in Panchavati, collect rare botanical flora, and secure the perimeter using sensory boundaries, leading to her tactical capture.
*   **Acts 6 - 8 (Captivity):** Survive Ashoka Vatika by using stealth and optical camouflage, gather intelligence on Lanka's volcanic guard patrol routes, and send signal relays (e.g. dropping jewelry pieces) to Rama.
*   **Acts 9 - 10 (Restoration):** Undergo the fiery thermal test (*Agni Pariksha*) to prove her biological purity, and return to Ayodhya to re-establish civil order.
