*   **Asset Category:** Dynamic Global Environment Systems

## 1. Solar & Lunar Optical Systems

Ambient lighting dynamics are governed by real-world atmospheric refraction and solar angles, directly affecting AI stealth detection algorithms.

### A. Dynamic Solar Cycles (Daylight Timelines)
*   **Sun Angle & Intensity:** Solar lighting cycles from warm dawn (`5,500 K`) to bright midday (`6,500 K`) and golden twilight (`3,200 K`).
*   **Direct Sunlight Exposure:** Sunlight acts as a natural visual sensor in-game. Standing in un-shaded solar rays exposes shapeshifting Asuric scouts, preventing them from utilizing optical camouflage.

### B. Lunar Light Pathing & The Crimson Moon
*   **Moonlight Paths:** At night, silver moonlight reflections on water surfaces act as high-visibility lanes. Stepping out of these lanes into deep shadows increases the player's camouflage rating by `+40%`.
*   **Lanka's Crimson Moon LUT:** Under Lanka's volcanic ash clouds, the moon appears red due to atmospheric Rayleigh scattering by fine ash particles. The red light shifts camera grading to high contrast, increasing Asuric aggressive AI states.

## 2. Aerodynamic Wind Systems (Vayu Force)

### A. High-Velocity Canyon Updrafts
*   **Physics System:** Thermal wind vectors driven by temperature differences between canyon floors and high peaks.
*   **Glider Mechanics:** Flying the [Kishkindha Glider](file:///../Vehicles/Kishkindha_Exo_Gliders.md) within these wind draft columns generates upward lift force vectors: $F_y = \frac{1}{2} \rho v^2 C_L A$, allowing players to climb vertically.

### B. Cyclonic Dispersal (Vayavyastra)
*   **Decompression Blast:** Firing a pneumatic compression arrow (*Vayavyastra*) creates a massive radial wind outward sweep (`800 N` force), instantly dispersing toxic swamp fumes and Asuric locust clouds within `5m`.

## 3. Thermodynamics & Fire Systems (Agni Force)

### A. Agni Altar Shield Zones
*   **Physics System:** Concentrated upward thermal currents generated by sacred firewood altars. The extreme upward heat creates a convective barrier that repels low-velocity organic vectors (locusts, toxic spores, swamp flies).

### B. Magma-Obsidian Phase Changes
*   **Volcanic Flow Cooling:** Active geothermal lava flows deal extreme thermal damage (`200 HP/s`).
*   **Cryo-Freeze Reaction:** Firing a sub-zero cryo-capsule arrow (*Varunastra*) at active magma triggers rapid phase cooling, converting the liquid rock into solid, walkable obsidian stone blocks in under `1.0 second`.
