*   **Vehicle Class:** Sub-Orbital VTOL Solar-Powered Jet-Cruiser (Luxury / Command Class)

## 2. Propulsion & Flight Mechanics

*   **VTOL Hovering Phase:** Dual central ducted fans driven by high-torque electric motors draw air from the upper hull vents and expel it downward, generating up to `150,000 N` of vertical lift force. This allows the jet to hover silently over Ashoka Vatika or the Dandakaranya forest.
*   **Supersonic Jet Phase:** Once vertical height is established, the rear hydrogen combustion scramjet engines ignite, vectoring thrust backward. The wing profiles adapt their sweep angle to minimize wave drag during supersonic transitions.
*   **Active Chameleon Camouflage:** Millions of micro-prisms on the carbon-fiber skin record ambient light from surrounding skies and project it on the opposite side of the fuselage. This masks the jet from ground visual and radar scanning.

## 3. High-Altitude Interception (The Dogfight)

In Act 5, during the abduction scene in the stormy stratosphere:
1.  **Optical Cloak Breach:** [Jatayu](file:///../Characters/Jatayu.md) uses his high-velocity talons and wing-lash wind shears (`3,500 N`) to physically tear through the outer carbon chameleon skin, disabling the active camouflage and exposing the gold hull.
2.  **Ducted Fan Disruption:** Jatayu dives directly into the port-side central ducted fan vent, sacrificing himself but clogging the intake. This forces the jet into a severe thrust imbalance, forcing Ravana to drop altitude and land the damaged cruiser in Lanka's northernTar docks.

## 4. Visual & Audio Design

### Visual Particles
*   **Volumetric Jet Heat-Haze:** Supersonic rear exhaust jets generate long, clear atmospheric heat-haze distortion waves that warp background light.
*   **Chameleon Shimmer:** Disabling the camouflage triggers a dynamic digital glitch shimmer, showing a pixelated transition from cloudy blue patterns back to the underlying gold carbon-fiber texture.

### Sound SFX
*   **Hovering Hum:** A deep, low-frequency hum (*"vrrrrrr-hummmm"*) from the ducted fans, resembling a high-end luxury vehicle.
*   **Scramjet Ignition:** An explosive, crackling roar (*"SCHROOOOOOM"*) that transitions into a high-pitched supersonic whistle as the jet accelerates.
