# Ram-G: Master Acts Index & GDD Timeline

This document serves as the master story-timeline and level directory for **Ram-G**. It breaks down the epic story of the **Ramayana** into 10 highly detailed playable game acts.

Each act links directly to its own deep-dive Game Design Document (GDD) under the `Docs/Acts/` subfolder, detailing the narrative flow, moral theme, active characters, locations, boss battles, costumes, dialogue, and 3D build asset requirements.

## Core Game Structure Overview

Throughout these 10 acts, the game cycles between three core gameplay loops:

1.  **Hub & Exploration (Ayodhya, Chitrakoot, Kishkindha):** The player interacts with sages and allies, upgrades weapons, accepts quests, and navigates beautiful platforming zones based on historical geography.
2.  **Dharmic Decision Making (Dialogue Intersections):** Non-combat dialogue sections where the player must choose options reflecting *Dharma*, *Satya*, and *Vinaya* (humility) to navigate palace intrigue, handle arguments, or recruit companions.
3.  **Active Combat & Astra Invocation:** Strategic real-time action combat combining physical bowmanship and swordplay with the channeled invocation of celestial *Astras* (invoked via mantras, operating under elemental balance rules).
