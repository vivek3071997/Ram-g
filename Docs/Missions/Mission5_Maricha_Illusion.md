# Mission 5: The Swarna-Mriga Chase

*   **Playable Protagonist:** Maricha (Playable Golden Deer / Demon Form)
*   **Reveals & Transitions to:** Jatayu (Playable Celestial Vulture King State)
*   **Theme & Focus:** Illusion and deception, high-speed parkour evasion, and the release of death through divine contact.

---

## 1. Scene & Environment

### Setting: The Enchanted Sun-Dappled Canopy & The Briar Valley
*   **Environment Elements:**
    *   *The Shimmering Canopy:* A beautiful, otherworldly forest clearing where the sun rays create glowing yellow trails. The grass is neon green, decorated with floating golden pollen spores and glowing crystal mushrooms.
    *   *The Briar Valley:* A tight, rocky gorge filled with dense thorny bushes and narrow stone arches, forcing the player to perform high-precision parkour moves.
*   **Atmospheric Mood:** Magical, dreamlike, fast-paced, and highly kinetic.
*   **Key Objects:** Sun prisms, illusory golden dust trails, Rama's tracking arrow marks, and the dark shadow portal of Ravana.

---

## 2. Basic Flow of Steps

1.  **Golden Sprint (Tutorial & Evasion):** Play as the Golden Deer (*Swarna-Mriga*). The player learns double-leaps, wall-runs on stone cliffs, and high-speed golden dashes while fleeing predatory forest panthers (*Vyaghras*).
2.  **The Decoy Prism (Puzzle):** Rama begins his hunt. The player must use sun prisms and forest dust to split into five identical shimmering deer decoys, tricking Rama into chasing a shadow.
3.  **The Briar Run:** Escape through a narrow gorge while dodging Rama’s long-range tracking arrows that freeze sections of the ground upon impact.
4.  **Boss Encounter (Prince Rama - The Silent Hunter):** A high-tension evasion boss battle. Rama chases the player on foot, climbing trees and shooting homing solar arrows. The player must use speed echoes and decoy maneuvers to survive for 3 minutes without getting caught.
5.  **The Transition Nerf (Lethal Snipe Strike):** Rama fires his ultimate homing weapon, the *Surya-Astra* (Solar Arrow). The arrow pierces Maricha's chest in a slow-motion cinematic.
6.  **Demonic Regression & The Cry:** Maricha loses his golden deer form, reverting to his massive, ugly demon body (stripping all speed and defense).
7.  **The Voice Mimicry:** In his final breath, Maricha uses his unique vocal abilities to mimic Prince Rama's voice perfectly, shouting: *"Ah Sita! Ah Lakshmana! Help me!"*
8.  **Reveal:** The camera follows the acoustic shockwave of his cry as it travels across the forest, reaching the highest cliff of the Dandaka range. The majestic, colossal vulture king Jatayu hears the scream, spreads his massive wings, and launches into the stormy sky. Control shifts to Jatayu.

---

## 3. Fun Monster Hunting

### Outrunning the Vyaghras (Enchanted Panthers)
*   **Mechanic:** Wild, corrupted forest panthers are hunting in the brush, leaping out to ambush the player from behind tree trunks.
*   **Gameplay Flow:** Maricha cannot fight. The player must use the **Swarna-Spur (Golden Dash)** to slide under falling tree logs and execute wall-repels off rock walls. Landing a dash at the exact moment a panther strikes triggers a **Speed-Echo Dodge**, leaving a golden illusion that stuns the beast.
*   **Enemy Type:** *Vyaghras* — High-speed, shadow-skinned panthers that pounce from high branches, vulnerable only to perfect evasion timings.

---

## 4. Fun Puzzle Solving

### The Decoy Split (Maya Chakra)
*   **Context:** Rama has tracked the player to a dead-end stone arena. The player must create clones to confuse his tracking sight.
*   **Puzzle Mechanics:**
    *   The arena contains five ancient sun-capturing crystal prisms standing on stone columns.
    *   The player must dash through the glowing pollen patches in the arena to charge their **Maya Bar (Illusion Energy)**.
    *   Once charged, the player stands in the center of the prisms and activates **Maya Chakra**. This refracts the deer's golden aura through the crystals, generating four perfect, running decoy clones that scatter into different forest exits, breaking Rama's targeting lock.

---

## 5. Boss Encounters

### Boss: Prince Rama (The Silent Hunter)
*   **Combat Arena:** A dense, circular redwood forest with vertical climbing structures.
*   **Boss Mechanics:**
    *   *The Pursuit:* Rama does not have a standard boss arena; he actively chases the player across branches and cliffs. He uses his bow, *Sharanga*, to launch high-speed seeking shots.
    *   *The Trap Fields:* Rama fires freezing arrows that create circle-zones of ice on the forest floor, slowing the player's movement by 80% if stepped on.
    *   *The Final Surge:* At 1 minute remaining, Rama begins using the trees to execute colossal jumps, landing ahead of the player to block exits. The player must use quick direction-swapping maneuvers to evade his line of sight.

---

## 6. The Character Nerf & Transition

### The Lethal Arrow & Demonic Regression
*   **The Strike:** Seeing the deer evade every standard snare, Rama draws his sacred *Surya-Astra*, breathing a mantra of truth. The arrow travels with light speed, homing in on Maricha's magical core.
*   **The Physical Nerf (Regression & Demise):**
    *   The arrow strikes Maricha's chest, shattering his illusion.
    *   *Demonic Regression:* The golden hide dissolves, the hooves turn into massive clawed feet, and Maricha reverts to his true, heavy, 30-foot-tall Asura form.
    *   *The Stats Strip:* All movement speed drops to 10%, health drops to 1%, and all parkour abilities are locked. Maricha falls to the forest floor, bleeding.
*   **The Voice-Cloned Cry:** Understanding that his death by Rama's hand guarantees his spiritual salvation, but still bound to Ravana's scheme, Maricha summons his remaining breath. He replicates Rama's exact vocal signature, sending a sonic scream echoing across the valley: *"Haa Sita! Haa Lakshmana!"*
*   **The Swapped Character (Jatayu):** The camera follows the physical sound waves rippling through the forest canopy, rushing upwards towards the mountaintop. Jatayu is revealed, his golden-feathered wings catching the wind. The player takes control of Jatayu as he takes flight to investigate.

## 8. Weapons & Equipment Used

*   **Swarna-Spur (Golden Dash):** High-speed sprint maneuver that allows Maricha to slide under low gaps and create illusory trails.
*   **Maya-Spundan (Illusion Pulse):** Emits a burst of golden pollen that blinds pursuing predators and charges active sun prisms.
*   **Vocal Copy (Ritual Skill):** A unique, narrative-locked skill used at the end of the mission to mimic other characters' voices and trigger transitions.
