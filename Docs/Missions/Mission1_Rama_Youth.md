# Mission 1: Vishwamitra's Forest Guard

*   **Playable Protagonist:** Prince Rama (Youth State)
*   **Reveals & Transitions to:** Princess Sita (Mithila Royal Gardens)
*   **Theme & Focus:** Dharmic duty, weapon initiation, and the discipline of disarming.

---

## 1. Scene & Environment

*   **Primary Active Scene:** [Scene: Siddhashrama Altar](../Scenes/Scene_1_Siddhashrama_Altar.md) (`SCENE_SIDDHASHRAMA_ALTAR`)
*   **Key Environment Elements:** The sacred, tranquil forest clearing of Siddhashrama bordered by ancient Sal trees, contrasting with the dark canyon valleys of Tataka Vana.
*   **Key Interactive Objects:** [Object: Vishwamitra's Sacred Altar](../Objects/Object_Vishwamitra_Sacred_Altar.md) (`OBJ_VISHWAMITRA_SACRED_ALTAR`), rotatable sun-mirrors, and Asuric bone barricades.

---

## 2. Basic Flow of Steps

1.  **Ashram Guard (Defense):** Guard Sage Vishwamitra's fire altar. The player learns basic archery, precision aiming, and close-quarters sword combat.
2.  **Monster Hunt (Sacrifice Defilers):** Fight off waves of shadow demons trying to extinguish the sacred yajna flames.
3.  **Vedic Mirror Puzzle:** Align three giant brass mirrors on stone pillars to focus sunlight on the yajna altar, purifying the zone.
4.  **March into Tataka Vana:** Navigate through the bone canyon, dodging falling rock traps and tracking Tataka's giant footprints.
5.  **Boss Battle 1 (Subahu):** Defeat the flying demon archer Subahu in a high-speed sniping duel.
6.  **Boss Battle 2 (Tataka):** Slay the colossal demoness Tataka in her forest clearing.
7.  **The Transition Nerf:** Enter Mithila's scholarly gates, surrendering all physical weapons and celestial arrows at the armory.
8.  **Reveal:** Transition control to Princess Sita as she walks through the beautiful flower gardens of Mithila.

---

## 3. Fun Monster Hunting

### Purging the Defilers
*   **Mechanic:** Demon hordes are swooping down to drop toxic blood and flesh onto the sacred fire, extinguishing the fire-barriers.
*   **Combat Flow:** Rama must use his Forest Bow to shoot these flying gargoyle-demons (*Kecharas*) before they reach the altar area. If they land, Lakshmana fights them in melee while Rama uses fire-infused arrows to ignite them.
*   **Enemy Type:** *Kecharas* — Winged, red-eyed bat-demons carrying toxic flesh pots. Highly agile in mid-air.

---

## 4. Fun Puzzle Solving

### The Vedic Sun-Mirror Alignment
*   **Mechanic:** The Asuric fog has blocked the sun, weakening the sacred altar's barriers. Rama must restore sunlight using three ancient Vedic brass mirrors.
*   **Puzzle Elements:**
    *   Rotate three stone pillars using physical leverage points to align the brass mirrors.
    *   One mirror is covered in thick demon vines; Rama must shoot fire arrows at dry brush nearby to ignite the vines without melting the mirror.
    *   Once all three mirrors are aligned, they focus a beam of concentrated solar energy onto the altar, burning away the Asuric fog and creating a permanent checkpoint.

---

## 5. Boss Encounters

### Boss 1: Subahu (The Shadow Aviator)
*   **Combat Arena:** A rocky gorge with high stone pillars.
*   **Mechanics:** Subahu flies between pillars, launching shadow daggers that shroud the screen in blackness. Rama must use his *Shabda-Bhedi Vidya* focus mode to listen to the wind whistle of Subahu's wings, pinpointing his location in the dark and shooting him down.

### Boss 2: Tataka (The Forest Monarch)
*   **Combat Arena:** A massive bone-strewn clearing inside Tataka Vana.
*   **Phase 1 (The Plague Swarm):** Tataka surrounds herself with a shield of black locusts. The player must shoot fire-mirrors to create columns of flames, driving the locusts away before striking her chest weak spot.
*   **Phase 2 (The Colossal Stamp):** Tataka rips up giant trees, hurling them at Rama. The player must use grapple arrows to scale the trees mid-flight, leaping onto Tataka's stone shoulders to strike her crown.

---

## 6. The Character Nerf & Transition

### The Courtly Disarmament
*   **The Journey:** Sage Vishwamitra takes Rama and Lakshmana to Mithila to attend the royal assembly of King Janaka, where the celestial bow Pinaka is kept.
*   **The Gatehouse Lock (Nerf):** Upon arriving at Mithila's glorious, ivory-white gates, the royal guards politely bow and invoke the ancient law of Videha:
    > *"In Videha, the city of scholars, philosophers, and holy ascetics, no weapons of war may cross the threshold. All bows, blades, and iron-pointed quivers must be surrendered to the gatehouse keepers."*
*   **The Loss of Arsenal:** Vishwamitra nods, and a reluctant Rama and Lakshmana surrender their Forest Bow, twin blades, and celestial astras. Rama's combat HUD changes: all weapon slots are locked, and his spiritual gauge is set to zero. He is completely unarmed.
*   **Character Swap:** Rama steps through the gates in simple white scholar robes. The camera pans past him, entering a royal garden filled with gold-leaf trees, blue lotuses, and white jasmines. A young princess (Sita) is seen tending to a wounded forest fawn, and control transitions directly to her.

## 7. Dialogue & Narrative Scripts

### Dialogue Segment: The Forest Altars
*   **Location:** Siddhashrama inner sanctuary.
*   **Characters:** Sage Vishwamitra, Prince Rama, Lakshmana.

```markdown
Prince Rama: (Nocking an arrow, looking at the dark sky) "Maharishi, the forest grows silent. The birds have fled, and the wind carries the smell of burning copper."

Sage Vishwamitra: "Steady, Rama. The demons Tataka and Subahu seek to destroy our penance. They fear the light of the altar. Keep the sacred flame burning, for it is the anchor of the forest."

Prince Lakshmana: (Drawing his daggers) "Let them come! My blades are thirsty, and my arms are strong. They will not touch a single blade of holy grass!"

Sage Vishwamitra: "Remember, Rama—your strength lies not in the bow, but in the truth of your purpose. Fire the arrow of Dharma, and the shadows will dissolve."
```

## 8. Weapons & Equipment Used

*   **The Forest Bow (Valkala Arch):** Rama's primary physical weapon in Act 1. Moderate draw speed, high precision.
*   **Iron-Point Quiver:** Standard ammunition, offering 30 arrows.
*   **Agneyastra (Single Charge):** Unlocked after defeating Subahu. Launches an explosive fire-infused arrow, useful for burning Tataka's locust shield.
