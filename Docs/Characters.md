# Ram-G: Master Character Index

This document serves as the master GDD index for all characters in the **Ram-G** game, categorized into four distinct structural design tiers. Each character has a dedicated, individual profile under the `Docs/Characters/` folder outlining their specs, gameplay archetype, visual/costuming states, and specialized Audio-Visual (A/V) properties.

---

## Tier 1: Divine (Deva / Celestial / Immortal)
*These characters are celestial beings, immortal spirits, or divine creatures possessing vast supernatural capabilities, shape-shifting abilities, and cosmic elements.*

1.  **[Hanuman (Anjaneya)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Hanuman.md)**
    *   *Role:* Playable Scout / Companion Brawler
    *   *Summary:* Immortal Vanara of supreme strength, shape-shifting, and absolute wind flight capabilities.
2.  **[Jatayu (Vulture King)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Jatayu.md)**
    *   *Role:* Aerial Playable Hero (Act 5) / Tragic Companion
    *   *Summary:* Noble bird king who intercepts Ravana's chariot in a dramatic mid-air clash.
3.  **[Sampati (Eagle Elder)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Sampati.md)**
    *   *Role:* Aerial Reconnaissance Ally NPC
    *   *Summary:* Jatayu's elder brother, whose solar-burned wings are restored. He locates Lanka using supreme sight.
4.  **[Garuda (Divine Eagle)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Garuda.md)**
    *   *Role:* Ethereal Summon / Status Purger
    *   *Summary:* The mount of Vishnu. Devours Indrajit's binding serpents (*Nagapasha*) to restore the player.
5.  **[Samudra Deva (Ocean God)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Samudra_Deva.md)**
    *   *Role:* Environmental Director NPC
    *   *Summary:* Deity of the Southern Sea. Guides Rama on bridge engineering and controls marine forces.

## Tier 2: Divine Human / Avatar (Incarnations of Deities)
*These characters are human-born physical incarnations of supreme cosmic deities, sent to restore universal balance (Dharma). They possess dormant divine power that manifests as exceptional skills.*

6.  **[Lord Rama (Rāmachandra)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Lord_Rama.md)**
    *   *Role:* Principal Playable Protagonist
    *   *Summary:* 7th avatar of Lord Vishnu, Crown Prince of Ayodhya, and master of all divine astras.
7.  **[Sita (Janaki)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Sita.md)**
    *   *Role:* Narrative Protagonist / Active Support Companion
    *   *Summary:* Incarnation of Goddess Lakshmi, daughter of Earth. Invokes sanctuary wards and earth structures.
8.  **[Lakshmana (Saumitri)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Lakshmana.md)**
    *   *Role:* Secondary Playable Companion / Dual-Wielder
    *   *Summary:* Avatar of Adi-Shesha. High-speed combatant wielder of twin blades, immune to sleep status.
9.  **[Prince Bharata (Regent Hero)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Prince_Bharata.md)**
    *   *Role:* Ayodhya Regent Ally / Base Manager
    *   *Summary:* Avatar of Vishnu's Sudarshana Chakra. Manages Ayodhya's upgrades and guards the home front.
10. **[Prince Shatrughna (General Ally)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Prince_Shatrughna.md)**
    *   *Role:* Ayodhya Garrison Commander / Quest Giver
    *   *Summary:* Avatar of Vishnu's Panchajanya (Conch). Commands defensive units and sweeps palace infiltrators.
11. **[Sage Parashurama (Bhargava)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Sage_Parashurama.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 2 Combat Boss / Legendary Axeman
    *   *Summary:* 6th avatar of Vishnu. A fierce warrior sage who tests Rama's avatar state in a high-speed axe duel.

## Tier 3: Strong Mortal (Kings, Sages, Rakshasas, and Vanaras)
*These characters are mortal or physical beings (including giant Rakshasas and Vanaras) possessing extreme physical strength, mastery of weapons/sorcery, or immense spiritual power gained through penances.*

12. **[Ravana (Lankesh)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Ravana.md)**
    *   *Role:* Main Antagonist / Ultimate Final Boss
    *   *Summary:* Emperor of Lanka, possessor of ten heads, and wielder of Shiva's *Chandrahasa* sword.
13. **[King Vali (Tyrant of Kishkindha)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/King_Vali.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 6 Combat Boss
    *   *Summary:* Mighty Vanara King whose celestial aura drains 50% of the attack power of anyone facing him directly.
14. **[King Sugriva (Vanara Exile)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/King_Sugriva.md)**
    *   *Role:* Active Combat Companion (Act 6)
    *   *Summary:* Exiled brother of Vali. Allies with Rama, providing the vast Vanara army scout units.
15. **[King Dasharatha (Solar Monarch)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/King_Dasharatha.md)**
    *   *Role:* Ayodhya Patriarch NPC
    *   *Summary:* Ruler of Ayodhya, veteran warrior of celestial battles, bound by absolute oaths.
16. **[Sage Vishwamitra (The Mentor)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Sage_Vishwamitra.md)**
    *   *Role:* Tutorial Guide / Weapon Vendor NPC
    *   *Summary:* Royal sage of supreme spiritual strength. Unlocks Rama's active Astra abilities.
17. **[King Janaka (Philosopher King)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/King_Janaka.md)**
    *   *Role:* Mithila Quest Director NPC
    *   *Summary:* Sita's father, keeper of Shiva's heavy bow *Pinaka*. Manages high-tier puzzle arenas.
18. **[General Indrajit (Meghanada)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/General_Indrajit.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 8/9 Sorcery Boss
    *   *Summary:* Ravana's eldest son, conqueror of Indra, master of invisibility and black magic.
19. **[Titan Kumbhakarna (The Sleeper)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Titan_Kumbhakarna.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 9 Colossal Raid Boss
    *   *Summary:* Sixty-meter giant brother of Ravana, possessing mountain-crushing physical stamps.
20. **[Shurpanakha (Demoness of Seduction)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Shurpanakha.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 4 Narrative Miniboss
    *   *Summary:* Ravana's shape-shifting sister. Her mutilation triggers the Dandaka war.
21. **[Maricha (The Illusionist)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Maricha.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 5 Speed Chase Boss
    *   *Summary:* Demonic shape-shifter who assumes the form of the Golden Deer to bait Rama.
22. **[Tataka (Forest Matriarch)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Tataka.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 1 Arena Boss
    *   *Summary:* Cursed Yakshini with the strength of a thousand elephants, terrorizing Dandaka forest.

## Tier 4: Weak Mortal (Palace Staff, Devotees, and Common Humans)
*These characters possess little to no physical combat capability. However, they play critical, high-impact GDD roles as narrative drivers, quest givers, traders, or emotional anchors.*

23. **[Queen Kaikeyi (Palace Plotter)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Queen_Kaikeyi.md)**
    *   *Role:* Act 3 Palace Intrigue Dialogue Boss
    *   *Summary:* Rama's stepmother, whose demands trigger the exile questline.
24. **[Manthara (The Whisperer)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Manthara.md)**
    *   *Role:* Dialogue Antagonist NPC
    *   *Summary:* Humpbacked maid whose toxic dialogue choices poison Kaikeyi's mind.
25. **[Guha (Boatman Chief)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Guha.md)**
    *   *Role:* Ferryman / River Pilot NPC
    *   *Summary:* Devoted chief of boatmen. Steers the player raft during the Ganges crossing hazards in Act 3.
26. **[Sabari (Elder Hermit)](file:///c:/Ram-G%20The%20game%20based%20on%20bharat%20mythical%20story%20ramayana/Docs/Characters/Sabari.md)**
    *   *Role:* Devotional Merchant / Healer NPC
    *   *Summary:* An elderly ascetic woman who feeds Rama tasted wild berries. Serves as the ultimate quest checkpoint healer.
