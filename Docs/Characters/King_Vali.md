## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Power Siphon (Indra's Boon):** A unique, highly tactical boss mechanic. Vali absorbs 50% of the physical strength, attack speed, and spirit energy of any opponent who directly faces him in a frontal arc. This forces the player (Rama) to utilize stealth and positional cover behind trees to inflict damage, mirroring the legendary lore.
*   **Indra's Golden Necklace:** A passive defense shield. Vali is 100% immune to frontal projectile strikes, reflecting all regular arrows. He can only be damaged by surprise flanking strikes or celestial piercing astras.
*   **Kishkindha Ground Slam:** Vali leaps 10 meters into the air and slams down with both fists, creating an expanding shockwave that shatters blocks and knocks down players within a 15-meter radius.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Primal Fists (Vajra-Mushti):** His primary weapon. Heavy hand-to-hand combat strikes that deal crushing damage, breaking through player blocking shields.
*   **Golden Gada:** A heavy iron-reinforced mace used for wide-sweeping clearing strikes.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Vali is the elder son of the rain god Indra and the king of Kishkindha. Blessed by Indra with a magical golden necklace that grants him supreme strength and absorbs half the power of any opponent who faces him, Vali was virtually invincible. He single-handedly defeated and bound Ravana, keeping him pinned under his armpit for months. Due to a tragic misunderstanding during a fight with the demon Mayavi inside a cave, Vali believed his brother Sugriva had betrayed him to usurp the throne. He exiled Sugriva, took Sugriva's wife Ruma, and ruled Kishkindha with an iron hand. Sugriva fled to Mount Rishyamukha and eventually forged an alliance with Lord Rama, who shot Vali from behind a tree during their duel.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 7 (The Duel of Brothers):** Act as the final boss of Kishkindha. The player must support Sugriva in a direct duel against Vali, managing Sugriva's health as Vali continuously drains his strength.
*   **Act 7 (The Flanking Strike QTE):** The player, controlling Lord Rama, must execute a high-stealth sniping sequence from behind the Saptachhada trees, matching Vali's movements to shoot the fatal arrow.
*   **Act 7 (Final Reconciliation):** Triggers a deeply emotional death cinematic where Vali realizes his error, passes his magical necklace and crown to Sugriva, and attains peace in Rama's presence.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Wild, aggressive, and primal tribal theme featuring rapid jungle log drums (Dhol), raw bamboo flutes, roaring shell trumpets, and deep male chorus grunts.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** High-intensity, crushing tempo, set in Raga Bhairav (destructive aspect), written in a driving B minor scale.
*   **Integration:** The boss duel features frantic, high-tempo percussion loops that accelerate as Vali's siphon gauge fills up; his death scene transitions to a solemn classical string piece.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rock-crushing monkey thuds accompanied by the jingling of golden bells on his divine necklace; heavy breathing and deep, resonant chest beats.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Intimidating monkey screeches and low throat growls; booming, mocking laughter when he absorbs the player's power.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Fist strikes sound like massive rocks slamming against wet clay; Gada swings create heavy, low-frequency wind shears.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Swirling ribbons of glowing golden light coiling from the player's character toward Vali, representing the active power-drain mechanic.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Cracks form in the dirt under his steps; the leaves of nearby trees rustle violently when he roars.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Blinding golden expansion dome coiling out from Indra's necklace; glowing red Sanskrit runes of "Siphon" floating near Vali's fists.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Impact of his blows creates heavy golden-yellow shockwaves; target attacks create tiny blue energy shields on Vali's chest, indicating damage absorption.
