## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Wing Hurricane:** Jatayu beats his giant wings, generating a localized 120-degree sandstorm gale. Knocks back enemies, deflects incoming arrows, and blinds targets for 4 seconds.
*   **Talons of Retribution:** High-damage swoop strikes that ignore 30% of target armor, leaving a bleeding status effect that drains health over time.
*   **Sky-Sight Recon:** A passive utility. Provides the player with full, unmapped area fog clearing while Jatayu flies overhead in scouting sequences.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Vajra-Talon (Celestial Claws):** His primary natural weapon. Indestructible talons blessed by his father Aruna (the sun chariot driver). Capable of breaking physical iron shields.
*   **Aruna's Gaze (Solar Pierce):** Charges solar energy into his beak, launching a concentrated laser-like heat pierce that melts demon magic barriers.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Jatayu is the younger son of Aruna (charioteer of the Sun God Surya) and nephew of the epic demi-god bird Garuda. He was a close friend of King Dasharatha. During their exile, Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana encountered Jatayu in the Panchavati forest, where he swore to protect Sita. When Ravana abducted Sita and fled in his sky chariot (Pushpaka Vimana), Jatayu heard her cries and engaged Ravana in a legendary aerial battle. Despite his extreme age, he shattered Ravana's chariot, bow, and slew his celestial mules before Ravana sliced his wings using the divine sword Chandrahasa. He survived long enough to guide Rama towards the south.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 3 (Forest Alliance):** Anchor the forest boundaries, providing the player with a safe sky-recon guide and establishing the border ward.
*   **Act 5 (The Sky Duel - Interactive Event):** Intercept Ravana in mid-air. The player briefly controls Jatayu in a thrilling dogfight, tearing down Ravana's external carriage shields and bows.
*   **Act 5 (Sacrificial Demise):** Fall to the ground after being struck by Chandrahasa, hold onto his life-force, transmit the key narrative memory to Rama, and attain Moksha through Rama's funeral rites.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Soaring and tragic brass-led epic arrangement featuring traditional horn sections (Kahala, Shankha), heavy woodwinds, and driving war kettledrums.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** High-energy, desperate tempo, written in G minor (expressing struggle and ultimate sacrifice) transitioning into a peaceful, major-key bamboo flute lament upon his passing.
*   **Integration:** Aerial boss phase features heavy brass and frantic temple percussion; his final dialogue sequence drops into a solo ambient Bansuri theme.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Heavy, metallic scraping of sharp talons on mountain stone; massive wing-flaps creating low-frequency air pockets.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Piercing, echo-layered celestial eagle shrieks; deep, raspy, heroic breathing under injury; a warm, resonant voice during his final meeting with Rama.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Wing beats generate powerful wind-rushing whooshes; talon strikes produce a sharp, steel-on-steel slicing sound; breaking Ravana's chariot creates splintering wood explosions.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A radiating heat-shimmer aura (representing Solar fire) that dances off his feathers when flying near the sun.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Strong wind currents swirl leaves and grass radially outward whenever he hovers, leaving trailing golden feathers that fade into light.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Bright, blinding sunburst flashes of golden-orange energy from his wings, accompanied by trail rings of heat distortion.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of golden-yellow feathers and stellar sparks when striking, and bright crimson slashes reflecting physical damage.
