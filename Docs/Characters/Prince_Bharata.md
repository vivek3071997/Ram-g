## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Sudarshana Aura:** As the incarnation of the Sudarshana Chakra (Vishnu's cosmic discus), Bharata emits a rotating blue solar aura. This aura reduces the damage taken by nearby allies by 30% and inflicts passive solar-burn damage on any demon that enters its radius.
*   **Paduka Blessing (The Sacred Sandals):** Placing Rama's wooden sandals (*Padukas*) on the altar creates a localized massive defense buff. It fully restores the spiritual energy of all allies in the sector and makes them immune to negative status effects.
*   **Ascetic Fortitude:** Bharata's passive focus gauge charges 50% faster, allowing him to launch divine counter-parries in quick succession.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Chakra-Shield:** A glowing golden circular energy shield that can be thrown at enemies, bouncing between up to 3 targets before returning to him, shredding their armor.
*   **Bharata Bow:** A heavy iron bow used to fire dense light arrows that detonate on impact, creating a localized gravity pull that clusters enemies together.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Bharata is the second son of King Dasharatha, born to Queen Kaikeyi, and the half-brother of Rama. He is the earthly incarnation of Lord Vishnu's Sudarshana Chakra. While away at his maternal kingdom, his mother Kaikeyi manipulated Dasharatha into exiling Rama and crowning Bharata. Upon returning and discovering the tragedy, a devastated Bharata refused the crown, severely rebuked his mother, and set out to the forest to bring Rama back. When Rama insisted on fulfilling his father's exile vow, Bharata took Rama's wooden sandals, placed them on the throne of Ayodhya, and ruled as a regent from a small hut in Nandigram, living as an ascetic for 14 years.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 3 (Forest Search):** Track Rama to Chitrakoot forest with a grand procession, refusing royal honors, and presenting the sandals to Rama in a highly emotional cinematic.
*   **Acts 4 - 9 (Nandigram Exile):** Guard the borders of Ayodhya from his hermitage, keeping the capital secure from demon raids while maintaining constant vigil.
*   **Act 10 (Royal Return):** Welcome Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana back to Ayodhya, hand over the kingdom in pristine condition, and assist in the coronation ceremony.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Solemn, deeply emotional, and devotional theme featuring solo Sitar, mournful Veena strings, soft bamboo flutes, and a gentle Sanskrit choir singing chants of brotherhood.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Slow, sorrowful tempo, written in Raga Desh (evoking longing, patriotism, and deep devotion), set in D minor.
*   **Integration:** Nandigram and forest sequences play a quiet Sitar and flute arrangement; his combat sequences introduce driving but controlled temple hand-drums and sweeping strings.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Gentle sandals clicking on stone; heavy rustle of coarse ascetic bark cloth.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Resolute, calm breaths; deeply emotional vocal calls of "Raghunandan" (Rama); focused, sharp war grunts during combat.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Throwing the Chakra shield generates a high-frequency, ringing metallic hum; arrow fire sounds like heavy iron rods slicing through wind.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Spinning circular rings of brilliant blue light (resembling the Sudarshana Chakra) coiling around his feet and shoulders when entering combat.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Sandalwood leaves and white flowers gently drift downwards around his altar; the soil beneath Rama's sandals glows with golden dust motes.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding circles of blue solar flames coiling outwards; rotating blue energy rings protecting allies within his aura.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of blue and gold circular sparks when the Chakra-shield hits, leaving briefly glowing star shapes.
