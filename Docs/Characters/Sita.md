## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Earth Attunement (Bhumija Shield):** As the daughter of Mother Earth, Sita can invoke localized soil structures. While in exile, she can summon protective root barriers to screen Rama and Lakshmana during boss fights.
*   **Dharmic Sanctum (Safe Zone):** Sita can cast an active spiritual ward. Minor demons (Asuras) cannot cross this boundary. (Serves as the basis for the *Lakshmana Rekha* barrier).
*   **Signet Beacon:** Players can track Sita's abduction path by locating glowing drops of her jewelry (e.g., her anklet, sash, hair gem) dropped in the forest during Act 5.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Floral Mala (Spiritual Grace):** Sita does not engage in physical weapon combat. Instead, she infuses the companion characters (Rama, Lakshmana) with spiritual energy, boosting their health recovery and mana regeneration.
*   **The Fire Vow (Agni Shuddhi):** Her ultimate capability, showing absolute purity. Purges all curses and status effects from companions, reflecting her high spiritual power.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Sita was found in a furrow of a ploughed field by King Janaka of Mithila, who raised her as his beloved daughter. She is an incarnation of Goddess Lakshmi. After Rama broke the Pinaka bow, they were wed in Mithila. When Rama was sentenced to exile, Sita voluntarily rejected royal comforts to accompany him. Later kidnapped by Ravana, she was kept captive in Lanka's Ashoka Vatika, where she withstood immense torment, temptation, and threats, remaining fiercely loyal and serving as the focal point of the great war.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 2 (Mithila):** Align the ancient energies of the earth, proving Rama's capability to lift the Pinaka bow.
*   **Acts 3 - 5 (Forest):** Keep the exile camp stable, support the sages, and survive the initial kidnap attempt by Ravana.
*   **Acts 6 - 9 (Lanka Captivity):** Maintain absolute mental and spiritual resistance in Ashoka Vatika, rejecting Ravana's advances and giving Hanuman her *Chudamani* (hair gem) as a token of hope for Rama.
*   **Act 10 (Return):** Prove her purity through the fire ordeal (*Agni Pariksha*), return to Ayodhya, and take her place as the Queen of Ramarajya.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Ethereal and serene classical ensemble featuring solo bamboo flute (Bansuri), delicate wooden harp (Santoor), and resonance-rich Esraj (bowed string instrument) over soft, atmospheric drone.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Slow, meditative tempo in Raga Yaman (invoking peace, devotion, and pure light) with gentle, swelling female vocal chants.
*   **Integration:** Plays during peaceful camp interactions and in Ashoka Vatika; during boss fights, the theme transforms into a protective, soaring spiritual choral arrangement when her active ward is cast.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Incredibly soft, delicate grass rustling sounds, layered with the faint, crystal-like chiming of silver toe-rings and anklets.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Gentle, calm breathing; soft, resolute exhales under duress; pure, clear Sanskrit incantations when executing spiritual grace abilities.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Whispering wind and forest leaf rustles when casting root shields; a soft, glass-chime chime sound upon active healing ticks; a warm, resonant hum when purifying allies.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A soft, radiant golden-green earth halo that emanates outwards from her stance in slow, circular ripples.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Glowing footprints where wild forest flowers and fresh green grass instantly sprout and bloom upon her step, fading slowly after she passes.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Rising columns of golden light entwined with emerald-green leafy vines and glowing lotus blossoms expanding outwards to form a protective dome.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Burst of shimmering golden sparkles and floating white lotus petals that dissipate into a warm, protective mist around her allies.
