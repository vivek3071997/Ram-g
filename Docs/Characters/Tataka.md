## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Plague Swarm (Insect Shield):** Tataka summons a swirling vortex of black, toxic locusts around her. The swarm acts as a passive shield, absorbing 50% of incoming physical damage and inflicting poison-damage ticks on the player if they stand inside her melee range.
*   **Forest Shatter (Tree Throw):** Rips up a massive forest tree and hurls it at the player, breaking blocks and creating a long debris trail that slows movement speed.
*   **Tamas Stone Rain:** Rains sharp, fiery rocks from the sky onto targeted spots on the ground, forcing the player to continuously dodge and move.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Bone Mace:** A massive stone-capped bone mace used to execute heavy ground slams, creating radial shockwaves.
*   **Toxic Spit:** Shoots high-velocity acid blobs that erode weapon durability and poison targets.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Tataka was originally a beautiful celestial Yakshini, daughter of the Yaksha king Suketu, who married Sunda. Blessed by Brahma with the strength of a thousand elephants, her husband Sunda was killed by Sage Agastya for desecrating his hermitage. Seeking revenge, Tataka and her son Maricha attacked Agastya, who cursed them both to transform into grotesque, blood-drinking Rakshasas. Tataka ravaged the fertile provinces of Malada and Karusha, turning them into a haunted desert forest (Tataka Vana). When young Rama and Lakshmana arrived with Sage Vishwamitra to defend the forest sacrifices, Rama overcame his moral hesitation about fighting a female and defeated her with a single arrow.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 1 (The Haunted Forest Campaign):** Guide the player through the dark, haunted Tataka Vana, dodging poison traps and fighting minor demon mobs.
*   **Act 1 (The Boss Duel):** Fight Tataka in the forest clearing. The player must use dodge-roll mechanics, destroy her insect shield using fire arrows, and parry her bone club strikes.
*   **Act 1 (The Final Sniping QTE):** The player, controlling Rama, must complete a precision arrow-draw sequence to strike her heart, clearing the forest of her corruption.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Terrifying, heavy tribal theme featuring deep wooden logs, booming drums (Dhol), dry shakers, screeching low brass horns, and aggressive male chanting.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Slow, heavy, crushing tempo, set in Raga Bhairav (destructive Rudra-Tandava style), written in a dark D minor scale.
*   **Integration:** The boss duel features deep, rolling percussion loops that shake the audio field; her sweeping attacks trigger explosive brass crescendos that amplify the threat level.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Ground-shaking heavy thuds accompanied by the jingling of bone necklaces; insect buzzing and throat growls.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Grotesque, high-pitched shrieks; deep, metallic throat-growls; wet, raspy breathing under injury.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Stone club swings produce heavy, whistling wind shears; tree throws create wooden splintering explosions.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A thick, coiling swarm of black insects and dark green poison vapors rising from her massive shoulders and limbs.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Plants wither and die instantly where she walks; wind-torn dust devils swirl around her feet.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding circles of dark purple smoke and green poison trails; glowing red slash marks floating on targets.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of dark copper ash and green poison spots upon impact, leaving sizzling trails in the air.
