## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Drishti-Yana (Infinite Vision):** Sampati has the boon of infinite sight. From Mount Mahendra, he can zoom in on Lanka (100 yojanas away), revealing enemy defenses, Ashoka Vatika, and Ravana's citadel. This acts as the player's strategic tactical map reveal before the Lanka sea-crossing.
*   **Solar Fortitude:** Due to surviving direct solar radiation, Sampati is 100% immune to fire, heat, and sun-based damage.
*   **Feather Regeneration (Divine Blessing):** Once Sampati provides key info about Sita to the Vanaras, a spiritual event cures his burns. He sprouts pristine golden wings, unleashing a shockwave of healing feathers that buff nearby allies.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Ash-Beak Strike:** In his wingless form, his beak is his primary weapon. It deals piercing damage that shatters demon armor.
*   **Suryastra Blessing:** After his wings are restored, he can harness solar rays to cast a wide-area blinding flash, incapacitating all enemies on the Southern Shore.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Sampati is the elder son of Aruna (the Sun's charioteer) and the brother of Jatayu. In their youth, the brothers flew high in the sky to see who could reach the sun. Jatayu flew too close, and to save him from burning, Sampati flew above his brother, shielding Jatayu with his wings. His wings were completely burned off, and he fell onto Mount Mahendra, where he lived in isolation for thousands of years. Upon meeting the Vanara search party (led by Angada and Hanuman) lamenting Jatayu's death, Sampati recounted the tale, used his infinite sight to pinpoint Sita's location in Lanka, and was instantly healed of his burns, sprouting new wings.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 8 (Southern Shore Search):** Act as a narrative quest anchor. The player must find Sampati on Mount Mahendra and bring him news of Jatayu.
*   **Act 8 (Tactical Recon Scan):** Guide the player's camera in a bird's-eye scan across the vast southern ocean, mapping Lanka's guards, underwater sea-monsters, and coastal defenses.
*   **Act 8 (Redemption & Wing Rebirth):** Trigger the cinematic wing restoration, unlocking his flight, and acting as a tactical transport companion for the initial beachhead assault.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Slow, atmospheric, and resonant tribal theme featuring low-pitched wooden flutes, ancient Esraj strings, and dry shaker percussion, shifting into a grand, soaring string symphony as his wings regenerate.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Very slow, mysterious tempo in C minor, swelling into a glorious, uptempo D major choir upon rebirth.
*   **Integration:** Initial interaction is quiet and somber with minimal instrumentation; the wing rebirth cinematic utilizes a full-scale orchestral swell with high choral harmonies.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Heavy, slow, scraping claw slides on sand and rock, conveying his wingless handicap; heavy panting and creaking bones.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Low, ancient, gravelly bird screech; wise, deep baritone speech; triumphant, ringing high screech upon wing restoration.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Wing rebirth sounds like a rushing, crackling solar flare; sun-ray flashes create high-tension laser hums.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Charcoal ash particles and heat vapors rising from his wings (initially) \| Radiating brilliant yellow solar heatwaves (restored).
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Scorches the dry brush around his resting place; when his wings are restored, a wave of wind blows away all loose dust and rocks, revealing green moss underneath.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Massive, expanding rings of solar heat and golden light rays shooting out in 360 degrees as his wings burst forth; glowing golden runes encircling his eyes when scanning Lanka.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Bursts of white, glowing feathers and hot sparks that float gently downward, healing all allies who touch them.
