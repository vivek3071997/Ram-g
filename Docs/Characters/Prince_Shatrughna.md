## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Panchajanya Sound Blast:** As the incarnation of Vishnu's divine conch shell (*Panchajanya*), Shatrughna can blow his war conch to emit a massive sonic shockwave. Stuns all enemies in a 60-degree cone for 5 seconds and shatters physical enemy posture.
*   **Silent Stride:** Grants Shatrughna and nearby allies active camouflage in high grass. Their footstep sound is reduced by 100%, allowing stealth takedowns in demon camp outposts.
*   **Twin Bond Buff:** When fighting alongside Prince Bharata, both brothers gain a passive 25% attack speed and coordination speed buff.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The Conch Bow:** A beautifully decorated ivory-white recurve bow that shoots concentrated sound bolts instead of physical arrows, bypassing enemy armor.
*   **Shatrughna Blade:** A straight, double-edged celestial sword used for rapid melee counterattacks.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Shatrughna is the youngest son of King Dasharatha, born to Queen Sumitra, and the twin brother of Lakshmana. He is the earthly incarnation of Lord Vishnu's divine conch Panchajanya. He is deeply devoted to his brother Bharata, serving as his shadow and constant guardian, just as Lakshmana serves Rama. Shatrughna remains in Ayodhya during the 14-year exile, working with Bharata to protect the kingdom. In the post-exile era, he leads a massive campaign to defeat the demon tyrant Lavanasura, establishing peace in Madhupura (Mathura).

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 2 (Ayodhya Riots):** Maintain internal security during the palace crisis, confronting palace conspirators and protecting the royal vault.
*   **Acts 3 - 9 (Border Defense Campaign):** Lead Ayodhya's heavy cavalry and archer legions in defending the borders from invading Asura armies, executing tactical siege defenses.
*   **Act 10 (Slayer of Lavanasura):** Lead the final side-quest campaign to locate, confront, and vanquish the demon tyrant Lavanasura, earning his title of "Destroyer of Enemies."

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** High-tempo military march featuring grand conch shell trumpet ensembles, marching snare drums, sharp classical sitar runs, and aggressive string sections.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Fast, disciplined tempo, set in Raga Bilawal (traditional scale representing courage and military victory), written in G Major.
*   **Integration:** Border defense combat sequences feature heavy military marching drums and soaring conch soundscapes; his conch abilities override active sound effects with a deafening, resonant blast.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Rhythmic, crisp clicking of iron-soled boots and heavy metal chainmail clinking; sliding grass steps.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Sharp, military commands; short, intense combat shouts; clear, powerful breath draws before blowing the conch.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Firing the Conch Bow creates a high-pressure sonic "thump" followed by a whistling soundwave; sword strikes sound like whipping metal wires.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** Swirling ribbons of bright white and silver light energy circling his limbs, representing the conch's purity.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Footsteps kick up tiny spirals of white wind dust that spin briefly on the ground; grass blades bend outward from his conch blasts.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding sonic rings of white and silver light waves coiling outwards in a cone; glowing Sanskrit runes of "Vijay" (Victory) flashing on stunned targets.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Impact of sonic arrows creates small shockwave ripples of white energy, splitting into sonic fragments.
