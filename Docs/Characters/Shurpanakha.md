## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Illusion Veil (Shape-Shifting):** Shurpanakha can cast an active illusion veil to appear as a beautiful, graceful maiden (*Chandra-Mukhi*). She uses this form to bypass forest outpost guards and approach the player before transforming back into her demon form to strike.
*   **Toxic Claw Scratch:** Her long, brass-hard claws carry a corrosive venom. Striking targets inflicts a "Corroded" status effect that reduces armor values by 25% and drains health over time.
*   **Enraged Shriek:** When her health drops below 50%, she unleashes a piercing shriek that raises her movement and attack speeds by 40% while calling minor forest demons to support her.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Vajra-Nails (Claws):** Her primary weapon. Sharp, knife-like brass claws that deal slicing damage.
*   **Illusion Dagger:** A small concealed blade used to execute surprise stealth stabs in her human form.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Shurpanakha is the daughter of the sage Vishrava and the Rakshasa princess Kaikesi, making her the younger sister of Ravana, Kumbhakarna, and Vibhishana. While wandering in the Panchavati forest during her exile, she encountered Lord Rama and was instantly infatuated by his beauty. After being rejected by Rama, who pointed out he was married to Sita, she approached Lakshmana, who also declined. Enraged and humiliated, she attacked Sita in her true demonic form, prompting Lakshmana to draw his sword and cut off her nose and ears. Bleeding and screaming, she fled to her brother Khara, sparking a battle where Rama defeated 14,000 demons, and eventually went to Ravana, instigating the plot to kidnap Sita.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 3 (The Infiltration):** Track Shurpanakha in her beautiful maiden form through the Panchavati forest camp, detecting her demon trail using Rama's focus gaze.
*   **Act 3 (Palace Duel):** Engage Shurpanakha in the clearing. The player must parry her claw combos and dodge her enrage phases.
*   **Act 3 (The Mutilation Trigger):** Triggers the legendary quick-time combat event where Lakshmana slices her nose as she leaps at Sita, concluding the fight and initiating the war campaign.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Seductive but tense tribal hybrid featuring soft bamboo flutes, classical Santoor chimes, and deep wooden shakers, shifting into a chaotic, screaming string arrangement as she transforms.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Moderate, slow (human form) \| Frantic, high-velocity (demon form), written in a dark G# minor scale.
*   **Integration:** Inhuman form features quiet, peaceful Santoor loops; her demonic transformation triggers a screaming orchestral fanfare and fast-paced hand drums.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Inaudible, sliding steps (human form) \| Grotesque clicking claws and heavy sand shuffles (demon form).
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Seductive, whispering voices; sudden demonic shrieks; growly breathing when enraged.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Claws produce sharp metal-slicing swooshes; nose-slicing QTE generates a clean metallic slice sound followed by a desperate shriek.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A thin, swirling shroud of dark purple shadow mist coiling around her limbs when transitioning forms.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Wild grass and flowers wither instantly where her demonic feet step; forest dust devils swirl near her claws.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant expanding circles of dark purple smoke and green poison trails expanding outwards; glowing red slash marks floating on targets.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Exploding bursts of dark purple ash and gold sparks upon impact, leaving sizzling trails in the air.
