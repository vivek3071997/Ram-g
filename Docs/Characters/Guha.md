## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Ganges Ferry Navigation:** Guha is the core river navigator of Act 3. The player must coordinate with Guha to steer wooden rafts through the turbulent rapids of the Ganges, avoiding rock collisions, whirlpools, and river demon attacks.
*   **Nishada Recon:** While rafting or camping near his outposts, Guha reveals all nearby forest resource nodes (healing berries, wood, crafting herbs) on the player's mini-map.
*   **Ferry Blessing (Rest State):** Resting at Guha's river campfire fully restores player health, mana, and arrows, providing a clean checkpoint save.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **The River Paddle:** A heavy, iron-wood boat paddle used for sweeping melee attacks, knocking back enemies.
*   **Nishada Hunter Bow:** A simple bamboo bow used to fire fast, light arrows.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Guha is the king of the Nishadas (a tribe of hunters and fishermen), ruling from his capital Shrigaverapura on the banks of the Ganges. He was a close childhood friend and admirer of Lord Rama. When Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana arrived at the Ganges during their first day of exile, Guha welcomed them with deep devotion, offering royal food and soft beds, which Rama politely declined as an ascetic. Guha washed Rama's feet, prepared a secure ferry boat, and personally guided them across the wide, holy river, guarding their camp at night while talking to Lakshmana.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 3 (The River Crossing):** Act as the river quest facilitator. Wash Rama's feet in a highly emotional cinematic, demonstrating his deep humility and devotion.
*   **Act 3 (The Ganges Rafting Event):** Guide the player through an action-adventure rafting sequence, steering the ferry through river demon attacks and rapid currents.
*   **Act 3 (The Campfire Vigil):** Guard the camp at night. The player, controlling Lakshmana, engages in a long dialogue sequence with Guha, outlining their protective duties.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Upbeat, warm, and rhythmic local folk theme featuring bamboo flutes, acoustic acoustic guitars, hand drums (Tabla, Dholak), and splashing water soundscapes.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Moderate, joyful tempo set in Raga Bhupali (bright, pentatonic scale representing joy, warmth, and simple devotion), written in G Major.
*   **Integration:** Rafting sequences feature high-energy folk percussion loops; his outposts play peaceful acoustic flute melodies.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Sand-shuffling footsteps; splashing water steps; soft rustling of grass sashes.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Warm, energetic young baritone voice; friendly row-whistles; resolute grunts during heavy rowing.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** River paddle swings produce heavy, wet swooshing sounds; boat steering creates deep water-splashing gurgles.
