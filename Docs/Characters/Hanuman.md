## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Mahima & Anima (Size Shifting):** 
    *   *Anima (Micro):* Hanuman shrinks to the size of an insect, ideal for stealth sequences, bypassing security grids, or lock-picking (used to infiltrate Lanka).
    *   *Mahima (Macro):* Hanuman grows into a colossal giant, crushing minor forts, stepping over oceans, and lifting mountains (used in Act 7 and 9).
*   **Vayu Gati (Wind Sprint):** Can dash through the air, leap massive distances, or glide over clouds, powered by his father Vayu (Wind God).
*   **Vajra Kaya (Diamond Skin):** Passive armor that reduces all physical damage by 50% and grants immunity to fire damage (he burns Lanka while his tail is on fire).

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Golden Gada (Mace):** A massive heavy mace used for ground slams, creating shockwaves, and breaking enemy shields.
*   **Tail Whip:** Hanuman's tail acts as a semi-autonomous whip, sweeping minor enemies or grabbing distant ledges.
*   **Sanjeevani Lift (Ultimate Utility):** Hanuman lifts the entire Dronagiri mountain. He flies over the battlefield, dropping healing pollen that resurrects fallen allies and fully restores health.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Hanuman is the son of the wind god Vayu and the Vanara princess Anjana. Blessed by all the major Vedic deities with boons of invincibility, immortality, and shape-shifting, he forgot his powers due to a childhood curse until reminded by Jambavan. Meeting Rama during his search for Sita, Hanuman pledged his eternal devotion. He served as the ultimate scout and warrior, leaping the southern ocean to Lanka, locating Sita in Ashoka Vatika, single-handedly burning the city of Lanka, and carrying the mountain of healing herbs to save Lakshmana.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 6 (Kishkindha):** Meet Rama, prove his loyalty, help restore Sugriva to the throne, and gather the Vanara scouts at the Southern Shore.
*   **Act 7 (Sundara Kanda - Playable Act):** Leap across the ocean, infiltrate Lanka undetected in micro-form, locate Sita, deliver Rama's ring, confront Ravana in his court, and burn down the capital with his burning tail.
*   **Acts 8 - 9 (Siege & Battle):** Work as a heavy battering ram against Lanka's gates, carry Rama on his shoulders, fly to the Himalayas to bring the Sanjeevani hill to heal the army.
*   **Act 10 (Victory):** Deliver the news of victory to Sita, fly back to Ayodhya, and remain on Earth as the eternal guardian of Rama's name.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Monumental and thunderous orchestral arrangement featuring giant bronze temple horns, heavy war-drums (Dundubhi), and a soaring male Sanskrit choir singing high-register devotional hymns.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Slow-to-moderate, powerhouse tempo, written in Raga Shankara (heroic, commanding scale associated with Lord Shiva's power), set in C major.
*   **Integration:** Exploration in Kishkindha plays deep temple woodwinds; heavy companion combat switches to massive brass and war drums; and when Hanuman triggers his *Mahima* (colossal) shape-shift, the "Jai Bajrangbali" choir swells into a majestic, room-shaking vocal wall.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** In base form, heavy, padded palm-slaps and solid thuds; in giant form, deep, structural earth-shaking rumblings that shake the player's camera.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Extremely deep, resonant chest grunts; booming, earth-shaking war-cries; a soft, humble, devotional whisper of "Ram" under breath when performing restorative acts.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** The golden Gada produces booming wind shears upon swings, culminating in a resonant thunderclap upon impact; tail whips generate high-velocity crackling snaps.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A radiating, shimmering golden-orange aura of holy light that expands and contracts in lockstep with his deep breathing.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** In base form, leaves rustle and bend towards him; in giant form, his footsteps leave deep, cratered depressions in the earth, and trees sway violently under the displacement of his wind.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Blinding golden expansion fields accompanied by rising pillars of holy orange flame; size-shifting manifests an expanding glowing golden wireframe of his target size.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Heavy, impact-burst shockwaves of golden light that ripple across the ground, sending dust, rock particles, and small shock rings outward.
