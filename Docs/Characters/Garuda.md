## 2. Combat & Gameplay Properties

### Powers & Special Abilities
*   **Nagantaka Aura (Serpent Terror):** A passive zone of supreme dominance. Any serpent-based enemy (including Indrajit's Nagapasha summons or snake-demons) within a 50-meter radius is inflicted with the "Terrorized" status effect, reducing their attack and speed by 75% or forcing them to flee.
*   **Amrita Cleansing:** Actively cleanses all active poison, venom, sleep, paralysis, and curse status effects from the entire party. Breaks the "Nagapasha Bind" lock-state immediately during the Act 9 boss fight.
*   **Suparna Whirlwind:** Flaps wings to create a blinding emerald-green cyclone that sucks in minor enemies, spins them, and tosses them back, dealing moderate wind and light damage.

### Weapons & Divine Astras
*   **Vajra Bow-Wings:** Garuda's wings are indestructible and can be used as shields to block all incoming physical projectiles.
*   **Celestial Claw Strike:** Swoops down and grabs enemies with his divine talons, slamming them into the ground and shattering their physical defense shields.

## 3. Narrative & Objectives

### Biography
Garuda is the king of birds, a demi-god, and the sacred mount (Vahana) of Lord Vishnu. Born to the sage Kasyapa and Vinata, his power was so immense that even the gods could not defeat him when he retrieved the nectar of immortality (Amrita) to free his mother from servitude. He holds a cosmic rivalry with serpents (Nagas). During the war of Lanka, when the crown prince Indrajit bound Lord Rama and Lakshmana with the venomous Nagapasha (Serpent Noose), casting the entire Vanara army into despair, Garuda swooped down from the heavens. His mere presence scattered the cosmic serpents, healing the princes and restoring their strength.

### Core Objectives
*   **Act 9 (Nagapasha Crisis):** Act as the ultimate tactical rescue summon. When Rama and Lakshmana are pinned down under a venomous binding doom-clock, the player must summon Garuda to purge the spell.
*   **Act 9 (Airborne Escort):** Provide aerial cover during the Lanka citadel assault, intercepting flying Rakshasa armies and clearing sky barriers.
*   **War Campaign:** Provide high-tier passive buffs to arrow speed and poison resistance for Rama and the Vanara army.

## 4. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Soaring, triumphant, and regal classical theme featuring divine brass horns (Karnal), thunderous temple drums, and high-frequency Veena strums layered with triumphant Sanskrit chants.
*   **Melodic Tempo & Key:** Double-tempo, heroic key of G# Major (representing absolute victory, purity, and celestial light).
*   **Integration:** The summon event triggers an explosive brass fanfare that completely overrides the dark battle boss track, filling the audio space with glorious, uplifting orchestrations.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Footstep Impacts:** Sharp, metallic clicks of heavy celestial claws; massive, high-velocity wing flaps that sound like distant rolling thunder.
*   **Vocalizations & Grunts:** Regal, echo-resonant eagle calls; commanding, deep celestial voice speaking with absolute authority; metallic wind grunts during aerial charges.
*   **Combat Action Sounds:** Wing flaps produce sweeping emerald wind-shears; talon slashes sound like hot metal slicing through ice; Nagapasha snakes dissolve with a sizzling, evaporating hiss.

## 5. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Appearance Effects
*   **Passive Aura:** A glorious, shimmering emerald and gold trail of feathers and divine light ribbons coiling around his path.
*   **Environmental Interaction:** Winds generated from his wings cause sand, grass, and dust to blow outwards in radial waves, instantly clearing all poison fog layers in the arena.

### Active Combat Particles
*   **Ability Activation:** Giant, expanding golden-green wings of light projecting from his back; bright Sanskrit runes of purification rotating around bound allies.
*   **Damage Impact Feedback:** Bursts of green light stars and golden sparkles exploding on strike, leaving trailing energy feathers that dissolve into dust.
