# Pinaka (The Bow of Shiva)

*   **Weapon Class:** Colossal Siege Bow (Heavy Ranged / Puzzle Objective)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Shiva (Creator) \| Lord Rama (Lister/Breaker)
*   **Mythological Origin:** The terrifying bow of Lord Shiva, used to destroy the triple city of Tripura. Gifted to King Devaratha of Mithila, it is preserved as a holy relic, requiring the strength of a thousand men to budget or string.

---

## 1. Combat & Puzzle Profiles

In the game, the Pinaka is not a standard inventory weapon, but rather the central **Puzzle Boss** of Act 2:

### Weight & Stagger
*   **Mass Index:** 10,000 kg (requires spiritual chakra alignment to budget).
*   **Stagger Potential:** 100% (Any attempt to lift without alignment causes Rama to drop it, triggering a heavy ground shockwave).

### The Bow-Stringing Mechanics
To lift and string the Pinaka, the player must execute three quick-time events (QTE):
1.  *Chakra Attunement:* Align three glowing rings using the analog sticks.
2.  *The Cosmic Draw:* Press and hold the triggers to gradually lift the bow to Rama's shoulder, resisting gravity-well pulses.
3.  *The Breaking Point:* Rapidly tap button prompts to attach the string. This pushes the bow beyond its structural limits, snapping it in a massive shockwave.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Passive Relic State
*   **Relic Aura:** The bow rests on an eight-wheeled iron cart, surrounded by rising blue incense smoke and glowing white runes.
*   **Gravity Distortion:** The space around the bow visibly warps, causing falling leaves and dust to slow down or float upwards.

### Lifting & Breaking
*   **Lifting Flare:** When Rama grips the bow, a massive column of cyan-colored lightning shoots up into the sky.
*   **The Snap:** When the bow snaps, it releases a ring-shaped, blue shockwave that travels 50 meters, blowing back flags, pavilions, and causing stone pillars to crack.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Heavy, thunderous brass (trombones, French horns) combined with high Sanskrit chants (*"Om Namah Shivaya"*), low bass hums, and massive temple gongs.
*   **Melodic Key:** C minor (epic, heavy, destructive).
*   **Integration:** The music starts as a quiet, ominous bass drone, building to an overwhelming, operatic choir chant at the moment of lifting, ending with sudden silence upon the snap.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A deep, reverberating hum like a tectonic plate shifting, mixed with the crackle of ancient stone under pressure.
*   **Release/Break SFX:** A deafening metallic snap like a high-altitude thunderclap (*"KRA-KABOOM!"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A rolling earthquake rumbling sound (*"GRR-RUMBLE"*) that rattles the player's controller/subwoofer.
