# Trishula (The Trident of Ravana)

*   **Weapon Class:** Polearm / Throwing Trident (Melee & Ranged)
*   **Wielder:** Emperor Ravana
*   **Mythological Origin:** Though traditionally associated with Lord Shiva, Ravana wields a dark, colossal golden trident gifted to him through his intense Shaivite penances, representing unmatched penetrative and elemental power.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Trishula is Ravana's primary throwing weapon, used to pressure the player at medium range:

### Base Combat Attributes
*   **Base Melee Damage:** 130 per thrust.
*   **Throwing Damage:** 160 on direct hit.
*   **Attack Speed:** 1.0 thrust/second.
*   **Special Trait:** *Trident Split:* When thrown, the spear splits in mid-air into three separate, homing bolts of red lightning, targeting the player's dodge paths.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Stance & Wielding
*   **Dark Electricity:** The three prongs of the trident constantly crackle with dark red, static electricity.
*   **Spiritual Heat:** A thermal ripple surrounds the blade, making the background air look warped.

### Projectile & Impact
*   **Split Lightning:** The thrown projectile travels as a red lightning bolt that visibly splits into three separate streaks leaving zig-zag red trails.
*   **Impact Explosion:** Impact on ground triggers a circular burst of red electric sparks and throws up blackened rock chunks.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Harsh, heavy electronic synthesizers (blended into the orchestra), sudden bass drops, and sharp, metallic drum rolls.
*   **Melodic Key:** C# minor (dissonant, threatening, high-voltage).
*   **Integration:** Lightning strikes from this weapon synchronize with sudden cymbal crashes and high-register string strikes in the boss track.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A high-pitched, electric buzz like a high-voltage power grid under rain.
*   **Release/Throw SFX:** A sharp, whip-like crack of thunder (*"CRACK-BOOM"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A heavy, sizzling electric frying sound (*"ZAP-shhh"*) accompanied by a deep rumble.
