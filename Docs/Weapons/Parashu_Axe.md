# Parashu (The Battleaxe of Parashurama)

*   **Weapon Class:** Heavy Battleaxe (Melee)
*   **Wielder:** Sage Parashurama
*   **Mythological Origin:** A fearsome battleaxe gifted to Parashurama by Lord Shiva after intense penance. Armed with this weapon, Parashurama single-handedly defeated the corrupt warrior caste (Kshatriyas) of the world twenty-one times.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Parashu is a legendary weapon designed for close-range combat, forcing the player to parry with precise timing:

### Base Combat Attributes
*   **Base Melee Damage:** 160 per swing.
*   **Posture Break Rate:** 180% (Physical blocks are instantly shattered).
*   **Attack Speed:** 0.7 swings/second (Very heavy, wide sweeps).
*   **Special Trait:** *Volcanic Fissure:* Slamming the axe head into the ground creates a forward-moving line of volcanic fissures, launching flames and staggering players.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Stance & Swing
*   **Molten Edge:** The blade edge of the axe glows with a deep, volcanic orange heat.
*   **Ash Trail:** Sweeps leave a thick trail of glowing orange embers and black volcanic soot.

### Ground Impact
*   **Earth Cracking:** Ground hits spark a burst of molten red lava sparks and split the ground surface.
*   **Screen Shake:** Impacts trigger a heavy, localized screen vibration effect, representing pure physical power.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Rhythmic, heavy battle drums (*Pakhavaj*), sharp wood-block hits, and aggressive cello strokes.
*   **Melodic Key:** D# minor (combative, aggressive, archaic).
*   **Integration:** Axe blocks and clangs generate metallic ringing accents that sync with the percussion of the duel.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** A crackling fire sound mixed with the heavy breathing of a seasoned warrior.
*   **Swing SFX:** A terrifying, heavy metal displacement whoosh (*"VWOOSH"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A deafening steel-shattering clang (*"CLANGG-shiiing"*) followed by a dry wood-cracking sound.
