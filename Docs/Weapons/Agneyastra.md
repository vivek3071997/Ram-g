# Agneyastra (The Celestial Fire Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** Ranged Elemental Astra (AoE / Fire)
*   **Wielder:** Lord Rama \| General Indrajit
*   **Mythological Origin:** Associated with Agni, the Lord of Fire, this weapon summons the heat and flames of a thousand volcanic eruptions, turning physical arrows into explosive meteors of purifying or destructive fire.

---

## 1. Combat & Stance Profiles

The Agneyastra serves as the primary area-of-effect (AoE) arrow attack to clear minor demon hordes (such as in Act 4 against the 14,000 Rakshasas):

### Combat Attributes
*   **Spiritual Prana Cost:** 25% Aura Gauge.
*   **Invocation Speed:** 1.0 second.
*   **Splash Radius:** 6-meter circular explosion zone.
*   **Base Damage:** 80 physical damage + 10 burn damage per second for 5 seconds.
*   **Elemental Counter:** Clears wood barricades, inflicts "Burn" status on enemies, and melts ice armor. Countered/extinguished by *Varunastra*.

---

## 2. Visual & Particle Effects (VFX)

### Drawing & Charging
*   **Volcanic Draw:** The bow string ignites with a blazing orange flame. The arrow shaft turns into glowing, molten volcanic glass.
*   **Rising Ash:** Flakes of black ash and glowing embers rise from the bow during the 1-second charge phase.

### Release & Impact
*   **Meteor Trajectory:** The arrow flies as a flaming meteor leaving a thick trail of orange fire and black smoke.
*   **Volcanic Blast:** On impact, it creates a visual expansion of orange fire, cracking the ground and launching flaming debris up.

---

## 3. Audio & Acoustic Design

### Musical Theme & Soundtrack
*   **Acoustic Style:** Fast, energetic hand drums (*Dholak*), fiery and sharp *Sitar* sweeps, and rising brass.
*   **Melodic Key:** E minor (intense, warming, active).
*   **Integration:** Wielding this arrow shifts the exploration music to a highly active, explosive rhythmic percussion track.

### Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **Channeling SFX:** The crackling of a high-temperature bonfire, mixed with a rising wind roar.
*   **Release SFX:** A combustion-like launch blast (*"FWOOSH-boom"*).
*   **Impact SFX:** A metallic explosive blast (*"KABOOM-crack"*) followed by the continuous crackling of burning embers.
