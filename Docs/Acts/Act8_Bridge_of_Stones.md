# Act 8: The Bridge of Stones
*   **Traditional Reference:** Yuddha Kanda (Chapters 1 - 49)
*   **Act Focus:** Confronting the Ocean God, constructing the floating *Rama Setu* bridge, crossing the ocean, defection of Vibhishana, Angada's embassy challenge, and the battle against Indrajit's *Nagapasha* (serpent bindings).

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
Upon Hanuman's return, the Vanara army reaches the southern ocean's edge. To cross the turbulent sea, Rama performs penance to the Ocean God (Samudra Deva), who advises them to build a bridge using stones. Led by the Vanara engineers Nala and Nila, the army writes Rama's name on heavy stones, causing them to float. 

Meanwhile, Ravana's brother Vibhishana begs him to release Sita. Ravana kicks him out of the court. Vibhishana flies across the sea, offering his allegiance and strategic advice to Rama. The bridge is completed, and the army crosses to Lanka, establishing a siege camp. Rama sends Angada as a final peace ambassador. In Ravana's court, Angada plants his foot firmly, challenging any demon to budge it; none succeed, proving the strength of Rama's side. The siege begins, but Indrajit, Ravana's master sorcerer son, uses his invisibility and casts the *Nagapasha* (snakes binding), trapping Rama and Lakshmana in a near-death state.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Collaboration & Faith (Sangathan):** The floating bridge teaches that even the heaviest burdens can float if handled collectively with absolute faith and coordination.
*   **Welcoming the Outcast (Sharanagati):** Rama's acceptance of Vibhishana shows that true righteousness welcomes anyone who seeks shelter and stands for justice, regardless of their origin.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Lord Rama (Playable):** Perform the ocean penance, direct the bridge building, cross the sea, and fight Indrajit's invisible arrows.
*   **Lakshmana (Combat Companion):** Defend the construction site from marine demon raids, and stand by Rama.
*   **Vibhishana (NPC):** Join Rama's camp, providing tactical GDD blueprints of Lanka's fortress gates.
*   **Angada (NPC):** Perform the "immovable foot" challenge in Ravana's court.
*   **Indrajit (Boss):** Launch surprise invisible spells and bind the heroes in the Nagapasha.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **The Setu Bridge Construction Site:** A sandy shore filled with giant boulders, floating stones, and bustling Vanaras.
*   **The Rama Setu Bridge:** A narrow, miles-long stone pathway stretching over a deep, stormy ocean.
*   **Lanka Outpost Camp:** A fortified beachhead camp at the foot of Lanka's volcanic mountain, surrounded by wooden fences and watchtowers.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: Indrajit (The Nagapasha Phase)
*   **Boss Character:** A spellcaster-trickster boss fight focusing on tracking and purging status effects.
*   **Phase 1 (The Invisible Rain):** Indrajit hides in dark storm clouds, raining lightning-infused arrows down on the player. The player must use *Vayavyastra (Wind Arrow)* to blow away the clouds, revealing his position.
*   **Phase 2 (Nagapasha Trap):** A scripted event where Indrajit fires the *Nagapasha*. Rama and Lakshmana are bound by glowing green spirit-snakes. The player's movement speed is reduced by 80%, and they take damage over time. The goal is to survive for 60 seconds while dodging Indrajit's ground spells.
*   **Phase 3 (Summoning Garuda):** The player must direct their spiritual energy to summon the giant divine eagle **Garuda**. Garuda descends from the sky, instantly devouring the serpents and curing the team. Hanuman and the Vanaras then drive Indrajit back into the city.
*   **Mythological Defeat:** The act ends on a cliffhanger. Though the serpents are dispersed by Garuda, the army realizes the terrifying power of Lanka's sorcery.

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Rama (to the Ocean):** *"I have prayed for three days, King of the Seas, yet you offer no path. Let my arrows dry your depths, exposing the ocean floor!"*
*   **Samudra Deva (appearing):** *"Rama, do not destroy my waters. Let Nala and Nila build a bridge. Write your name on the rocks; my waves will hold them aloft."*
*   **Vibhishana:** *"I have left my home, my family, and my king. I seek shelter at your feet, Lord Rama. I know Lanka's gates, and I will help you tear them down."*
*   **Angada (in Ravana's court):** *"If any warrior in this court can move my foot even an inch, we will pack our tents and return across the sea without fighting!"*
*   **Indrajit:** *"Look at you, mighty princes of Ayodhya! Bound in my serpent coils, gasping for air. Your journey ends here on Lanka's sands!"*

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Nala & Nila:** Muscular grey-furred Vanaras wearing toolbelts and marking-chalk.
*   **Vibhishana:** Clean white silk robes, simple bead necklace, holding a tactical scroll, looking calm and scholarly.
*   **Angada:** Green fur, gold head-crown, chest armor made of polished stones.
*   **Garuda:** A magnificent half-man, half-eagle deity with brilliant golden wings, wearing a shining crown.

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Ocean God, Nala/Nila, Angada, Garuda, Indrajit (flying stance), Spirit Snakes.
*   **Environments:** Setu shoreline, Ocean bridge (floating stone assets, massive water wave mesh), Lanka Outpost (mud, wooden spikes, burning torches).
*   **Particle Effects:** Green poison vapors, glowing letters ("RAMA" in Sanskrit) on floating stones, lightning flashes, wind dispersion lines.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* Rhythmic, uplifting chanting and hammer strikes for bridge building; suspenseful, screeching strings and lightning cracks for Indrajit's ambush; celestial choir for Garuda's descent.
*   *SFX:* Lightning strikes, snake hissing, ocean waves crashing, stones splashing in water.

### Level Design Elements
*   Floating stones placement mini-game.
*   "Status Purge" QTE gauge.
