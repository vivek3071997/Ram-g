# Act 1: The Divine Prince
*   **Traditional Reference:** Bala Kanda (Chapters 1 - 30)
*   **Act Focus:** Birth, Childhood, Gurukul Training, protecting Sage Vishwamitra's sacrifice, and slaying the forest demoness Tataka.

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
The game begins in the golden empire of Ayodhya. King Dasharatha, troubled by the lack of an heir, conducts the holy Putrakameshti Yagna. Lord Vishnu chooses to incarnate as Dasharatha's eldest son, Rama, to free the cosmos from Ravana's invincibility boon (which protects him from gods, but not mortals). 

As teenagers, Rama and Lakshmana are sent with the fierce Sage Vishwamitra to the Dandakaranya forest to protect the sages' yajna (sacred fires) from demonic interruptions. The act culminates in their first real battle against the terrifying cursed demoness Tataka, who has laid waste to the forest.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Dharma of Obedience (Pitrudharma & Duty):** Rama accepts the harsh demands of his father and Sage Vishwamitra without question, demonstrating that true strength begins with humility, discipline, and respect for elder wisdom.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Lord Rama (Playable):** Master combat training, defend the sacrificial grounds, and defeat Tataka to restore natural order.
*   **Lakshmana (Combat Companion):** Defend Rama's flanks, shoot flying demons, and help hold the perimeter.
*   **Sage Vishwamitra (NPC):** Teach the princes celestial weapon mantras (Astras) and guide them through the forest paths.
*   **Tataka (Boss):** Terrorize the forest and destroy the sacred yajna fire before sunset.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **Ayodhya Palace Grounds:** Beautiful, sprawling marble courtyards with flowing water canals. Serves as the Tutorial and Training Grounds.
*   **The Hermitage of Vishwamitra (Siddhashrama):** Simple wooden huts decorated with flower garlands under massive banyan trees.
*   **The Cursed Dandaka Forest (Tataka's Domain):** Once a lush grove, now a desolate, twisted forest filled with thorns, black fog, and falling boulders.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: Tataka (Demoness of the Forest)
*   **Boss Character:** A towering, grotesque giantess with elephantine strength, claws like scythes, and dark magic.
*   **Phase 1 (The Dust Storm):** Tataka summons a heavy dust storm, reducing player visibility. Rama must dodge sweeping claw attacks and wind hazards, targeting her red glowing joints.
*   **Phase 2 (Shape-Shifting Ambush):** Tataka vanishes into the canopy, leaping down from random angles. Lakshmana alerts the player from which direction she is leaping.
*   **Phase 3 (Absolute Invisibility):** As the sun begins to set, Tataka turns fully invisible and rains giant boulders. Player must close their eyes (triggering the *Dharmic Sight* mechanic) and shoot using audio/sound-cues (*Shabda-Bhedi arrow*).
*   **Mythological Defeat:** Rama tracks her voice through the darkness and fires a single piercing arrow through her chest just before the sun sets.

---

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Vishwamitra:** *"Rama, do not hesitate because she is a woman. Her curse has made her a vessel of pure chaos. To protect the innocent is the highest duty of a Kshatriya."*
*   **Rama:** *"My hand is steady, Master. My arrow will seek only her heart, ending her suffering and restoring this forest."*
*   **Tataka (screeching):** *"Mortals! You dare enter my shadow? I will grind your bones to ash and feed your flesh to the soil!"*

---

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Princes (Rama & Lakshmana):** Yellow and green silk dhotis, matching headbands, and minimal jewelry suitable for royal training.
*   **Sage Vishwamitra:** Simple orange cotton wrap, a string of rudraksha beads, and long white hair tied in a bun.
*   **Tataka:** Tattered dark-grey hide-garments, skull bracelets, and wild, unkempt gray hair.

---

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Young Rama, Young Lakshmana, Vishwamitra, Tataka (Giant Beast), minor Asura soldiers (Subahu/Maricha minor forms).
*   **Environments:** Ayodhya Royal Arena (sandstone textures, marble steps), Siddhashrama (huts, sacred fire-pit), Dandaka Wilds (dead trees, black thorny vines).
*   **Particle Effects:** Glowing sacrificial fire sparks, heavy dust storm clouds, falling boulder dust, glowing trails for divine arrows.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* Orchestral brass and traditional Indian dhol drums for Ayodhya; tense woodwinds and sitar for the cursed forest; heavy, rhythmic percussion for the Tataka boss fight.
*   *SFX:* Arrow releases, claw slashes, heavy thud of falling boulders, Tataka's screech, sacred fire crackling.

### Level Design Elements
*   Sacred fire protection zone (player must keep enemies out of a glowing circle).
*   Shifting wind zones that affect arrow trajectories.
