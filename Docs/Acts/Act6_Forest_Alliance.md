# Act 6: The Forest Alliance
*   **Traditional Reference:** Kishkindha Kanda (Chapters 1 - 67)
*   **Act Focus:** Meeting Hanuman, forging the sacred friendship pact with Sugriva, slaying the invincible Vanara King Vali, and mobilizing the Vanara search forces.

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
Devastated by Sita's loss, Rama and Lakshmana head south. They reach the beautiful Pampa Lake and Rishyamukha Hill. The exiled Vanara prince Sugriva, hiding from his tyrant brother Vali (who has stolen his wife and kingdom), sends his minister Hanuman to inspect the strangers. Hanuman, recognizing Rama's divinity, carries the princes on his shoulders to Sugriva. 

A pact is sworn over fire: Rama will help Sugriva defeat Vali, and Sugriva will mobilize his worldwide Vanara army to locate Sita. Vali possesses a celestial boon that siphons half the strength of anyone who faces him in combat. Rama advises Sugriva to challenge Vali to a duel. During their fierce wrestling match in Kishkindha's cave city, Rama remains hidden behind a tree and fires a fatal shot to Vali's chest, bypassing the siphon boon. Sugriva is crowned king, and the search for Sita begins.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Maitri (Righteous Friendship) & Justice:** Rama demonstrates that alliances must be built on mutual trust and honor. Slaying Vali represents removing an unjust, abusive ruler to establish a righteous kingdom.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Lord Rama (Playable):** Guide Sugriva in combat, find a hidden cover spot during the duel, and deliver the pinpoint shot to Vali.
*   **Hanuman (Playable Companion):** Mediate the alliance, showcase platforming skills to guide the team, and carry companions over massive gaps.
*   **Sugriva (Combat Companion):** Duel Vali in close-quarters combat, holding his ground until Rama can strike.
*   **King Vali (Boss):** Defeat Sugriva, withstand physical attacks, and use his power-draining aura.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **The Pampa Lake & Rishyamukha Hill:** Beautiful rocky hills covered in green grass, lotus lakes, and clean mountain waterfalls.
*   **Kishkindha Cave City:** A colossal subterranean stone empire, with rough stone thrones, massive rope bridges, and high stone balconies.
*   **The Southern Shore Cliffs:** A windy, rocky cliff side overlooking a vast, turbulent ocean.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: King Vali (The Duel of Giants)
*   **Boss Character:** A unique tactical combat boss fight.
*   **Phase 1 (Sugriva vs. Vali):** The player controls Sugriva in a heavy wrestling match against Vali. Vali has double the health and drains Sugriva's attack power if the fight goes on too long. The goal is to survive and build up the "Rama Opportunity Gauge" by executing perfect parries.
*   **Phase 2 (The Cover Archery):** The perspective shifts to Lord Rama, hidden behind a grove of trees. The player must aim their bow, but the two brothers look identical. The player must wait for Sugriva to put on a garland of wild vines (as a visual marker) and shoot Vali without hitting Sugriva, all while dodging Vali's heavy rock throws.
*   **Phase 3 (Vali's Redemption):** Wounded, Vali confronts Rama on the ethics of his hidden shot. The player must choose dialogues explaining Vali's sins (exiling his brother, taking his brother's wife, violating Dharma) to make Vali accept his fate and pass the crown to Sugriva.
*   **Mythological Defeat:** Vali is pierced in the chest by Rama's arrow. He realizes Rama's divinity, begs for forgiveness, and leaves his son Angada in Rama's care before passing away.

---

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Hanuman (bowing):** *"I am Hanuman, son of the Wind. My master Sugriva seeks your friendship. If you are Rama of Ayodhya, my heart and strength are yours forever."*
*   **Sugriva:** *"Vali is a monster, Rama. He has taken my kingdom, my freedom, and my wife. Help me, and the entire Vanara race will serve your quest."*
*   **Vali (dying):** *"Rama! You are born of the Solar dynasty. Why did you strike me from the shadows like a coward, without facing me in open combat?"*
*   **Rama (cradling him):** *"Vali, you exiled your own brother, stole his wife while he lived, and ruled with pure terror. A hunter does not stand in the open to capture a wild beast that has forgotten all Dharma."*

---

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Rama & Lakshmana:** Ascetic bark-clothing, now carrying leather quivers gifted by Sage Agastya.
*   **Hanuman:** Simple white dhoti, golden armlets, and a sacred thread (*Yajnopavita*) representing his high Vedic scholar status.
*   **Sugriva:** Orange fur, wild leafy sash, and stone bracelets.
*   **Vali:** Dark golden fur, a shining gold necklace gifted by Indra (which preserves his life force), and a heavy royal stone crown.

---

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Hanuman, Sugriva, Vali (Muscular beast), Angada (Vali's young son), Wild Vine Garland.
*   **Environments:** Rishyamukha rocks, Pampa Lake (water lilies, green grass), Kishkindha Caves (subterranean architecture, torch stands).
*   **Particle Effects:** Glowing golden dust of Vali's power siphon aura, arrow trail, falling stone dust.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* Tribal hand drums, didgeridoo, and heavy wooden flutes for Vanara territory; tragic, slow cello and veena for Vali's passing.
*   *SFX:* Fist impacts, heavy groans, stone bridge collapsing, wind howling at the Southern cliffs.

### Level Design Elements
*   Archery zoom-in targeting system with wind indicators.
*   Rope bridge balancing mechanic.
