# Act 9: The Clash of Giants
*   **Traditional Reference:** Yuddha Kanda (Chapters 50 - 91)
*   **Act Focus:** The awakening and raid of the titan Kumbhakarna, the wounding of Lakshmana, Hanuman's journey to bring the Sanjeevani hill, and Lakshmana's final duel to slay Indrajit at the Nikumbhila altar.

---

## 1. Overview & Narrative Flow
Panicked by his losses, Ravana orders his mountain-sized brother, Kumbhakarna, to be awakened from his six-month sleep. Woken by trampling horses and drums, the titan Kumbhakarna enters the battlefield, devouring Vanaras. Rama battles the giant, systematically severing his limbs and ending his charge. 

Furious, Indrajit returns, using a stealth ambush to pierce Lakshmana's chest with the divine Shakti lance, leaving him near-death. The royal physician declares that only the *Sanjeevani* herb from the Himalayas, harvested before dawn, can save him. Hanuman flies to the Himalayas, but unable to identify the herb, he lifts the entire **Dronagiri Mountain** and flies back, healing Lakshmana. A fully recovered Lakshmana, guided by Vibhishana, infiltrates the dark caverns of Nikumbhila, where Indrajit is conducting a secret sacrifice to gain invincibility. Lakshmana disrupts the ritual, leading to a high-octane duel where he slays Indrajit.

---

## 2. Moral Theme
*   **Brotherly Loyalty & Duty:** Hanuman's flight to save Lakshmana shows that true love knows no limits. Lakshmana's slaying of Indrajit at the altar teaches that dark power built on corrupt, selfish sacrifices will always collapse under pure, disciplined duty.

---

## 3. Active Characters & Objectives
*   **Lord Rama (Playable):** Face the colossal Kumbhakarna, dodging stampedes, and sever his limbs with high-tier Astras.
*   **Hanuman (Playable Scout):** Fly to the Himalayas, locate the glowing Dronagiri peak, lift it, and navigate back through storm-winds.
*   **Lakshmana (Playable Combatant):** Infiltrate Nikumbhila temple, disrupt Indrajit's sacrifice, and defeat him in a sword-and-bow duel.
*   **Kumbhakarna (Boss):** Smash the Vanara barricades, throw mountain cliffs, and attempt to devour the player.
*   **Indrajit (Boss):** Guard the sacrificial altar, casting fire and illusion duplicates.

---

## 4. World & Locations
*   **The Lanka Plains (Warzone):** A desolate, ash-covered battlefield filled with trenches, barricades, and giant footprints.
*   **The Snowy Himalayas (Dronagiri Peak):** A high-altitude, freezing mountain peak covered in snow and glowing blue medicinal herbs.
*   **The Nikumbhila Altar Cavern:** A dark, subterranean temple filled with glowing red lava, bone carvings, and a dark stone altar.

---

## 5. Act Boss Battle: Kumbhakarna (Titan Raid) & Indrajit (The Ritual Duel)
This act features two massive, contrasting boss encounters:

### Boss 1: Kumbhakarna (The Colossal Titan)
*   **Phase 1 (The Calf Battle):** The player fights at Kumbhakarna's feet. Rama must dodge massive stamps and kick sweeps, targeting his iron anklets to trip him.
*   **Phase 2 (The Scale Climbing):** Once tripped, Rama uses grapple-arrows to climb Kumbhakarna's arms and shoulders, dodging swatting hands and falling boulders.
*   **Phase 3 (The Decapitation):** Kumbhakarna opens his mouth to suck in the army. Rama uses *Vayavyastra* to resist the suction, then fires the *Aindrastra* to sever his arms and head.

### Boss 2: Indrajit (The Altar Duel)
*   **Phase 1 (Disrupting the Rite):** Controlled by Lakshmana. The player must destroy four spiritual pillars channelled by Indrajit's demon priests before the sacrifice timer expires.
*   **Phase 2 (Dual Blade Duel):** Indrajit draws dual curved daggers. The player engaging in fast close-quarters combat, parrying his fast strikes.
*   **Phase 3 (The Final Arrow):** Indrajit uses teleportation. Lakshmana must track his teleport coordinates, charge his *Saumitrastra*, and fire a pinpoint shot as Indrajit materializes.
*   **Mythological Defeat:** Lakshmana's arrow decapitates Indrajit. His crown falls to the temple floor, signaling the end of Ravana's greatest weapon.

## 6. Script & Dialogue
*   **Kumbhakarna:** *"Ravana, you have committed a sin by taking another's wife. But you are my brother, and my club will crush your enemies before I return to my sleep!"*
*   **Rama:** *"Kumbhakarna! You are a giant of strength, but you tread the path of dust. Stand and face the bow of Rama!"*
*   **Hanuman (at the mountain):** *"Which herb is the Sanjeevani? They all glow with blue light! There is no time. I will carry the entire mountain to my Lord!"*
*   **Lakshmana (at the altar):** *"Your dark ritual ends here, Indrajit! A warrior does not hide behind sacrificial blood. Stand and face me!"*
*   **Indrajit:** *"You survived my snake coils, beggar prince? No matter. My Shakti lance left you bleeding. Today, I will finish the work!"*

---

## 7. Costumes & Clothing
*   **Kumbhakarna:** Sixty meters tall, wearing a massive copper crown, iron shoulder guards, and carrying a colossal spiked mace.
*   **Lakshmana (Armed State):** Coarse forest garments but now wearing a leather chest plate and wielding a glowing green bow.
*   **Indrajit (Ritual Garb):** Dark silk wraps, bare chest smeared with sacrificial blood, wearing a heavy ruby crown.

## 8. Asset & Build Requirements

### Art Assets
*   **3D Models:** Titan Kumbhakarna (60m high), Snowy Himalayan Mountain, Altar Pillars, Indrajit (Ritual form), Spirit Priests.
*   **Environments:** Ash battlefield, Himalayan snow peak (glacier shaders), Nikumbhila temple (black basalt rock, red lava lava-mesh).
*   **Particle Effects:** Glowing blue herb aura, rising snowstorm, sacrificial red fire, teleportation smoke, blood particles.

### Audio Assets
*   *Soundtrack:* Deep, booming brass and low wind instruments for the titan fight; epic, soaring strings for Hanuman's flight; dark, tense sitar and rhythmic Sanskrit chanting for the altar duel.
*   *SFX:* Giant foot stomps, mountain lifting roar, blade parries, altar pillars shattering.

### Level Design Elements
*   Titan scaling / grapple-climbing system.
*   Temple sacrifice timer bar.
*   Rapid parrying gauges.
