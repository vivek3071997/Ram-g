✦ The Vision ✦
Why we're building this

Dharma Made Playable

Every culture has its mythology. Greece has God of War. Norse legends have their champions. But India — with the oldest, richest, most layered mythological tradition on Earth — has been waiting.

Ram-G is our answer. Not an adaptation. Not a retelling. The actual Ramayana — its characters, its weapons, its moral philosophy, its sacred geography — translated into a living, playable world.

"We didn't want to make a game about the Ramayana. We wanted to make a game that is the Ramayana."

— Vivek, Lead Designer

Every character in Ram-G has a canonical basis in Valmiki's Bal Kanda, Aranya Kanda, Sundara Kanda, and Yuddha Kanda. Every weapon — every astra — works exactly as described in the original Sanskrit. The world is built from Puranic descriptions of sacred geography. The music is composed in classical Indian ragas.

We are building for India. And we are building for the world.

✦ Mission 0 ✦
Where the journey begins

Kid Hanuman Chases the Sun

Before Rama. Before Lanka. Before the war — there is a golden morning on Mount Sumeru, and a child who mistakes the rising sun for a ripe mango.

Mission 0 is the prologue. You play as Kid Hanuman — a small, divine, impossibly powerful Vanara child who climbs through four distinct zones: the Sacred Orchard, the Cliff Ascent, the Cloud Deck, and the Storm Arena where Lord Indra awaits.

It ends with the Curse of Forgetfulness — Hanuman's powers are locked away by the Sage Council, and the camera pulls back to reveal a baby crying in a palace crib in Ayodhya. Prince Rama has been born.

The Four Zones

01
Ground Level · 5:15 AM

Mount Sumeru — Sacred Orchards

Collect 7 sacred fruits for Mother Anjana. Chase Tyaksha imps who steal them. Master vine grappling and basic traversal.

02
Cliff Ascent · 4,000m Equivalent

Peak Ascent — Cliff Traversal

Solve 3 escalating Stone Windwheel puzzles to unlock Thermal Updraft columns. Wall-run, ledge-grab, and ascend the divine mountain.

03
Cloud Deck · Troposphere

The Cloud Platforms

Cross timed cloud platforms that dissolve after 3 seconds. Boss: Airavat — the colossal Celestial Elephant. Climb his tusks, tickle his ear, put him to sleep.

04
Storm Arena · Upper Stratosphere

Lord Indra's Domain

Bullet-hell boss fight on Indra's circular storm arena. Dodge lightning ring waves from the Vajra Core. Survive two chariot orbits — then grab Indra's golden crown to trigger the endgame cinematic.

✦ Characters ✦
The Mission Chain Protagonists

7 Heroes. 1 Unbroken Story.

Ram-G's most radical design choice is its Mission Chain Revelation System. Each mission ends with the current protagonist being mechanically "nerfed" by a story event — and the next playable character is revealed. This creates a continuous, deeply connected narrative while completely refreshing the gameplay loop.

✦ AI + Dev ✦
How we're building faster

AI-Accelerated Indie Development

Ram-G is one of the first Indian mythology games to use a systematic AI integration pipeline — not to replace artists, but to dramatically accelerate production timelines without sacrificing artistic vision.

Across Mission 0 alone, we've mapped 41% of tasks as AI-assistable, saving an estimated 312 person-days of production time. Here's how each tool fits in:

PhaseAI ToolRole in Pipeline
Concept ArtMidjourney, DALL-E 3, Adobe FireflyMood boards, character variation drafts
3D Base MeshMeshy AI, CSM.aiBlock mesh generation for sculpting
TexturesAdobe Firefly, Stable DiffusionAlbedo, roughness, fur pattern drafts
Script WritingClaude, ChatGPT, GeminiScript drafts, VA direction briefs, lore
MusicSuno AI, AIVA, UdioDemo stems in Indian ragas for composer review
Sound FXElevenLabs Sound FXAmbient loops, impact SFX prototypes
CodeGitHub Copilot, ClaudeGameplay systems, UI logic, shader graphs
Lip-syncSpeech Graphics AIPhoneme-driven facial animation
LODInstaLODAutomatic LOD generation for all assets

"The AI doesn't replace the artist. It gives them a head start — so they can spend their energy on what only a human can do: the soul of it."

— Ram-G Development Philosophy

Every script draft is reviewed and revised by our lead writer. Every AI-generated concept gets refined by a human artist. Every AI music stem is arranged and recorded with live instruments. The AI handles the starting point. The human handles the finish.

Cultural Authenticity — Non-Negotiable

No AI makes cultural decisions. For Ram-G, every piece of Sanskrit dialogue, every sacred symbol, every divine attribute, and every costume element goes through human review:

📖 Valmiki Ramayana source texts 📖 Tulsidas Ramcharitmanas 🔤 Sanskrit consultant phoneme review 🏺 Puranic iconography verification 🎵 Classical raga selection 🌿 Canonical flora & fauna ✅ Pre-Gold cultural sign-off gate
