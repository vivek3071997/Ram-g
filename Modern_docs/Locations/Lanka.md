# Lanka (The Volcanic Citadel)

*   **World Region:** Volcanic Island Empire of the South (Acts 8, 9, and 10)
*   **Aesthetic Direction:** High-Contrast Obsidian Cyber-Gothic. Colossal, dark basalt and titanium-alloy fortress walls, active geothermal energy vents emitting warm red light, and towering sky-citadels lit by neon-gold solar grids.
*   **Acoustic Theme:** **Raga Bhairav** (War/Fortress state - dark, heavy, high-contrast, terrifying) and **Raga Kedara** (Climax duel - epic, high-tension strings).

---

## 1. Geothermal & Structural Defense Layout

Lanka is a hyper-industrial, fortified volcanic island city, utilizing geothermal heat generators to power its massive automated laser defenses and coastal security.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Architectural / Scientific Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Material Base** | Polished Volcanic Obsidian + Titanium Alloys | Extreme structural yield strength; absorbs thermal stress. |
| **Energy Grid** | Geothermal Molten Heat Generators | Converts volcanic core heat into high-voltage electrical energy. |
| **Defensive Gates**| 20m Triple-Reinforced Titanium Gates | Impenetrable to standard kinetic battering rams. |
| **Sensor Obstacles**| Light-Refractive Laser Detection Grids | Invisible light fences that trigger alarm sirens when crossed. |
| **Ashoka Vatika** | Isolated Botanical Canopy Garden | Closed containment zone utilizing dense, damp foliage layers. |
| **Summit Arena** | Active Geothermal Vent Grid (Caldera Crater) | High-temperature surface with dynamic thermal updraft columns. |

## 2. Dynamic Traversal & Ashoka Vatika Stealth Loops

*   **Ashoka Vatika Stealth Loops:** The botanical garden of Lanka is built with highly layered, light-anisotropic *Golden Ashoka* foliage. Players must navigate this zone using silent stealth loops, crawling through shadowed paths, and avoiding optical light-refraction sensor grids. Firing a *Varunastra* arrow at sensor nodes temporarily freezes the optical lenses, allowing passage.
*   **The Geothermal Summit Crater:** The climax boss duel with Ravana occurs on an active volcanic caldera crater. The ground features large geothermal vents that expel high-pressure, super-heated air columns at regular intervals (`6.0s` cycle). Wielding the [Kishkindha Glider](file:///../Vehicles/Kishkindha_Exo_Gliders.md) within these thermal columns generates massive vertical lift, allowing Rama to launch high-altitude archery strikes.
*   **Molten Lava Hazards:** Flows of active molten magma act as lethal environmental bounds. Players can freeze lava flows using sub-zero *Varunastra* arrows, converting them into walkable basalt bridges.

## 3. Visual & Audio Design

### Visual Particles
*   **Obsidian Reflection Maps:** Beautiful PBR obsidian shaders displaying sharp, high-contrast reflections of molten red lava and golden solar tower grids.
*   **Volumetric Volcanic Ash:** Dense, dark-gray ash particles floating gently through the air, creating a high-contrast post-processed silhouette look.

### Sound SFX
*   **Citadel Ambience:** The deep, low-frequency rumble of active volcanic chambers; the metallic hum of high-voltage geothermal generators; distant, terrifying war horns.
*   **Laser Grid Trips:** A sharp, high-frequency electric zap, followed by a loud, cycling alarm siren (*"BEE-WOO-BEE-WOO"*).
*   **Lava Cracks:** The boiling, crackling sound of molten rock flowing; the sharp *hiss* of steam when frozen.
