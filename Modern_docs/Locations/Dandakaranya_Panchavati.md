# Dandakaranya & Panchavati (The Primeval Forest)

*   **World Region:** The Wilderness Exile Zone (Acts 3 to 6)
*   **Aesthetic Direction:** Bioluminescent Primeval Rainforest. Towering ancient Sal and Nyagrodha Banyan trees, dense foliage, moss-covered basalt cliffs, and deep mist-veiled river canyons.
*   **Acoustic Theme:** **Raga Lalit** (Mist-covered forest dawn - solitary, mysterious) and **Raga Bhairavi** (Forest twilight/resolution - tranquil, deep, reflective).

---

## 1. Ecological & Physical Geography

Dandakaranya is a vast, primeval jungle operating under strict biological and geographic rules, featuring fast-flowing river rapids and massive forest canopies.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Environmental / Physics Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Canopy Height** | 45m - 60m (Sal & Banyan Trees) | Creates multi-layered horizontal traversal paths. |
| **River Flow Velocity** | 3.5 m/s - 6.0 m/s (Godavari Rapids) | Generates high drag vectors for rafts and boats. |
| **Atmospheric Mist** | Volumetric Fog (Density: `0.35`) | Lowers long-range visual pathing, favoring acoustic tracking. |
| **Panchavati Cottage** | High-Strength Timber Structural Frame | Built by Lakshmana using mechanical wood interlocking joints. |
| **Boundary Defense** | Mechanical Wire & Laser Tripwire System | The *Lakshmana Rekha*: high-voltage perimeter security line. |
| **Camouflage Foliage**| Anisotropic Golden Ashoka leaves | Reflects sunlight at sharp angles, aiding stealth concealment. |

---

## 2. Dynamic Traversal & Active Hazards

*   **High Canopy Branch Paths:** Symmetrical, thick horizontal Banyan branches form natural high-speed running lanes. Players can swing between branches using high-tensile organic vines, maintaining height and avoiding forest floor predators.
*   **Godavari Rapid Crossings:** Fast-flowing river rapids requiring players to pilot the silent [Ganga Skiff](file:///../Vehicles/Ganga_Stealth_Hydrofoil.md), vectoring thrust around giant basalt rocks and managing water hydrodynamics to prevent hull cracking.
*   **The Panchavati Perimeter Defense:** The *Lakshmana Rekha* is a highly logical mechanical perimeter set by Lakshmana. By running high-tensile copper wire along boundary posts and connecting it to a high-voltage solar accumulator capacitor, it forms a visual electrical barrier that repels minor Asuric beasts. If a beast crosses, it triggers a loud alarm chime and an electrical discharge.

---

## 3. Visual & Audio Design

### Visual Particles
*   **Bioluminescent Forest Spores:** Soft green and gold glowing spores floating gently in the air around Banyan roots, serving as passive path indicators.
*   **High-Velocity River Spray:** Dense volumetric water spray and mist erupting off Godavari river rocks.

### Sound SFX
*   **Forest Ambience:** The rich, complex soundscape of whistling wind through Sal leaves, distant bird screeches, and the constant, low-frequency roar of Godavari rapids.
*   **Boundary Hum:** The low, continuous electrical hum (*"hzzzzz"*) of the active *Lakshmana Rekha* boundary line.
*   **Footsteps on Leaves:** The soft, crisp *crunch-crunch* of running flats on dry forest litter, layered with wet moss squelches.
