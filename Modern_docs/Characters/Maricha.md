## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Light-Bending Decoy Projection (The Golden Fawn):** Maricha is an elite optical technician. Wielding a hand-held, high-precision crystalline prism array (*Sphatika Decoy Prism*), he projects a highly detailed, 3D holographic illusion of a rare, glowing golden fawn into the forest canopy. The decoy reflects and refracts real sunlight, making it indistinguishable from a living animal. While the fawn decoy moves, Maricha's real body is rendered `95%` invisible under an active-camouflage cloak.
*   **Sensory Frequency Vocal Mimicry:** Using a specialized brass frequency modulator throat-collar, Maricha records real-time vocal profiles of targets. He can perfectly duplicate the acoustic voice frequencies, pitch, and timbre of Lord Rama (e.g. screaming *"Ah, Lakshmana! Help me!"*), transmitting the signal over long distances to manipulate enemy movements.
*   **Stealth Repositioning Smoke Relays:** When his decoy prism is shattered by a precision arrow strike, Maricha deploys a high-pressure flash grenade, generating a blinding `800,000 cd` light flash and a dense carbon smoke screen to cover his physical retreat.

### Tactical Evasion Gear
*   **Decoy System:** Wields the [Sphatika Decoy Prism](file:///../Environment_Elements/Relics_Artifacts_Modern.md#b-sphatika-alignment-prisms-optical-puzzles), a highly polished natural crystal prism that projects volumetric light illusions.
*   **Equipped Costume:** [Maricha Stealth Mesh](file:///../Clothing/Materials_Textures.md). Built from ultra-thin carbon-fiber active camouflage mesh with built-in sensors that capture and project background textures.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
Tataka's son, Maricha survived Rama's early border clearance campaigns and retired to a quiet, off-grid hermitage to escape the war. However, Ravana tracks him down and forces him under threat of immediate execution to execute a critical tactical mission: lure Rama away from the Panchavati cottage. Utilizing his advanced optical decoy prism to project the legendary "Golden Fawn" and using his voice modulator to mimic Rama's cries, Maricha successfully divides Rama and Lakshmana, directly enabling Sita's abduction.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 2 (Recon):** Serve as Tataka's tactical scout, mapping regional border trade lanes and deploying optical decoy hazards.
*   **Act 5 (The Illusion):** Position himself near the Panchavati border, deploy the high-fidelity "Golden Fawn" holographic decoy to lure Rama deep into the dense canopy, and execute the vocal frequency mimicry to draw Lakshmana away.
