## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Adrenaline Rage Overdrive (Rage Debuff):** When Ravana's attacks are parried or his health falls below `50%`, his nervous system floods with extreme levels of adrenaline. This increases his physical kinetic damage by `40%` and grants high stagger immunity. However, this rage blocks logical decision-making: his movements become wild, high-inertia, and predictable, preventing him from blocking or playing smart. The rage also rapidly spikes his lactic acid to `14 mmol/L`, causing a severe crash after `15 seconds` (movement speed reduced by `40%`, leaving him wide open to Rama's precision strikes).
*   **Ten-Core Cognitive Processing (Ten Heads Visual):** Visually represented as a rapid, shifting red targeting overlay in his tactical HUD. While Ravana's immense brain can process ten parallel inputs at once (allowing him to track up to ten separate targets in arena combat), this high cognitive load makes him highly vulnerable to rapid lateral displacement. If Rama moves quickly to his blind spot, the processor glitches, causing a short stun.
*   **Chandrahasa Kinetic Sweeps:** Wields a colossal, high-carbon obsidian blade. Using his extreme muscle leverage, Ravana executes massive `360-degree` horizontal swings that generate up to `8,000 N` of physical force, creating shockwaves of high-pressure air that knock players backward.

### Colossal Modern-Military Gear
*   **Boss Weapon:** [Chandrahasa Sword](file:///../Weapons/Chandrahasa.md). A massive, high-carbon steel-alloy broadsword with a dark, vacuum-treated vacuum-edge blade.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
The emperor of Lanka and absolute lord of the volcanic southern island, Ravana is a brilliant scholar, scientist, and warrior who has surrendered entirely to his immense pride and raw passions. Walking in a towering, physically hyper-dense biological body, he commands the most advanced naval and geothermal base in the ancient world. Driven by supreme ego, he abducts Princess Sita as a display of ultimate power, completely ignoring the strategic warnings of his commanders, leading his empire into a catastrophic physical war with Rama's forces.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 5 (Expansion):** Consolidate volcanic geothermal grids in Lanka, establish naval supremacy across the Southern Ocean, and execute the silent aerial capture of Sita using Maricha's diversion.
*   **Acts 6 - 8 (Fortification):** Deploy automated laser-barrier obelisks around Lanka's volcanic peaks, contain the captured Sita in Ashoka Vatika, and reject diplomatic peace proposals out of extreme pride.
*   **Acts 9 - 10 (Climax):** Command heavy armored ground troops at the volcanic bridge breach, deploy his giant brother Kumbhakarna as a massive human battering ram, and engage in the final, brutal physical duel with Rama.
