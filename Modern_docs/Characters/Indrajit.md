## 2. Grounded Skills & Physics-Based Powers

### Peak Human Capabilities & Biological Overclocks
*   **Low-Light Optical Camouflage (Meghanada Illusion):** Indrajit is a master of situational light and shadow. By utilizing a highly specialized, tightly woven dynamic textile that shifts between dark gray and forest patterns depending on ambient light levels, he achieves a `90%` visual concealment rating in shadowed areas, effectively disappearing from target sightlines.
*   **High-Tensile Wire Trap Arrays (Nagapasha Snares):** Deploys physical, spring-loaded high-tensile steel wire traps across lanes. When triggered, the cables spring inward, constricting around the target's limbs. This causes localized arterial pressure drops and severe kinetic deceleration, trapping the target until manually cut.
*   **Carbon-Powder Smoke Concealment:** Launches localized smoke canisters filled with dense, light-absorbing carbon micro-powder. This instantly breaks line-of-sight visual paths, scatters laser targeting grids, and allows Indrajit to silent-dash to high structural scaffolding.

### High-Precision Tactical Gear
*   **Primary Weapons:** Deploys a high-tension composite recurve hunting bow firing poison-tipped kinetic arrows, and a specialized tactical lance (*Shakti Lance*).
*   **Equipped Costume:** [Meghanada Stealth Suit](file:///../Clothing/Materials_Textures.md). Built from lightweight, silent carbon-polymer mesh with micro-grooves that dampen the rustle of movement, integrated with a dynamic optical camouflage cloak.

## 3. Narrative, Objectives & Logical Drive

### Lore Biography
The eldest son of Ravana, Meghanada earned the title *Indrajit* (conqueror of Indra) due to his supreme tactical capture of the leader of the celestial military forces. Unlike his father, who relies on raw physical size and massive rage-sweeps, Indrajit is a cold, highly logical, and brilliant combat strategist. Operating out of Lanka's volcanic sky platforms, he uses stealth ghillie camouflage, environmental traps, and calculated ambushes, serving as the most dangerous tactical threat to Rama and Lakshmana's forces.

### Strategic Core Objectives
*   **Acts 1 - 5 (Security):** Coordinate Lanka's automated airspace perimeter, establish optical surveillance arrays along the southern coastline, and train high-speed commando units.
*   **Acts 6 - 8 (Defense):** Deploy silent sniper units in Ashoka Vatika, monitor Rama's naval landing coordinates, and execute the silent infiltration of Rama's base camp.
*   **Acts 9 - 10 (War):** Ambush Lakshmana on the battlefield using dynamic smoke and optical camo, deploy high-tensile steel trap lines (*Nagapasha*), and defend Lanka's volcanic command center.
