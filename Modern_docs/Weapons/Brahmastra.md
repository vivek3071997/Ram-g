# Brahmastra (The Solid-State Solar Laser Arrow)

*   **Weapon Class:** High-Intensity Solid-State Solar Laser Arrow (Ultimate Class)
*   **Primary Wielder:** Lord Rama (requires maximum spiritual focus alignment)
*   **Biomechanical Invocation:** Requires a full `100%` Focus gauge (`300 Joules`). Rama locks into a steady, three-point knee-mounted stance for `3.0 seconds` to stabilize his breathing, align targeting coordinates, and engage the high-voltage shoulder battery.

---

## 1. Technical & Energy Specifications

The Brahmastra is a highly advanced solid-state energy guidance arrow. Rather than a physical projectile, it is an optical alignment shaft that focuses a massive electrical discharge into a coherent, high-energy laser beam.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Engineering / Physics Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Power Source** | Shoulder-Mounted High-Density Solar Battery | Stores solar energy gathered throughout exploration phases. |
| **Energy Discharge** | 1.2 Megajoules (MJ) in 1.5 seconds | Equivalent to the energy of a heavy tactical railgun strike. |
| **Laser Class** | Solid-State Coherent Light Beam | Achieves absolute line-of-sight propagation with zero trajectory drop. |
| **Guidance System** | Micro-Prismatic Crystal Alignment Rings | Focuses the scattered high-voltage charge into a tight, `15mm` beam. |
| **Cooling Profile** | Immediate Liquid-Nitrogen Thermal Purge | Prevents carbon-composite limbs from melting post-discharge. |
| **Penetration Rating** | Absolute (Bypasses all physical armor) | Vaporizes steel plates and concrete blocks instantly. |
| **Stance Constraint** | Fixed Aim Lock (3.0 seconds draw) | Player cannot move or dodge during charge, leaving them fully exposed. |

---

## 2. Combat Mechanics & Overheat Penalty

Because of its colossal energy release, the Brahmastra is balanced by strict scientific constraints:
*   **Single-Use Charge:** Requires a full solar battery. Recharging the battery requires the player to stand in direct, un-shaded sunlight for `60 seconds` or collect rare high-efficiency solar cells.
*   **Core Overheat:** Post-discharge, the *Kodanda* limbs suffer intense thermal stress. Archery is disabled for `5 seconds` while the automatic liquid-nitrogen cooling vents purge the system, emitting a thick cloud of steam from the riser.
*   **Vaporization Cleave:** The laser beam travels instantly, vaporizing all minor and elite Asuric units in a straight, linear path and inflicting massive structural melt damage to bosses.

## 3. Visual & Particle Render (VFX)

### Charging Sequence
*   **Thermal Light Focus:** Shimmering, high-intensity white-hot sparks draw from the air into the arrow rest, accompanied by a dynamic optical lens flare that blooms the player's screen.
*   **Riser Vent Steam:** Micro-coolant vents on the riser emit high-pressure white steam jets during the final `1.0 second` of charge.

### Release & Beam Trajectory
*   **The Solar Beam:** A thick, solid ribbon of blinding white-gold coherent light instantly spans the arena from the bow to the horizon, persisting for `1.5 seconds` with heat-haze distortion along its borders.
*   **Vaporized Air Rings:** Concentric rings of heated air expand outwards along the beam path, indicating extreme thermal shockwaves.
