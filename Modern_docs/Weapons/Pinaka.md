# Pinaka (The Heavy Compound Bow of Mithila)

*   **Weapon Class:** Heavy Subterranean Magnetic Launching Bow (Anti-Siege Static Class)
*   **Primary Wielder:** Sage Parashurama (original custodian), tested by Lord Rama in Mithila.
*   **Biomechanical Challenge:** An average human cannot lift it. Stringing it requires a team of engineers using dual hydraulic winch systems. Rama strings it manually by aligning his entire physical skeletal structure to override a colossal physical tension of `12,000 lbs`.

---

## 1. Technical & Material Specifications

The Pinaka is a colossal, ancient heavy compound bow designed as a static fortification defense weapon. Its limbs are constructed from multi-layered titanium-alloy plates, and its string is a high-tensile carbon-steel aircraft cable.

| Parameter | Metric Value | Engineering / Physics Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Structure Class** | Fixed Static Anti-Siege Compound Bow | Massive size prevents mobile handling by standard humans. |
| **Limb Material** | Laminated Beta-Titanium Alloys | Extremely high yield strength and modulus of elasticity. |
| **Cable System** | 12mm High-Tensile Carbon-Steel Aircraft Cable | Withstands extreme tension loads without snapping during winching. |
| **Draw Tension** | 12,000 lbs (Peak Draw Load) | Designed to launch heavy kinetic iron-tipped ballista shafts over `500 meters`. |
| **Recoil System** | Dual High-Capacity Hydraulic Shock Dampers | Absorbs colossal energy release to prevent structural concrete base cracking. |
| **Calibration Rig** | Ashtapada Gear Assembly & Winch | Heavy gear reducer system used to wind and tension the steel cable. |
| **Energy Release** | 14,500 Joules | Colossal kinetic release, capable of piercing armored tanks or structural city gates. |

## 2. The Trial of Mithila: Mechanical Structural Failure

In Act 2, Rama is challenged to string the Pinaka at the subterranean terracotta vault. The mission translates this legendary feat into a realistic, grounded engineering and biological trial:
1.  **Hydraulic Manual Override:** Rama manually disengages the hydraulic safety locking pins of the winch rig, choosing to string the carbon-steel cable purely through biological leverage.
2.  **Ultimate Biomechanical Tension:** By planting his feet on the titanium base block and utilizing perfect kinetic bone-alignment (transferring power directly from his core and legs), Rama pulls the `12,000 lbs` high-tension steel cable.
3.  **Catastrophic Material Overload:** As the cable reaches the locking notch, the extreme force exceeds the titanium limbs' ultimate tensile strength limit. The limbs suffer immediate, explosive structural failure (breaking the bow in half), releasing a massive kinetic shockwave that vibrates the subterranean stone cavern like a `4.2` magnitude earthquake.

## 3. Visual & Particle Render (VFX)

### Stringing & Material Strain
*   **Stress Thermal Glow:** Limbs display a visible, computer-modeled color shift in Rama's HUD, turning from deep blue (stable) to neon red (structural yield limit) as tension increases.
*   **Metal Splinter Flashes:** As the titanium limbs crack and snap, high-velocity metal splinters and high-pressure hydraulic fluid spray outwards in a radial trajectory conforming to authentic physics.

### Acoustic Shockwave
*   **The Seismic Crack:** The snap of the `12mm` steel cable and titanium limbs creates an explosive, dry sound crack (`125 dB`), accompanied by a low-frequency rumble that shakes loose dust and stone particles from the cavern roof. No magical particles are generated.
